25. contoh dialog bahasa inggris tentang give appreciation to someone


Giving appreciation for someone artinya adalah memberikan apresiasi atau penghargaan kepada seseorang.

Contoh dialog give appreciation for someone:

Dialog 1:

A : Hi, B. What do you think about my dress? (asking for opinion)

B: It look so beautiful. Is it a new dress? (giving appreciation)

A: Thank you. No, it's an old dress, but my mother redesigned it.

B: But, it is not old at all. And you suit to wear it. (giving appreciation)

A: Thank you, B. Wow, you look nice with your hair cut. (giving appreciation)

B: Really? Do you like it?

A: Yes, of course. You look more handsome than before. (giving appreciation)

B; Thank you.

Dialog 2:

Son: Dad, I am really sorry.

Dad: What happened? Why do you look so sad?

Son: I got bad score for my English final test, Dad.

Dad: Let me see it.

Son: I am sorry, Dad. I studied last night, but I didn't do it.

Dad: Hmmm.. It's okay, Son. I knew you studied hard last night. Don't give up.

Son: Thank you, Dad. You are the best father. (giving appreciation)

Dad: I proud of you because you are my son and you never give up. (giving appreciation)

Writing Dialog Using Expression of InvitationJawaban
Pendahuluan

Invitation adalah sebuah ungkapan yang digunakan ketika kita ingin mengundang orang lain untuk hadir ke sebuah acara/kegiatan atau ketika seseorang mengajak orang lain untuk melakukan suatu tindakan.

Beberapa contoh kalimat invitation:

Would you like to come to my party?How about dinner on weekend?Will you come to my performance?Can you join me to the zoo on Sunday?Would you mind coming over and say hello to my parents?I have two concert tickets, maybe you would like to join me?
Pembahasan

Anna: Hey, Jessica! I have something to tell you. My sister is getting married in two weeks, would you like to come to her wedding party?

Jessica: Hey, Anna. Yeah sure, I would love too. When it will be?

Anna: It's in the next two weeks but I'm not sure about the date, I will deliver the invitation to your house in the evening, is that fine?

Jessica: Yeah, absolutely. No worries Anna. Thanks for inviting me.

Anna: You're welcome, Jess.
