7. Mohon BantuannyaBahasa inggris Kelas 8 Smpdijadiin simple past seperti contoh MOHON SERIUS JAWABNYA...​


Jawaban:

1. (+) She wanted to go to the cinema yesterday. (Ia ingin pergi ke bioskop kemarin.)

( – ) She did not want to study Math yesterday. (Ia tidak ingin belajar Matematika kemarin.)

( ? ) Did she want to study Math yesterday? (Apakah dia ingin belajar Matematika kemarin.)

2. ( + ) Last year we went to Malang. (Tahun lalu kami pergi ke Malang.)

( – ) Last year we didn’t go to Solo. (Tahun lalu kami tidak pergi ke Solo.)

( ? ) Did you go to Malang last year? (Apakah kamu pergi ke Malang tahun lalu?)

3. ( + ) I planned my summer holiday last week. (Saya merencanakan liburan musim panas minggu lalu.)

( – ) I didn’t do my Art project last week. (Saya tidak mengerjakan proyek Kesenian minggu lalu.)

( ? ) Did you do your Art project last week? (Apa kamu mengerjakan proyek Kesenian minggu lalu?)

4. ( + ) When I gave him a candy, he stopped crying. (Ketika aku memberinya permen, ia berhenti menangis.)

( – ) If I didn’t give him a candy, he would cry. (Jika aku tidak memberinya permen, ia akan menangis..)

( ? ) Why did you give him a candy? (Mengapa kamu memberinya permen?)

5. ( + ) Teena started school when she was five. (Teena mulai bersekolah ketika ia berusia 5 tahun.)

( – ) Teena didn’t go to school when she was four. (Teena tidak ke sekolah saat ia berusia 4 tahun.)

( ? ) Did Teena start school when she was four? (Apa Teena mulai bersekolah saat ia berusia 4 tahun?)

30. tuliskan rumus tenses,simple present tenses,simple past tenses,simple future tenses,beri masing 5 contoh ( +,-,? )​


Jawaban:

Rumus simple present tense yaitu

(+) subject + verb 1

(-) Subject + do/does + not + verb 1

(?) Do/does + subject + verb 1?

Rumus simple past tense yaitu

(+) subject + verb 2

(-) Subject + did + not + verb 1

(?) Did + subject + verb 1?

Rumus simple future tense yaitu

(+) subject + will + verb 1

(-) Subject + will + not + verb 1

(?) Will+ subject + verb 1?

Penjelasan:

Contoh kalimat simple present tense yaitu

(+) I drink a glass of milk everyday.

(-) I don't drink a glass of milk everyday.

(?) Do I drink a glass of milk everyday?

Contoh kalimat simple past tense yaitu

(+) I slept on the sofa last night.

(-) I didn't sleep on the sofa last night.

(?) Did I sleep on the sofa last night?

Contoh kalimat simple future tense yaitu

(+) She will come to my house tomorrow.

(-) She will not come to my house tomorrow.

(?) Will she come to my house tomorrow?

penjelasan:

Belajar Bahasa Inggris Dari Lirik Lagu

Kalimat yang akan kita bahas dalam lagu di atas adalah :

I should've seen before.

Kalimat di atas digunakan untuk mengungkapkan penyesalan di waktu lampau. Agar lebih jelas mari kita lihat fakta yang sebenarnya terjadi di waktu lampau.

Fakta :

I didn't see.

Penyesalannya :

I should've seen before.

Mari kita bandingkan lagi contoh yang lain!

Fakta :

I loved her too much.

Penyesalannya :

I shouldn't have loved her too much.

Dari segi arti, fakta selalu bertentangan dengan penyesalannya. Kalau faktanya dalam bentuk negatif, penyesalannya positif dan sebaliknya.

Dari segi pola kalimat, faktanya dalam bentuk Simple Past Tense, yaitu Subyek + Kata Kerja II atau Subyek + did + not + Kata Kerja I.

Sedangkan penyesalannya dalam bentuk pola Subyek + should (not) + have + Kata Kerja III .

Sekali lagi, jika kita melakukan sesuatu di masa lampau, lalu kita menyesalinya, gunakan pola Subyek + should + have + Kata Kerja III.

Sebagai penutup, mari kita lihat penyesalan seseorang telah menelantarkan orang tuanya ketika hidup dan faktanya di dalam kurung.

I should have seen my parents more often. ( I didn't see my parents often. )

I should have taken care of them more patiently. ( I didn't take care of them more patiently.)

I should have given them what they loved. ( I didn't give them what they loved .)

38. Jelaskan tentang simple past tenses​


Jawaban:

Simple Past Tense adalah kalimat yang menceritakan kejadian masa lampau

Penjelasan:

Rumus Nominal Simple Past Tense<-> Kalimat Positif : Subject + To be (Was/Were) + Complement <-> Kalimat Negatif : Subject + To be (Was/Were) + not + Complement<-> Kalimat Tanya : To be (Was/Were) + Subject + Complement + ?Rumus Verbal Simple Past Tense<-> Kalimat Positif : Subject + Verb 2 + Complement<-> Kalimat Negatif : Subject + did + not + Verb 1 + Complement<-> Kalimat Tanya : Did + Subject + Verb 1 + Complement + ?

Contoh Nominal Simple Past Tense :<-> Kalimat Positif : He was two books every week<-> Kalimat Negatif : He was not two books every week<-> Kalimat Tanya : Was he two books every week?

Contoh Verbal Simple Past Tense :<-> Kalimat Positif : Andi played football in the field <-> Kalimat Negatif : Andi did not played football in the field<-> Kalimat Tanya : Did Andi play football in the field?

Noted:

penggunaan was untuk to be -> I , He , She , It

penggunaan were untuk to be -> You, We, They dan benda berjumlah jamak

Singkatnya, simple pas tenses digunakan menyatakan kegiatan di masa lampau

Rumus : S +V2
