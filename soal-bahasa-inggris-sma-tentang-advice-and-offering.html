9. Bahasa Inggrisnya Kemaren bersama ibu melda kami belajar asking and giving suggestion an offering


yesterday we with miss melda learned

Answer:


In translating phrases or sentences, you need to avoid literal translation or word by word one as it may confuse the reader of the target language and the meaning itself as it doesn't suit the context. The sentence above is about past tense as there is a word 'kemarin' which is yesterday. So, here is the proper translation of such sentence

- Yesterday, we learned about asking and giving suggestion as well as offering from Ms.Imelda.'

OR

' Ms. Imelda taught us about asking, giving suggestion and offering yesterday.'


Hopefully that suits your expectation.

20. Contoh percakapan dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya,yang menerima dan menolak sesuatu(suggesting and offering)


A: what are you doing, B? (Apa yang kamu lakukan, B?)

B: i'm painting a flower. What do you think about it? (Aku sedang melukis bunga. Bagaimana menurutmu?)

A: that looks amazing, you're good at it. But it will be more beautiful if you add some butterfly. (Itu terlihat luar biasa, kamu berbakat dalam bidang ini. Tetapi ini akan menjadi lebih indah jika kamu menambahkan kupu-kupu.)

B: wow, that's a good idea. (Wow, idea yang bagus.)

A: do you need some help? (Apakah kamu memerlukan bantuan?)

B: yes, can you past me that brush? ( Ya, bisa tolong operkan kuas itu?)

A: sure, anything else? (Baiklah, ada lagi?)

B: no, thanks. That's all. You're very kind. (Tidak terima kasih. Itu saja. Kamu sangat baik sekali.)

1. Mention the expressions of ignoring/declining a help offer? "no thank you" "I'm fine thanks I do not need that" "its okay I got this"
2. Mention the expressions of taking a help offer? "Yes I need that!" "it will be nice!" "of course"
3. Mention the expressions of gratitude after giving a help offer! "its always my job!' " happy to help!" "no problem!"
4. Mention the expressions of dissatisfaction after denying help offer ! "I'm sorry, what were you saying?" "I think its better for you to help me, I apologize"
5. does "can i help you?" includes as a help offer? yes it is
6. why do people denied help offers? because sometimes its not the same as their opinions or not enough time for doing the 'help'
7. why do people take help offers? because they think it would help them finishing some 'things' or 'works'
8. does "I cannot help you" count as offering help? no it doesn't because clearly it said "cannot help"
9. is it nice to ignore someone's offer? sometimes it is rude because we tend to ignore people for offering us help, we just need to say something like "sorry" or "I can help myself" without saying rude things like that "
10. why do people offer help? because they think they know the solutions of the work other peoples doing

28. apa tujuan advice dan offering,,, "purpose"???


Advice (nasihat) bertujuan untuk memberikan saran-saran yang bersifat membangun.
* the purpose of an Advice is kinda like an expression that contain some of suggestions  to motivate someone.
Offering ( penawaran) bertujuan untuk memberi seseorang sebuah bantuan yang bersifat mengajak untuk melakukan sesuatu.
* the purpose of an offering is to give someone some helps which have characteristic to ask someone to do it.
