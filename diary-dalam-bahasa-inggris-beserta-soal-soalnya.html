Recount Text

Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau. Â 

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

1. Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.

2. Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).

3. Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

1. Menggunakan Past tense

2. Menggunakan action verb

3. menggunakan thinking verb.

Pembahasan

Mini diary bisa dikategorikan sebagai recount text karena menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian lampau.

Contoh mini diary tentang kejadian di sekolah:

This morning I went to school late because I woke up late too. My teachers angry to me. Â He punish me to do push up 40 times.

After completing the punishment, I went to class. At that time, Math teacher was teaching our class. After I opened the door, he stared at me angrily. He scolded me and ordered me to stand in font of class until the math class ended.

I felt very tired at that time. At the break time, I want to buy a glass of iced tea in the canteen. Then I realized that I didn't bring any money. So I couldn't buy a glass of iced tea.

I kept being thirsty and hungry until the school was over. I ran quickly to my house. In the middle of my way to home, I was crushed by someone who rode a bicycle. I fell and got dirty. It was a bad day for me. I don't want to be late again.
