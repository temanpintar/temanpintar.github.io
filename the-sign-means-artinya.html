The sign that indicates "we must not park here" is typically the "No Parking" sign. This sign usually features a red circle with a diagonal line crossing a parked car symbol, indicating that parking is prohibited in the designated area. It serves as a clear indication to drivers that parking is not allowed in that specific location

The sign that means "we must not park here" is the international no-parking sign. It consists of a red circle with a diagonal slash across it, similar to a "no smoking" sign. The sign is commonly used in parking lots, streets, and other areas where parking is prohibited. It indicates that vehicles are not allowed to be parked in the designated area, and failure to comply may result in a fine or towing of the vehicle.

37. the sign beside means


Pada tanda disamping berarti?

Kata "beside" memiliki arti yang sama seperti "next to" yang berarti disamping


Contoh kalimat: I stand next to him


Kata beside tergolong dalam prepositions of place



PREPOSITIONS LAINNYA


Between


Mengartikan diantara 2 orang. Apabila diantara lebih dari 2 orang, maka prepositions yang tepat adalah "in the middle of ..."


Contoh: He sit between Judy and Rudy.



Behind


Mengartikan dibelakang


Contoh: the library is behind the class.



On, above, over


Ketiganya memiliki arti yang sama, namun penggunaan yang berbeda. Arti ketiga prepositions tersebut adalah diatas.


Contoh

1. The lamp is over the classroom

2. The pencil is on the table

3. The baloon is above the ground
