Task 1: Think of yesterday. What did you do? What didn’t you do? List the verbs of your activities yesterday.

What did I do?

2. Sleeping

3. Running

4. Swimming

1. Play tennis

2. Open the WhatsApp

3. Reading some novels

4. Watch video on Tiktok

5. Go to zoo

Task 2: Make the sentences of task 1 to a simple past.

What did I do?

1. I do eating some snacks yesterday

2. I do sleeping yesterday

3. I do running yesterday  

4. I do swimming yesterday  

What didn’t I do?

1. I didn’t play tennis yesterday.

2. I didn’t open the WhatsApp yesterday.

3. I didn’t read some novels yesterday.

4. I didn’t watch some video on tiktok yesterday.

5. I didn’t go to zoo yesterday.

Pembahasan:

A subject is a part of a sentence that contains the person or thing performing the action (or verb) in a sentence. (See What is a verb?) Here are some examples:

Example : Jennifer walked to the store. In this sentence, the subject is "Jennifer" and the verb is "walked."

Verbs are words that describe an action or talk about something that happens. They take many different forms depending on their subjects, the time they refer to and other ideas we want to express.

Simple past tense adalah kalimat yang mengekspresikan kejadian yang sudah terjadi dimasa lampau. Verb yang digunakan yaitu verb 2. Terdapat 2 bentuk verb yakni:

Irregular (tidak beraturan) = Awake - Awoke

Regular (beraturan) = tambahkan -ed/-d di akhir kata kerja. = Stay - Stayed.

Contoh :

I played football with my friends yesterdayJK went to school with his new car last weekThey met me at school two days ago

11. Jawaban buku bahasa inggris kelas 12 halaman 12 k-13


Berikut ini adalah contoh dialog bahasa Inggris dalam situasi seperti yang diberikan dalam soal nomor 1:

1. (In this situation, the group already working on the project for a while).

Hassan: "Guys, we already working on our history project for almost two hours at this point. My part is nearly done. How is your part going?"

Me: "I'm almost done with mine! How about yours, Feli?"

Feli: "I'm really sorry beforehand, but I start to feel nauseous, dizzy and cold a while ago. I think I need to go home to rest. Can I finish my part and submit it later at night?"

Me: "The project deadline is tomorrow morning though."

Hassan: "Yeah, and you look very pale, Feli. I think it's better if you just rest tonight."

Me: "Hassan and I can work on the remaining of your part. Focus on your health first, Feli. Do you need a ride home?"

Feli: "My mom is going to pick me up in half an hour, so don't worry. "

Hassan: "Let's get to it while waiting for Feli to get picked up, then."

Pembahasan

Dialog merupakan percakapan yang dilakukan oleh dua orang atau lebih dalam suatu interaksi. Dialog dapat dilakukan secara tertulis maupun secara lisan. Secara tertulis, dialog biasanya dituliskan dalam dua tanda kutip yang mengawali dan mengakhiri masing-masing dialog.

Jawaban:

1. Mother: I'll be at the store for a while. I want you to clean the house while I'm away. I'll be back as soon as possible.

Me: Okay, I'll clean the house right away Mom.

2. Me: I can't come to our Japanese course this afternoon. Would you please tell our tutor about my absence, please?

My Friend: Yes, I'd tell him later.

3. Me (Leader): Please forward this message to everyone regarding our working bee plan to clean the environment. Thank you!

Member: Yes, sir! Right away!

4. Me: I'm sorry that I can't come on time to join our English club activity because I have to help my sister do her homework first.

My friend: That's okay, don't worry about that! It's better late than never.

5. Teacher: We are going to have a virtual trip to a zoo tomorrow! I want everyone to prepare their laptop, smartphone and internet connection ready!

Students: Yes teacher!

Penjelasan:

Untuk nomor 1. pola kalimat mengikuti Simple Present Tense & Future tense karena menyatakan kegiatan di masa depan.

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object (Simple Present Tense)

(+) Subject + Will + Verb-1 + Object (Simple Future Tense)

Untuk nomor 2. pola kalimat memakai Modal Verb:

(-) Subject + can + not + Verb-1 + Object

(?) Would + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + ?

Untuk nomor 3. Memakai pola kalimat Imperative:

Polite form: (+) Please, Verb-1 + Subject + Object

Untuk nomor 4. mengikuti pola kalimat Simple Present Tense dan Modal Verb:

Nominal: (+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adjective (kata sifat)

Modal Verb: (+) Subject + can + Verb-1 + Object

Untuk nomor 5. mengikuti pola kalimat Simple Future Tense dan Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + going to + Verb-1 + Object (Simple Future Tense)

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object
