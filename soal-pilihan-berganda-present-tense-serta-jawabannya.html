jawabane: c a d c b baffirmative form : subject + to be + base+ing
ex : - he is reading his new book
- they are testing out there handmade kites
- grandma is visiting uncle matthew

negative : subject + to be not + base+ing
- mom isn't cooking for lunch
- they aren't coming to the party
- the teacher isn't happy about that

interrogative : to be + subject + base+ing
- Is she going to school ?
- Are they mad at you?
- Is dad going to buy the groceries?
