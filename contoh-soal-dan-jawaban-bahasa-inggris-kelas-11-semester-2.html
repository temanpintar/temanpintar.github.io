2. contoh soal bahasa inggris dan jawabannya kelas 10 semester 2 soal HOTS​


Berikut ini adalah beberapa contoh soal Bahasa Inggris tingkat kelas 10 semester 2 dengan tingkat kesulitan tinggi (HOTS) beserta jawabannya:

Soal 1:

Read the following passage and answer the questions that follow.

Passage:

Climate change is a pressing global issue that requires immediate action. It is causing a rise in global temperatures, resulting in melting ice caps, sea-level rise, and extreme weather events. The consequences of climate change are far-reaching and impact both human and natural systems. To mitigate the effects of climate change, it is crucial to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, promote renewable energy sources, and implement sustainable practices.

Questions:

1. What are the consequences of climate change?

2. What measures can be taken to mitigate the effects of climate change?

Jawaban:

1. The consequences of climate change include melting ice caps, sea-level rise, and extreme weather events.

2. Measures that can be taken to mitigate the effects of climate change include reducing greenhouse gas emissions, promoting renewable energy sources, and implementing sustainable practices.

Soal 2:

Choose the correct word to complete the sentence.

1. The teacher asked the students to ________ their assignments by tomorrow.

a) submit

b) submitted

c) submitting

d) submits

Jawaban: a) submit

Soal 3:

Rearrange the following words to form a meaningful sentence.

easy / It / is / to / English / learn.

Jawaban: It is easy to learn English.

Soal 4:

Read the dialogue and answer the question that follows.

Tom: Have you ever been to Paris?

Sara: No, I haven't. But I would love to visit someday.

Question:

What is Sara's response to Tom's question?

Jawaban: Sara's response to Tom's question is, "No, I haven't. But I would love to visit someday."

Offering help and service merupakan suatu ungkapan dalam Bahasa Inggris yang berfungsi untuk menawarkan bantuan dan layanan kepada seseorang. Kunci jawaban Bahasa Inggris halaman 11 kelas 12 semester 1 antara lain sebagai berikut:

Patterns:

a. May I help you?

b. May I do it for you?

c. May I call a taxi for you? No, thanks. I am fine.

Responses:

a. Thanks a lot.

b. That will be very helpful.

c. Thanks.

Patterns:

a. What can I do for you?

b. What can I do to help you?

c. What can I read the text?

Responses:

a. Can you show me the dictionary, please?

b. Please, I want to read the book.

c. Nothing. I can finish it myself. Thanks for offering.

32. tugas bahasa inggris kelas 11 semester 2 .tolong jawab ya bantu please​


Jawaban:

1. Doesn't require

2. Be conducted

3. Occurs

4. Is produced

5. Is used

6. Are formed

8. Are inundated

9. Take in

Jawaban:

1. doesn't require

2. be conducted

3. occurs

4. is produced

5. is used

6. are formed

7. are overwatered

8. are inundated

9. take in

10. deprive

Penyelesaian:

Respiration occurs in all living cells, including leaves and roots. Since respiration doesn't require light energy, it can be conducted at night or during the day. It occurs when glucose, sugar which is produced during photosynthesis, combines with oxygen to produce useable cellular energy. This energy is used to fuel growth and all of the normal cellular functions. Carbon dioxide and water are formed as by-products of respiration.

However, respiration does require oxygen which can be problematic for roots which are overwatered or in soil with poor drainage. If roots are inundated for long periods of time, they cannot take in oxygen and convert glucose to maintain cell metabolic processes. As a result, water-logging and excessive irrigation can deprive roots of oxygen, kill root tissues, damage trees, and reduce yield.

Catatan:

- can (diikuti oleh V1 )

misal : can play, can read, can write

- bentuk pasif (is/are diikuti V3)

misal : is played, are written

is/are tergantung subyek yg di depannya, tunggal/jamak
