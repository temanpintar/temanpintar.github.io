4. jelaskan apa itu personal participant dalam bahasa Inggris, terimakasih ​


Jawaban:

"Personal participant" refers to an individual who is personally involved or participating in an activity or event, such as a conference or seminar, without representing a specific organization or company. The term is often used to distinguish individual participants from those who are representing an organization.

Penjelasan dalam Bahasa Indonesia:

"Personal participant" dalam bahasa Inggris mengacu pada seseorang yang terlibat atau berpartisipasi secara pribadi dalam suatu aktivitas atau acara, seperti konferensi atau seminar, tanpa mewakili organisasi atau perusahaan tertentu. Istilah ini sering digunakan untuk membedakan partisipan pribadi dari partisipan yang mewakili sebuah organisasi.

30. Merangkum dalam bahasa Inggris Personal Lettersekalian tolong jawab semua​


Jawaban:

Personal Letter:

A personal letter is a type of communication where individuals express their thoughts, feelings, and experiences to someone they have a personal connection with. It is usually written in an informal and conversational tone, allowing for a more intimate and personalized expression of thoughts and emotions.

Cause and Effects:

Cause and effect is a relationship between events or actions where one event (the cause) brings about another event (the effect). It explores the reasons why something happens (cause) and the consequences or results that follow (effect). This type of analysis helps to understand the underlying factors and outcomes of various phenomena, actions, or situations.

Song:

A song is a musical composition typically consisting of lyrics and melody. It is a form of artistic expression that combines words and music to convey emotions, tell a story, or express ideas. Songs can evoke various emotions and can be used for entertainment, self-expression, social commentary, or communication of personal experiences.

Explanation Text:

Explanation text is a type of non-fiction text that aims to explain or clarify a particular subject or concept. It provides factual information, definitions, processes, or descriptions to help readers understand a specific topic. Explanation texts are often found in educational materials, scientific articles, manuals, or guides and focus on presenting information in a clear and concise manner.
