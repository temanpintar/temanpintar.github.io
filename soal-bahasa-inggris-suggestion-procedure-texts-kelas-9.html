5. Ada tugas bahasa inggris kelas 11 tentang suggestion, ada yang bisa bantu? :)


Explanation:


Giving suggestion is a type of expression which is used when you want to give an advice or recommendation for someone.

Here are some expressions that you can use:

- You should ...

- You ought to ...

- It's better for you to ...

- I suggest you ...

- I advise you to ...

- Why don't we/you ... ?

- How about ...?


Answer:

Here's the suggestion sentence for each statement

1. Be economical with detergent and washing-up liquids. a little will probably wash just as clean as a lot.

Suggestion: We should be economical with detergent and washing-up liquids because a little will probably wash just as clean as a lot.


2. If you are buying a car, remember that small cars pollute less than big ones.

Suggestion: It's best not to buy a big car because big cars pollute more than small ones.


3. Try not to throw away household equipment that is still usable just because a new model is on the market

Suggestion: The economists suggest us not to throw away household equipment that is still usable just because a new model is on the market.


4. Avoid too many 'disposables' like paper plates, cups, if you can't burn them.

Suggestion: The ecologists recommend that we should avoid too many 'disposables' like paper plates, cups, if we can't burn them.


5. Buy non-coloured toilet paper. the dyes used in colored paper pollute water.

Suggestion: The environmentalists suggest that we should buy non-coloured toilet paper because the dyes used in colored paper can pollute water.


6. Try making a compost heap in the garden. It enriches the land.

Suggestion: The Greenpeace members suggest we should try making a compost heap in the garden because It can enrich the land.

12. persamaan dan perbedaan procedure text dan manual procedure (text dalam bahasa Inggris) ​


Jawaban:

helloooo saya bantu jawab yah :)

PERSAMAAN (equality/sameness)

1. General Purpose (tujuan umum): Both procedure text and manual procedure have the general purpose of providing instructions or guidance on how to perform a specific action or process.

2. Similar Structure (struktur serupa): They both have a similar structure, consisting of steps or a sequence of actions that must be followed by the reader or user.

DIFFERENCE (perbedaan)

1. Content and Expertise (konten dan keahlian): Procedure Text is for everyday tasks like cooking or personal care and is easy to understand. Manual Procedure is for technical or professional tasks, often requiring specialized knowledge.

2. Context (konteks): Procedure Text is used in everyday situations, while Manual Procedure is used in technical or industrial settings where precision and safety are critical.

14. Contoh soal tentang expression suggestion dalam bahasa inggris


Giving Suggestion

Giving suggestion atau memberi saran adalah ungkapan yang digunakan seseorang untuk memberikan saran atau nasihat kepada orang lain.

Pattern yang digunakan untuk giving suggestion adalah:

Would you be so kind enough to + verb

Contoh kalimat:

Would you be so kind enough to play with me?

Would you be so kind enough to watch movie with me?

I would be so grateful if you would.....

Contoh kalimat:

I would be so grateful if you would play me.

I would be so grateful if you would watch movie with me.

How/What about... + Gerund

Contoh Kalimat:

How about watching movies?

How about playing football?

Suppose

Contoh kalimat:

Suppose that you want to play with me.

Suppose that you want to watch movies me.

How to Make Brownies 


Ingredients
• 150gram of butter.
• 150gram of dark chocolate.
• 300 gram of flour                 
• 4 eggs.
• 600 gram of regular sugar
• 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps
1. Melt the chocolate with the butter.
2. Stir the eggs with the sugar and the vanilla extract.
3. Preheat the oven at low temperature
4. Combine the mixes you made.
5. Add the flour and stir with a wooden spoon.
6. Grease a brownie tin.
7. Add a little flour to cover the tin.
8. Add the brownie dough.
9. Bake for 10-30 minutes.
10. To check the brownies, put a knife in the mix. The knife must be moist!
11. Eat them 15 minutes after you took them out of the oven.

32. 10 contoh soal dan jawaban tentang giving suggestion (dalam bahasa inggris)


Giving suggestion (memberikan saran) diucapkan oleh seseorang kepada lawan bicaranya dengan tujuan untuk menyampaikan saran atau masukan kepada lawan bicaranya, agar orang tersebut bersedia melaksanakan saran atau masukannya. Saran atau suggestion yang diberikan ini bisa berupa nasihat, pendapat, gagasan, dan yang semacamnya.

Beberapa contoh ekspresi yang sering digunakan untuk giving suggestion di antaranya adalah:

I think .......

What about .............

What if ....................

You should ....................

Let's ..............................

Maybe you want to ............................

How if .......

How about .....

Contoh 10 pertanyaan pilihan ganda tentang giving suggestion.

1. Tom looks very tired. What's your suggestion to him?

a. you should eat more.

b. you should study harder

c. I think you ought to get some rest

d. what if you go for a walk?

jawaban: c

2. Linda is working on her homework, but she finds some difficult questions. What will you suggest her?

a. I think you should stop studying.

b. maybe you can sleep now.

c. What if you drink to refresh yourself?

d. what about going to your friend's house to study together?

jawaban: d

3. A: .............................. go to the cinema and watch a good film?

B: That's a good idea

a. you must

b. you have

c. I think you should

d. what if we

jawaban: d

4. A: I feel drowsy.

B: ........................................

a. you should take a rest.

b. what if you study harder?

c. what about calling your friend for help?

d. you had better call the police.

jawaban: a

5. This is Saturday night. What will you suggest your friend?

a. I think we should go to the town square for sight seeing.

b. what about going to the doctor?

c. you should speak to your teacher.
