How to properly wash your hands.

First of all, you have to prepare three main things: soap, clean water, and a towel. Please remember that the water must be flowing water to safely wash all the dirt and disease away. Second, pour water into your hands completely, and wash your hands with soap. Don't forget to wash between your finger. Do this washing for at least 30 seconds. Next, wash your hands with water cleanly so there's no soap left on your hands. And finally, dry your hands with a towel.Pembahasan

Membuat teks yang berisi instruksi termasuk ke dalam procedural text, yaitu teks yang berisi urutan proses melaksanakan suatu kegiatan atau proses membuat sesuatu.

Ada ciri khas kata-kata yang bisa digunakan dalam membuat procedural text, yaitu kita membuat kata-kata penghubung seperti next. Kita dapat menggunakan next sebagai penghubung  untuk merujuk pada sesuatu yang mengikuti segera setelah sesuatu sebelumnya. Kata-kata di awal instruksi bisa berupa: first of all.

Kita bisa pula menggunakan kata-kata : First, second, third, dan seterusnya untuk menunjukkan urutan instruksi. Untuk instruksi terakhir bisa kita pakai: finally (akhirnya) untuk menutup instruksi.

Semua procedural text di atas kita terapkan pada teks instruksi yang kita buat di atas, seperti ini terjemahannya:

Cara mencuci tangan yang benar.

Pertama-tama, Anda harus menyiapkan tiga hal utama: sabun, air bersih, dan handuk. Harap diingat bahwa air harus mengalir untuk membersihkan semua kotoran dan penyakit dengan aman. Kedua, tuangkan air ke tangan Anda sepenuhnya, dan cuci tangan Anda dengan sabun. Jangan lupa untuk mencuci sela-sela jari Anda. Lakukan pencucian ini setidaknya selama 30 detik. Selanjutnya, cuci tangan dengan air bersih agar tidak ada sisa sabun di tangan. Dan terakhir, keringkan tangan Anda dengan handuk.

1. Purpose

This procedure will gave the good method how to wash the hand in British laboratory. Recently, we sometime forget to wash the hands correctly.

2. Scope

These procedures are used to all the students, the work officers, laboratory assistants , instructions and all the people who want to enter the laboratory in British laboratory.

3. Responsibility

All of the people who want to enter and leave the laboratory must wash their hands cleanly in British laboratory. Everyone has a responsibility to keep the cleanness their selves and the labaroty.

4. Material and Procedure

1. Material

· Sink with hot water

· Sink with cold wate

· Clean small towel

· Laboratory coat

· Hair cover

2. Procedure

· Make sure that there is not something on

your fingers, hand and wrist. Such

as jewelry, ring, bracelet, watch and other

accessories

· Wear the Laboratory coat

· Wear Hair cover

· Keep ready the towel

· Pour the hot water and clod in the sink, it

must not be too hot and not be too cold, it is

depended on your want

· Wash your hand regularly

· Apply the liquid soap or bar of soap. Make

sure that all your fingers, twist and hand are

washed for 1,5 minutes

· Rinse your hands and dry them by the dry

towel.

· Finish

1.Wet your hands (to the wrist) with clean, running water (the temperature doesn’t matter). Turn off the tap, and apply a good amount of soap.

2.Lather up the soap by rubbing your hands together. Don’t forget to spread that lather to the backs of your hands up to your wrists, between your fingers, and under your nails.

3.Scrub your hands for at least 20 seconds. No one expects the rest of us to scrub as much, but that gives you an idea of what is needed to really kill most germs.

4.Rinse your hands thoroughly under clean, running water.

Dry your hands using a clean paper towel (best bet), hand dryer (OK), or let them air dry (in a pinch).

40. text prosedur cara mencuci tangan dalam bahasa inggris minimal 3 menit​


Procedure text adalah teks yang memberikan petunjuk melakukan sesuatu dengan benar. Ciri dari procedure text adalah adanya urutan langkah yang bertujuan agar hasil yang diperoleh sesuai dengan harapan penulis.

Pembahasan

Procedure text cara mencuci tangan dalam bahasa Inggris yaitu

How to Wash Hands to Avoid Coronavirus

Respiratory viruses like coronavirus disease (COVID-19) spread when mucus or droplets containing the virus get into your body through your eyes, nose or throat. Most often, this happens through your hands. Hands are also one of the most common ways that the virus spreads from one person to the next.  During a global pandemic, one of the cheapest, easiest, and most important ways to prevent the spread of a virus is to wash your hands frequently with soap and water.

To wash your hands, you only need clean water, soap, and towel or paper tissue.

To eliminate all traces of the virus on your hands, a quick scrub and a rinse won’t cut it. Below is a step-by-step process for effective handwashing.

First, wet your hands with running water.

Second, apply enough soap to cover wet hands.

Next, scrub all surfaces of the hands, including back of hands, between fingers and under nails for at least 20 seconds.

Then, rinse thoroughly with running water.

And the last step, dry hands with a clean cloth or single-use towel.
