5. Apa bedanya like dan likes bahasa inggris


Like, sebagai kata kerja, berarti suka/menyukai.

Penggunaan like atau likes tergantung pada subject-nya. Bila subject tunggal, menggunakan likes. Bila subject jamak, menggunakan like.

I, you, they, we = like

she, he, it, name = likes

contoh dalam kalimat

1. I like to eat fried chicken

2. She likes to drink orange juice.

3. We like to play football.

4. My father likes to read magazine

5. The students like to study in the library.

6. Everyone likes to sing that song together.

Everyone, meski artinya setiap orang tetapi tetap dianggap tunggal, karena everyone merupakan kata ganti (pronoun).

Roseanne Park (born February 11, 1997 in Auckland, New Zealand) better known as Rosé, is an Australian singer and dancer, currently based in South Korea. She is a member of BLACKPINK.

Life and career

Rose's birth name is Roseanne Park and her Korean name is Park Chaeyoung. Her family consists of her mother, a businesswoman, her father, a lawyer, and sister, Alice Park, who is four years her senior and also a lawyer. She was born in Auckland, New Zealand and raised in Melbourne, Australia.

As a child, Rose already had a passion for singing, and was a part of her church's choir. When she found out that YG Entertainment were holding their auditions in Sydney, Australia back in 2012, she quickly went there coming all the way from Melbourne to audition. Rose ranked first in the auditions, and she became a part of YG Entertainment on the same day. After training for four years, YG finally put her as the main vocalist and lead dancer of Blackpink.
