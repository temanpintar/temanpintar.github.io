Simple Present Tense

Penulisan kalimat dalam bentuk simple present tense mengikuti pola/rumusan berikut:

Bentuk verbal:

(+) S + verb-1(+s/es) ...(-) S + do/does + not + verb-1 ...(?) Do/Does + S + verb-1 ... ?

Bentuk nominal:

(+) S + am/is/are ...(-) S + am/is/are + not ...(?) Am/Is/Are + S ... ?

Dengan kata "There":

(+) There is/are ...(-) There is/are + not ...(?) Is/Are there ... ?
Pembahasan

Bagian 1 - Tuliskan 5 kalimat dalam bentuk positif, negatif dan interogatif:

1. (+) Susan walks to her office.

(-) Susan doesn't walk to her office.

(?) Does Susan walk to her office?

2. (+) The boys enjoy playing football.

(-) The boys don't enjoy playing football.

(?) Do the boys enjoy playing football?

3. (+) I usually go to school by bicycle.

(-) I don't usually go to school by bicycle.

(?) Do you usually go to school by bicycle?

4. (+) Michael is very good at physics.

(-) Michael is not very good at physics.

(?) Is Michael good at physics?

5. (+) The girls are in the gym.

(-) The girls are not in the gym.

(?) Are the girls in the gym?


Bagian 2 - Pertanyaan dengan 'How many/How much...' beserta jawaban menggunakan 'There is/There are ...'

There are 35 students in my class.

2. How much petrol is (there) in the tank?

There is about 3 litres of petrol in the tank.

3. How many books are (there) in the box?

There are 30 books in the box.

4. How much water is (there) in the can?

There is no water in the can.

5. How many days are (there) in April?
