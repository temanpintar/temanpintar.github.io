2. contoh describing place wisata tangkuban parahu (memakai bahasa inggris)


   TANGKUBAN PERAHU MOUNT

Tangkuban perahu is a volcano, 25 kilometers north of Bandung direction of Lembang. Its location is in between Sagalaherang village, Sagalaherang district, Subang regency and Cikole village, Lembang district, Bandung regency. This is Bandung's most famous tourist volcano just 28 km north of the city. This volcano offers many places to see and explore. Whether we look into the huge crater or hike down into it, stroll through the forest on its slopes, or simply enjoy the splendid panoramic scenery. Mount Tangkuban Perahu is an interesting destination that everyone in the Bandung area is fond of visiting. When seen from Bandung, Mount Tangkuban Perahu has a distinctive shape, like an upside down boat. Tangkuban Perahu means, in fact, "up-turned boat" This peculiar shape has stimulated the fantasy of the Sundanese people from early times as expressed in the Legend of Sangkuriang. Geologically, Mount Tangkuban Perahu has played a significant role in the development of Parahyangan highlands. Eruptions have contributed immensely to the hills north of Bandung through lava flowing into the valleys and hardening into rock, thus forming big cliffs over which waterfalls leap. Likewise, mud flows have formed a semi-circular cone of gentle gradient , which is now a mass that blocked the valley of the ancient Citarum River near present day in Padalarang (some 18 km west of Bandung), this caused a lake to form covering the whole Bandung plain.

22. ungkapan describing history place


contoh describing history place tentang Borobudur 

Borabudur – located in Magelang, Central Java, Indonesia – is a famous nineteenth – century Mahayana Buddhist Temple. The monument consists of six square, nine stacked platforms, and 3 circulars, topped by a central dome. The temple is beautified with 2,672 relief panels and 504 Buddha statues. The central dome is circled by 72 Buddha statues, each seated inside a perforated stupa. It is the world’s largest Buddhist temple in the world.

37. Make a descriptive text! - Describing People - Describing Place Describing animal - Describing thing jawaban​


Jawaban:

Descriptive Text - Describing People:

My best friend, Sarah, is a tall and slender girl with long, wavy brown hair that cascades down her back. She has big, bright blue eyes that sparkle when she laughs, and a wide, infectious smile that lights up the room. Sarah is always dressed in the latest fashion, with a keen sense of style that makes her stand out in a crowd. She is also incredibly kind and thoughtful, always putting others first and going out of her way to help those in need. Sarah is the kind of person who makes you feel better just by being around her, and I feel lucky to have her as my friend.

Descriptive Text - Describing Place:

The beach is my favorite place to relax and unwind. The soft sand stretches out for miles, and the sound of the waves crashing against the shore is both soothing and invigorating. The salty sea air fills my lungs, and the warm sun on my skin makes me feel alive. I love walking along the water's edge, feeling the cool water wash over my feet, and searching for shells and other treasures that have washed up on the shore. The beach is a place of peace and tranquility for me, where I can escape the stresses of everyday life and just be in the moment.

Descriptive Text - Describing Animal:

The majestic tiger is a truly awe-inspiring creature. With its striking orange and black stripes, powerful muscles, and piercing eyes, it commands respect and admiration from all who see it. The tiger is a skilled hunter, capable of taking down prey much larger than itself with ease. It moves with a grace and agility that is both beautiful and terrifying, and its roar can be heard from miles away. Despite its fearsome reputation, the tiger is also a devoted parent, fiercely protective of its cubs and willing to risk everything to keep them safe. The tiger is a symbol of strength, power, and beauty, and it is no wonder that it is revered by so many.

Descriptive Text - Describing Thing:

My favorite possession is my old, beat-up guitar. It may not look like much, with its chipped paint and worn strings, but to me, it is a thing of beauty. The guitar has been with me through thick and thin, always there to provide comfort and solace when I need it most. Its rich, warm tones fill the room, and the melodies that I play on it are like a balm for my soul. The guitar is more than just an instrument to me; it is a friend, a confidant, and a source of endless inspiration. I will always treasure my old guitar, and I know that it will be with me for many years to come.
