9. tolong buatkan dialog bahasa inggris 3 orang dengan menggunakan expressions admitting, promising, blaming, accusing


Explanation:

Expression of admitting is a kind of expression that is used when someone admits that he/she is the one who makes a mistake.
Here are some expression of admitting that you can use:
- Yes, I did
- I'm sorry, it is my mistake
- Yes, I admit what I have done is wrong

Expression of promising is a kind of expression that is used when we want to make a promise to someone.
Here are some expression of promising that you can use:
- I swear that...
- I promise you that ...
- I can assure you that ...

Expression of blaming is a kind of expression that is used when someone blames another person for a mistake happened.
Here are some expressions of blaming that you can use:
- It's your fault
- I think you are the one who could have done it
- You are the one to blame

Expression of accusing is a kind of expression that is used when someone said that another person is the one who makes a mistake although he/she doesn't know whether it's right or wrong
Here are some expressions of accusing that you can use:
- It's ... fault
- It must have been ... fault
- ... must be the doer

Answer:
Here's the example dialogue using the expression of admitting, promising, blaming, and accusing.

A: *Looking for something*
B: Are you looking for something, A?
A: Yes, I'm looking for my wallet. It is gone.
B: Where did you put it?
A: I put it on my table during break time.
B: Oh really? I was in the classroom during break time.
A: You were in the classroom during break time? I think you are the one who could have done it!
B: You are wrong. I didn't do it. I was doing my homework. It must be C's fault. She's the only one who entered the classroom during break time.
*C comes*
A: Did you take my wallet, C?
C: You wallet? How does it look like?
A: It's a square red wallet with a teddy bear face in front.
C: Oh, I saw it.
A: Did you take it?
C: Yes, I did
A: What did you do that?
C: I thought it was dangerous to put a wallet on the table so i took it to keep it safe. I'm sorry, it is my mistake.
A: I forgive you, please don't do this anymore.
C: I promise you that I won't do it anymore.
A: Please keep your promise.

Detil tambahan:
Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: Bahasa inggris
Kategori: Expression of admitting, promising, blaming, and accusing

One day a man went for a walk on the beach. He saw that a ship full of passengers struck a rock and capsized in the sea. The ship went under water and all the people on board fell into the sea. As they did not know how to swim, they drowned one by one. The man watched all this from the shore but could not do anything to help the people. 

In the evening, he narrated the sad event of the ship's drowning to his friends. Everyone felt sorry for the dead people. The man said, "God has not been just. To kill one sinner aboard the ship he killed so many other innocent people." 

As he was saying this, he felt a pinch on his feet. He looked down. A red ant was biting his toe. Many other red ants were near his feet. In anger, he started stamping his feet to crush the red ants. 

God appeared at the spot and said, "See, how you are killing other innocent ants to kill the one which bit you. You were blaming me for my unjust act. Look at your own actions before blaming me for anything." 

The man felt ashamed as he realized his mistake.
