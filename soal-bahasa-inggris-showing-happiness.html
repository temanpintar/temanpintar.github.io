16. Ringkasan Materi Bahasa Inggris SHOWING APRECIATION​


Showing appreciation adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memberi penghargaan atau pujian atau komentar terhadap seseorang mengenai penampilan, prestasi, dan sebagainya. Showing appreciation ini bisa juga disebut Admiration

Expression of Compliments/Appreciation:

Well done!

That’s great!

How beautiful you are!

What a nice shirt!

Nice work!

You’re great!

I appreciate your effort

How clever you are!

I like you ....

Responses:

Thank you

That’s very kind of you

Yeah, thanks

It’s nothing

I’m glad you like it

Thanks. It’s nice of you to say so

Do you really think so?

Many thanks

Untuk memberi pujian atau kekaguman kepada seseorang kita dapat menggunakan kalimat kekaguman dengan menggunakan “How/What”. How atau what di sini bukan berarti kata tanya atau question word tetapi berati “Alangkah, betapa atau sungguh”

Contoh:

How handsome you are! (Alangkah gantengnya kamu)How smart you are! (Betapa pintarnya kamu)How clean this room is! (Sungguh bersih ruangan ini)What a kind man he is! (Betapa baiknya laki-laki itu)What a nice t-shirt! (Betapa bagus kaos ini)What an expensive motorcycle it is! (Alangkah mahalnya sepeda motor itu)What cheap laptops they are! (Sungguh murah laptop-laptop itu)

Demikian, semoga bermanfaat.

35. Dialog bahasa inggris untuk 4 orang materi asking for repetition, showing attention,showing admiration


Explanation:

Asking for repetition is an expression which is used when we hear something unclearly and want our partner to repeat what they said.
Expression of asking for repetition:
- What did you say?
- Pardon?
- Sorry, can you repeat it?
- I'm sorry, I didn't hear you.
- Sorry, I don't follow you.

Showing attention is an expression which is used to show that you are interested in the topic that your speaking partner is talking about.
Expression of showing attention:
- Really?
- Are you serious?
- Tell me more about it.
- And then what?
- What's next?

Showing admiration is an expression which is used to show that you admire someone's work, achievement, or appearance.
Expression of showing admiration:
- What a...!
- How...!
- It is awesome!
- That is amazing!
- Wow. You look ...!


Answer:
Here's the example dialogue using those expressions above for 4 persons.
Billy: Hey guys, have you heard the news?
Kylie: No.
Tasya: what news?

Mike: Sorry, what did you say?
Billy: I said Grace Allison won the first place at the Chemistry International Competition.
Mike: She won the first place? Tell me more about it.
Billy: Yeah, she won the first place after beating Bob Flash.
Mike: She beat Bob Flash? That's awesome! I heard he is unbeatable.
