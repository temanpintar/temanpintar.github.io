12. Susi has brought all the books before I read it. The PASSIVE VOICE form is _____ A. All the books was brought by Susi before I read it. B. All the books has been brought by Susi before I read it. C. All the books were brought by Susi before I read it. D. All the books have been brought by Susi before I read it. E.BAll the books are brought by Susi before I read it.


Jawaban:

All the books have been brought by Susi before I read it.

jadi jawabannya adalah D

Penjelasan:

Rumus aktifnya

S + HAVE + V3 + O

Rumus passive nya

S + HAVE + BEEN + V3 + O

Subject jafikan object

object jadikan subject
