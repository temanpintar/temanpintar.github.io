28. turunan fungsi dari f (x) = cosec x adalah...


[tex]f(x)=\csc x\\f(x)=\frac{1}{\sin x}\\\\u=1\\u'=0\\v=\sin x\\v'=\cos x\\\\f'(x)=\frac{u'v-uv'}{v^2}\\f'(x)=\frac{(0)(\sin x)-(1)(\cos x)}{(\sin x)^2}\\f'(x)=\frac{-\cos x}{(\sin x)(\sin x)}\\f'(x)=\frac{-\cot x}{\sin x}\\f'(x)=-\cot x\csc x[/tex]

[tex]\huge{\green{\sf Soal~pertama}~:}[/tex]

Karena[tex]~\cosec~x=\frac{1}{\sin~x},~[/tex]maka nilai [tex]~\cosec~x~[/tex]mengikuti nilai dari [tex]~\sin~x,~[/tex]dan nilai [tex]~\sin~x~[/tex]positif terdapat pada sudut yang berada pada [tex]\red{\huge{\sf kuadran~I~dan~II}}[/tex]

[tex]\\[/tex]

[tex]\huge{\green{\sf Soal~kedua}~:}[/tex]

[tex]\begin{array}{rcl}\cosec~400\degree&=&\frac{1}{\sin~400\degree}\\~\\&=&\frac{1}{\sin~(360\degree+40\degree)}\\~\\&=&\frac{1}{\sin~360\degree.\cos~40\degree+\cos~360\degree.\sin~40\degree}\\~\\&=&\frac{1}{(0).(\cos~40\degree)+(1).(\sin~40\degree)}\\~\\&=&\frac{1}{\sin~40\degree}\end{array}[/tex]

[tex]\red{\huge{\cosec~400\degree=\cosec~40\degree}}[/tex]

Dari perhitungan di atas :

[tex]\begin{array}{rcl}\cosec~400\degree&=&\frac{1}{\sin~40\degree}\\~\\&=&\frac{1}{\sin~(90\degree-50\degree)}\\~\\&=&\frac{1}{\sin~90\degree.\cos~50\degree-\cos~90\degree.\sin~50\degree}\\~\\&=&\frac{1}{(1).(\cos~50\degree)-(0).(\sin~50\degree)}\\~\\&=&\frac{1}{\cos~50\degree}\end{array}[/tex]

[tex]\red{\huge{\cosec~400\degree=\sec~50\degree}}[/tex]

32. Turunan pertama y = cosec (3x + 5) adalah​


Turunan pertama dari fungsi trigonometri y = cosec (3x + 5) adalah y' = - 3 cosec (3x + 5) cotan (3x + 5). Dapat diselesaikan menggunakan aturan rantai.

y = cosec (3x + 5)

Ditanyakan:

y'?

[tex]\frac{dy}{dx} \:=\: \frac{dy}{du} \times \frac{du}{dx}[/tex]

Misalkan u = 3x + 5 [tex]\frac{du}{dx} \:=\: 3[/tex] y = cosec u [tex]\frac{dy}{du} \:=\: - cosec \: u \: cotan \: u[/tex]

[tex]\frac{dy}{dx} \:=\: - cosec \: u \: cotan \: u \times 3[/tex]

[tex]\frac{dy}{dx} \:=\: - 3 cosec \: u \: cotan \: u[/tex]

y' = - 3 cosec (3x + 5) cotan (3x + 5)

33. Turunan dari f(x) = cosec 4x


f(x) = cosec(4x)
f(x) = 1/(sin(4x))

misal :
u = 1
u' = 0
v = sin(4x)
v' = 4cos(4x)

f'(x) = (u'v - uv') / (v^2)
= (0.sin(4x) - 1.4cos(4x)) / (sin^2(4x))
= (4cos(4x)) / (sin^2(4x))
= 4.cos(4x)/sin(4x) * 1/sin(4x)
= 4.cotan(4x).cosec(4x)
