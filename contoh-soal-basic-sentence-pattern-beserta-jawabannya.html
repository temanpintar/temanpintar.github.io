Rumus dari future continuous adalah..

Answer :

Future continuous :

(+) S + Will + be + V-ing + O

(-) S + Will + not + be - V-ing + O

(?) Will + S + be + V-ing + O

Contoh 1 :

(+) They will be playing football at eight o'clock tomorrow morning:

(-) They will not be playing football at eight o'clock tomorrow morning.

(?) Will they be playing football at eight o'clock tomorrow morning?

Contoh 2 :

(+) She will be going to the movie.

(-) She will not be going to the movie.

(?) Will she be going to the movie?

Future continuous adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan suatu kegiatan/perbuatan yang akan sedang terjadi.

Tanda waktu nya :

At (eleven o'clock) tomorrow morningAt this time tomorrowAt the same time tomorrowAth this time next week

Struktur Teks Prosedur Kompleks

Dalam pembentukannya, teks prosedur memiliki tiga struktur diantaranya yaitu tujuan, material dan langkah-langkah.

✨Bagian Tujuan

Bagian tujuan teks prosedur bisa berupa judul dan juga berisikan tujuan dari pembuatan teks prosedur tersebut atau hasil akhir yang akan di capai jika kita melakukan tahapan pada teks prosedur tersebut.

✨Bagian Material

Bagian material teks prosedur berisi mengenai bahan, alat atau material yang dibutuhkan. Tapi tidak semua teks prosedur terdapat bagian ini. Pada umumnya penggunaan bagian material terdapat pada teks prosedur tentang pembuatan suatu hal seperti makanan dan sebagainya.

✨Bagian Langkah-Langkah

Bagian ini berisi mengenai langkah-langkah yang harus dilewati untuk mendapatkan hasil sesuai dengan tujuan teks prosedur. Pada bagian ini setiap langkah harus dilakukan dengan urut tidak boleh secara acak.

30. white pattern (past tense) and give sentence exampleplease bantu jawab​


Jawaban:

Examples of past tense sentences:

(Contoh kalimat past tense)

(+) Alex saw Rina doing her homework yesterday.(-) Alex didn't see Rina doing her homework yesterday.(?) Did Alex see Rina doing her homework yesterday?

Penjelasan:

Past tense is used to describe an activity done in the past.

The pattern for past tense sentences is as follows:

(+) subject + verb2 + object(-) subject + did not/didn't + verb1 + object(?) Did + subject + verb1 + object?

verb2 = the past form of verb1 (the present form of verb)

Examples:

verb2 of "see" is "saw"verb2 of "meet" is "met"verb2 of "catch" is "caught"verb2 of "eat" is "ate"verb2 of "drink" is "drank"

Jadi yang di kiri itu verb1-nya, yang di kanan itu verb2-nya.

Contoh kalimat:

(+) Alex saw Rina doing her homework yesterday.(-) Alex didn't see Rina doing her homework yesterday.(?) Did Alex see Rina doing her homework yesterday?

Nah, verb2 "see" itu = "saw", jadi rule verb-2-nya berlaku buat (+) aja, sisanya pake see aja.

Contohnya lagi:

(+) Nino met Elsa at the bar last night.(-) Nino didn't meet Elsa at the bar last night.(?) Did Nino meet Elsa at the bar last night?

Nah, verb2 "meet" itu = "met", jadi rule verb-2-nya berlaku buat (+) aja, sisanya pake meet aja.

Contohnya lagi:

(+) Rendy caught some fish for dinner the day before.(-) Rendy didn't catch any fish for dinner the day before.(?) Did Rendy catch any fish for dinner the day before?

Nah, verb2 "catch" itu = "caught", jadi rule verb-2-nya berlaku buat (+) aja, sisanya pake catch aja.

31. please write the sentence pattern of present tense!​


The sentence pattern of present tense

1. Verbal Sentence(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O/C(-) S + Do/Does + not + V1 + O/C(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O/C?2. Nominal Sentence (+) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adj/Adv/Noun(-) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + Adj/Adv/Noun(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + S + Adj/Adv/Noun?

PEMBAHASAN Present Tense

➡️ Digunakan untuk menyatakan:

Kegiatan berulang/rutinKebiasaan (habitual) hal-hal bersifat umum (fakta).

➡️ Ket. waktu Present Tense

Every morningalwaysusuallyOften

Rumus Present Tense

1. Verbal Sentence adalah kalimat verba, yaitu kalimat yang ada kata kerja (verb) di dalamnya. Contohnya:

(+) You drink a glass of milk every morning(-) You do not drink a glass of milk every morning(?) do you drink a glass of milk every morning?

➡️Subject + Auxiliary

I, you, we, they + doHe, She, it + does

2. Nominal Sentence adalah kalimat Nomina, yaitu kalimat yg tidak ada kata kerja di dalam kalimatnya. Contohnya:

(+) You are a good person (-) You are not a good person (?) are you a good person?

➡️ Subject + Tobe

I + amYou, we, they + areHe, She, it + is
