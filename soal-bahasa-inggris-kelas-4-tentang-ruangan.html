Jawaban:

1. **They study in the classroom.**

- (+) They study in the classroom.

- (-) They do not study in the classroom.

- (?) Do they study in the classroom?

2. **He went to the market yesterday.**

- (+) He went to the market yesterday.

- (-) He did not go to the market yesterday.

- (?) Did he go to the market yesterday?

3. **My uncle is painting in the living room.**

- (+) My uncle is painting in the living room.

- (-) My uncle is not painting in the living room.

- (?) Is my uncle painting in the living room?

4. **My friend and I played soccer in the field this morning.**

- (+) My friend and I played soccer in the field this morning.

- (-) My friend and I did not play soccer in the field this morning.

- (?) Did my friend and I play soccer in the field this morning?
