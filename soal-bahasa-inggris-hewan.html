Animal: Monkey

Habitat: tropical forest with trees and branches. Food: they are omnivore, so they eat smaller animal, worm, insects, fruits.Activity: They swing through the trees and branches. Characteristics: Monkey has hair with various color, top speed run reaching 35 mph and weighing 6-12 Kg. Pembahasan

Soal di atas meminta kita untuk menuliskan berbagai ciri-ciri dan characteristics (karakteristik) monkey (monyet). Hewan tersebut bisa kamu amati di kebun binatang di kota kamu. Atau bila tidak ada kebun binatang, kamu bisa melakukan searching (pencarian) di perpustakaan sekolah, toko buku atau internet tentang hewan ini. Jawaban:

Owls

Owls are a group of birds that are members of the order Strigiformes. This bird belongs to the class of birds of prey ( carnivores , meat eaters) and is a night animal ( nocturnal ). In all, there are about 222 known species, which range worldwide except Antarctica , most of Greenland , and a few outlying islands.

Owls are known for their large, forward-facing eyes , unlike most other types of birds whose eyes face sideways. Together with the sharp hooked beak like an eagle 's beak and the arrangement of feathers on the head that form a circular face , the "face" of this owl is impressive and sometimes scary. Moreover, this bird's neck is so flexible that its face can rotate 180 degrees backwards .

Generally owls have mottled, brownish or gray fur with black and white patches. Combined with its behavior which is often frozen and does not move much, this bird is not easily seen; so even when sleeping during the day under the protection of leaves.

The owl's tail is generally short, but the wings are large and wide. Its wingspan reaches about three times the length of its body.
