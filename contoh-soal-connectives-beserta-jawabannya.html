Kategori: clause of contrast
Pembahasan:

There are several connectors for saying clause of contrast.Here they are.
- although/ eventhough
- despite/ in spite of
- in contrast/ on the contrary.

Example:
- Although he was injured, he managed to score a wonderful goal.
- Despite his injury, he managed to score a wonderful goal.
- The police needs to be a role model for people. Yet, on the contrary, they are still like what they used to be, the one you should be avoiding before problems pop up.

3. haloo, tolong jelasin tentang time connectives yaa, sama contoh paragraf yang pakai time connectives tentang diri sendiri, TOLONG BANGEETT DIBANTU DIKUMPULIN BESOK PAGII ​


Bolehhhhh, time connectives adalah kata atau frasa yang digunakan untuk menghubungkan peristiwa atau aksi dalam urutan waktu tertentu dalam penulisan atau pembicaraan. Mereka membantu membentuk alur cerita atau urutan kronologis dan membantu pembaca atau pendengar memahami kapan peristiwa-peristiwa tersebut terjadi. Berikut adalah beberapa contoh time connectives umum:

First of all: Digunakan untuk memulai urutan waktu. Contoh: First of all, I wake up early in the morning.Then: Menunjukkan peristiwa berikutnya dalam urutan waktu. Contoh: Then, I brush my teeth and get ready for the day.After that: Digunakan untuk menggambarkan peristiwa yang terjadi setelah peristiwa sebelumnya. Contoh: After that, I have my breakfast.Meanwhile: Menunjukkan peristiwa yang berlangsung secara bersamaan dengan peristiwa lain. Contoh: Meanwhile, my family is still asleep.Later: Mengindikasikan waktu di masa depan dalam urutan waktu. Contoh: Later, I go to school or work.Finally: Digunakan untuk mengakhiri urutan waktu atau menggambarkan peristiwa terakhir dalam urutan. Contoh: Finally, in the evening, I relax and watch TV.

Berikut adalah contoh paragraf yang menggunakan time connectives dalam bahasa Inggris tentang diri sendiri:

On weekdays, my daily routine is quite consistent. First of all, I wake up early in the morning, usually around 6:30 AM. Then, I spend a few minutes stretching and doing some light exercises to wake myself up. After that, I head to the kitchen and prepare a hearty breakfast. Meanwhile, I check my emails and plan my tasks for the day. After that, I leave for work, where I spend most of my day in meetings and completing assignments. Later, in the evening, I return home, and finally, I unwind by reading a book or chatting with my family before going to bed.

Dalam paragraf di atas, time connectives seperti "first of all," "then," "after that," "meanwhile," "later," dan "finally" membantu mengatur urutan peristiwa sehari-hari saya dalam bahasa Inggris.

21. Connectives : Contrast (Unexpected Result) Although you are a kind person, I hate you. a. Use "however" to rewrite the sentence above. b. Use "despite" to rewrite the sentence above.Gunakan format grade 4/5! ​


Instruction:

Rewrite the sentence by transition however and preposition despite.

Answer:a. You're a kind person. However, I hate you.

b. Despite you're a kind person, I hate you.

Discussion:Unexpected results

Menyatakan sesuatu yang terjadi tidak diduga memperoleh hasil yang di luar harapan. Dengan sesuatu mencirikan bertentangan dari harapan dan kenyataan itu tiba-tiba mendatangkannya. Kita sebut dengan kata lain—hasil yang tidak diduga adalah sesuatu mengejutkan orang itu.

Dalam mengungkapkan unexpected results—berarti kita menghubungkan suatu kejadian/peristiwa/kegiatan yang memiliki sebab dan akibat, tidak menyesuaikan dengan kenyataan, dan tidak berharap kenyataan tidak akan terjadi.

Kita dapat mengungkapkan unexpected results ke dalam empat golongan dengan memiliki arti sama pada suatu kalimat.

Adverb of clause:

(Klausa adverbia)

even thoughalthoughthough

Contohnya:

Even though you're a kind person, Jennifer hates you.Although the day was rainy, I went to my home.Though she's a bad person, Luke loves her.

Conjunction:

(Kata hubung)

butyetanyway

Contohnya:

You're a kind person, but I really not like you very much.Yulia is a pretty woman, yet she is unpopular.I met a pure kind girl, still, nobody knows where she lives anyway.

Transition:

(Transisi)

neverthelessnonethelesshowever

Contohnya:

It was snowing outside. Nevertheless, we went to the hospital.She only thinks you're her only one man. Nonetheless, betrays behind you is what she does.The capital city will be ready relocated soon. However, some people have to move out for their job.

Preposition:

(Kata depan)

despitein spite of

Contohnya:

Despite ordering import vaccines, the government can't afford limitless dosage for societies.I hate you in spite of your kindness.

Pada pernyataan yang diberikan: you're a kind person. I hate you dapat digunakan dengan bentuk pengungkapan unexpected results:

Jangan gunakan pernyataan adverb of clause apabila hanya even.

Even you're a kind person, I hate you. ✖️

Seharusnya:

Even though you're a kind person, I hate you. ✔️You're a kind person, even though I hate you. ✔️

Setiap kata conjuction tidak digunakan di awal kalimat.

But you're a kind person, I hate you. ✖️

Seharusnya:

You're a kind person, but I hate you. ✔️You're a kind person, I hate you anyway. ✔️

Dalam menyatakan kata transition bisa digunakan adjective/much/many:

However kindness of yours got, I hate you.I hate you, however much you're kind!

Untuk kata yang berhubungan dengan preposition dapat diikuti dengan nominal atau kata berimbuhan -ing.

Despite you're a kind person, I hate you.In spite of being kind, I hate you.
