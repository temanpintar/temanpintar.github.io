Mari kita bagi dan kumpulkan ekspresi berakar yang diberikan:

3√17 ÷ 7√25

3√17 = √(17^3) = √4913

7√25 = √(25^7) = √78125

Jadi, 3√17 ÷ 7√25 = √4913 ÷ √78125 = √(4913/78125) = √(1/16) = 1/4

(1/4) ÷ 5√18

5√18 = √(18^5) = √1889568

Jadi, (1/4) ÷ 5√18 = (1/4) ÷ √1889568 = (1/4) ÷ √(2^17) = (1/4) ÷ 2^8 = (1/4) ÷ 256 = 1/1024

1/1024 + 7√38

7√38 = √(38^7) = √20880442496

Jadi, 1/1024 + 7√38 = 1/1024 + √20880442496 = 1/1024 + 144898.59375

144898.59375 + 9√87

Jadi, 144898.59375 + 9√87 = 144898.59375 + √442183776890962
