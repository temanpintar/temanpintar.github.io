1. contoh soal pilihan ganda bahasa inggris cause and effect


Cause and Effect

Anna: Hey Lulu, I recognize that your face is looking brighter and your look slimmer! What’s the secret?

Lulu: Well, thanks for noticing Ann. For the past months I have been working out regularly at least three times a week. Thanks to yoga!

Anna: Wow, sounds very interesting.

Lulu: Yes, but not only it. I also have been taking care of my food. Due to my lactose intolerant, I try to avoid dairy as much as possible. And it works for me!

Anna: I’m very inspired Ann! As a result of my laziness and eating too much junk food, I gained 5 kilograms in a month! I need to get back in shape and do more workout.

Lulu: Yes, I support you!

6. Tolong bantu buat dialog bahasa inggris cause and effect tentang kemacetan


Cause and Effect

Cause and Effect yang sering juga disebut sebagai kausalitas, adalah kalimat yang menunjukan akibat yang ditimbulkan atau hasil dari suatu tindakan lain.

Kata-kata yang menunjulkan sebuah Cause and Effect adalah:

1. Because

2. Since

3. For

4. Due to

5. Because of

6. Therefore

7. as

8. So, dan lain lain

NB:

1. Untuk penulisan because, since, dan for harus diikuti sebuah kalimat. Contoh:

The flight is delayed because/since/for the weather is bad.

2. Sedangkan untuk due to dan because of, harus diikuti frasa kata benda (noun phrase).

Contoh:

The flight is delayed due to/ because of a bad weather.

Contoh lain kalimat cause and effect:

1. I help you because I like you.

2. I study hard because I want to pass the exam.

3. We have to go home since we have no idea where to go next.

4. You need to hurry up since it’s very late now.

5. I can’t go out as it’s raining.

6. I can’t run fast as I’m too fat.

7. I love you, so I will do everything to make you happy.

8. I didn’t eat anything, so I’m very hungry now.

9. We had to cancel the picnic because of bad weather.

10. I did it because of you.

11. The flight was canceled due to the storm.

12. I came home late due to the traffic jam.

13. He didn’t work hard. As a result, he was fired.

14. I was busy this evening. Therefore, I couldn’t come to your party.

Pembahasan

Cause and Effect

Cause and Effect yang sering juga disebut sebagai kausalitas, adalah kalimat yang menunjukan akibat yang ditimbulkan atau hasil dari suatu tindakan lain.

Kata-kata yang menunjulkan sebuah Cause and Effect adalah:

1. Because

2. Since

3. For

4. Due to

5. Because of

6.  Therefore

7. as

8. So, dan lain lain

NB:

Untuk penulisan because, since, dan for harus diikuti sebuah kalimat. Contoh:

The flight is delayed because/since/for the weather is bad.

Sedangkan untuk due to dan because of, harus diikuti frasa kata benda (noun phrase).

Contoh:

The flight is delayed due to/ because of a bad weather.

Contoh lain kalimat cause and effect:

1. I help you because I like you.

2. I study hard because I want to pass the exam.

3. We have to go home since we have no idea where to go next.

4. You need to hurry up since it’s very late now.

5. I can’t go out as it’s raining.

6. I can’t run fast as I’m too fat.

7. I love you, so I will do everything to make you happy.

8. I didn’t eat anything, so I’m very hungry now.

10. I did it because of you.

Membuat dialog cause-effect tentang forest fires in Sumatra.

A: Brian, how do you think about forest fires in Sumatra?  

B: Yes, Andy, it is a big forest fire in Sumatra. It causes a lot of smokes, which is harmful to health. I think it is because of many deforestation by lot of people.

A: Deforestation? How does deforestation become the main cause of forest fire?

B: Companies and farmers often cut their land before they plant new crops. The problem is that they burn the trees after cutting them. The fire spread and burn everything there. Especially in dry season.

A: Yes, I agree with you.

B: Fire forest can happen faster in dry season. It's also caused by the high temperature in dry season.  

A: Hopefully the fire can be put down fast before it spread more. The smoke can cause lungs and throats irritation. It is very dangerous for people to breathe in such dirty atmospheres.  

B: Yes it is very dangerous. The smoke hamper and disrupt transportation too.  Flight had to be cancelled and driving cars in smoke is very risky.

29. Buat dialog dalam bahasa Inggris: 1. Cause and effect tema olahraga


Dialog dalam bahasa Inggris tentang cause and effect tema olahraga adalah sebagai berikut:


Anna: Hey Lulu, I recognize that your face is looking brighter and your look slimmer! What’s the secret?

Lulu: Well, thanks for noticing Ann. For the past months I have been working out regularly at least three times a week. Thanks to yoga!

Anna: Wow, sounds very interesting.

Lulu: Yes, but not only it. I also have been taking care of my food. Due to my lactose intolerant, I try to avoid dairy as much as possible. And it works for me!

Anna: I’m very inspired Ann! As a result of my laziness and eating too much junk food, I gained 5 kilograms in a month! I need to get back in shape and do more workout.

Lulu: Yes, I support you!


Pembahasan

Untuk mengungkapkan cause and effect dalam bahasa Inggris, kita menggunakan signal words berikut:

due tobecause ofowing tothanks toas a consequence ofas a result ofbecausesinceasfor

Nah, untuk menunjukan cause and effect dalam kalimat ada dua jenis yakni yang diikuti kata benda (followed by noun) dan yang diikuti kata kerja (followed by verb).


Followed by noun

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause


Due to Jen’s laziness, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: Due toCause: Jen’s lazinessEffect: She didn’t pass the test

He was sick as a consequence of his ignorance.

Signal phrase: As a consequenceCause: His ignoranceEffect: He was sick

Followed by verb

Signal word/phrase + Cause + Effect

Effect + Signal word/phrase + Cause


Because Jen was lazy, she didn’t pass the Math test.

Signal phrase: BecauseCause: Jen was lazyEffect: She didn’t pass the Math test

As he ignored his health, he will be sick,

Signal word: AsCause: He ignoredEffect: He will be sick

38. Tolong buatlah dialog bahasa inggris cause & effect covid 19!​


CAUSE & EFFECT DIALOGUE

Jono : Where did you go two days ago, Dew?

Dew : I went to Surabaya.

Jono : What did you do there ? A holiday trip ?

Dew : No, I didn't. I went there because of a business trip.

Jono : The polices ban the cars to across the town due to the COVID19 impact.

Dew : Yes, that's true. Fortunately, I could travel out of town because I had a negative Rapid Antigent test result.

Jono : When is the due date for the Rapid Antigent Detection result test?

Dew : The due date of Rapid Antigent Detection result test is marely 3 days. Everytime we are planning to travel out of town, we must present the Rapid Antigent Detection result test.

Jono : It's better to not travel out of town during the coronavirus outbreak cos it can prevent the coronavirus transmission. But, if it's urgent to travel out town, you must boost your immune system by taking some vitamins and pay attention for the health sanitation since it can keep you health. As the result of the great death rate, our government warns the society to apply the health protocol for COVID19.

Dew : I see. Thank you for reminding.

EXPLANATION

Cause and effect, kalimat yang terhubung adanya keterangan sebab akibat dengan bantuan frasa, seperti :

Due to, because of, because, owing to, therefore, since, as, for, as the result.

Dalam penulisan frasa cause and effect diatas terdapat perbedaan. Frasa-frasa diatas pada umumnya tertulis dengan diikuti oleh kata kerja ataupun kata benda.

Berikut ini signal phrase yang harus diikuti dengan noun/noun phrase adalah

because of, owing to, due to, as a result of.

Sedangkan, signal phrase yang harus diikuti dengan clause/verb phrase adalah because, since, as, for
