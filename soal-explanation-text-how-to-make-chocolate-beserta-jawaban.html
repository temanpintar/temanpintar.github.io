11. How to make a chocolate cake


Chocolate Cake Recipe


If you want to make a homemade chocolate cake from scratch, this is a simple, yet easy recipe to make. It's one you are certain to enjoy.

Chocolate Cake Ingredients:

3/4 cups butter or margarine, softened

3 eggs

2 cups sugar

2 cups flour

3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

1 teaspoon baking soda

3/4 teaspoon baking powder

2 teaspoon vanilla

1 1/2 cup milk

How to make Chocolate Cake:

Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.

Grease and flour three 6" X 1 1/2" round cake pans.

Mix together flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and baking soda. Set aside.

In a large bowl, beat butter, eggs and vanilla.

Gradually add sugar.

Beat on medium to high speed for about 3-4 minutes until well mixed

Alternately combine in flour mixture and milk to batter while beating.

Continue to beat until batter is smooth.

Pour equal amounts of batter into greased and floured round cake pans.

Bake 30 to 35 minutes.

Check with a toothpick to se if it is done. Bake a few minutes more, if needed.

Remove from oven and allow cakes to cool in pans for a few minutes.

Place cakes on a wire rack, to them allow to completely cool.

17. how to make chocolate dipped strawberries


Jawaban:

Making chocolate-dipped strawberries is a simple and delicious treat. Here's a step-by-step guide to do it:

Ingredients:

- Fresh strawberries

- Chocolate chips (semi-sweet or dark chocolate work well)

- Optional: White chocolate chips or toppings for decoration (e.g., chopped nuts, sprinkles)

Instructions:

1. Wash the strawberries: Rinse the strawberries under cold running water and gently pat them dry with paper towels. Make sure they are completely dry before dipping them in chocolate.

2. Melt the chocolate: Place the chocolate chips in a microwave-safe bowl or use a double boiler on the stovetop. If using a microwave, heat the chocolate in short intervals, stirring between each, until it's fully melted and smooth.

3. Dip the strawberries: Hold each strawberry by the stem and dip it into the melted chocolate. Swirl it around to coat about two-thirds of the berry, leaving the top third uncovered.

4. Allow excess chocolate to drip off: Lift the dipped strawberry and allow any excess chocolate to drip back into the bowl.

5. Optional: Decorate the strawberries: If you want to add decorations like chopped nuts or sprinkles, do it while the chocolate is still wet.

6. Place on a baking sheet: Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or wax paper. Carefully place the dipped strawberries on the lined sheet, making sure they are not touching each other.

7. Cool and set: Let the chocolate-covered strawberries cool at room temperature for about 30 minutes, or you can place the baking sheet in the refrigerator for quicker setting.

8. Optional: Drizzle with white chocolate: If you want to add a decorative touch, you can drizzle melted white chocolate over the cooled strawberries.

9. Serve or store: Once the chocolate is fully set, your chocolate-dipped strawberries are ready to enjoy! If you have any leftovers, store them in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

Chocolate-dipped strawberries make for a delightful treat and can be a great addition to any celebration or gathering. Enjoy!

BANANA CHOCO

Peel, then cut each banana in half crosswise and insert a craft stick into each half. Place on a tray, cover with plastic wrap and place in the freezer until frozen, about 3 hours.

Place the peanuts in a shallow dish or on a plate. Melt the chocolate in the top of a double boiler over slightly simmering water, over the lowest possible heat, stirring frequently. Make sure the water is not touching the bottom of the top pan. Pour the melted chocolate into a tall glass. Dip each frozen banana into the chocolate, turning it to coat, and immediately roll in the peanuts. Place on a tray covered in waxed paper. Serve immediately or wrap individually in plastic wrap or waxed paper and freeze for up to 2 weeks.
