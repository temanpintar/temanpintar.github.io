Jawaban:

1. positif: Me and Anika ate fried rice yesterday

negatif: Me and Anika didn't eat fried rice yesterday

interogatif: did me and Anika eat fried rice yesterday?

2. positif: The teacher and the teacher (married) drank coffee at the cafe last week

negatif: The teacher and the teacher (didn't married) drank coffee at the cafe last week

interogatif: Did the teacher and the teacher (married) drink coffee at the cafe last week?

3.positif: 6th graders read storybooks last Friday at library

negatif: Class 6E students didn't read story books last Friday at library

interogatif: Did the 6E grade students read the storybook last Friday at library?

4. positif: The security guard heard music last Saturday in the language laboratory

negatif: The security guard didn't hear the music last Saturday in the language laboratory

introgatif: Did the security guard listen to music last saturday at the language laboratory?

5. positif: Teacher (married) taught English 2 weeks ago in class 6E

negatif: Teacher (unmarried) taught English 2 weeks ago in class 6E

introgatif: Did the teacher (unmarried) teach English 2 weeks ago in class 6E?

ARTINYA:

Penjelasan:

1. positif: Saya dan Anika makan nasi goreng kemarin negatif: Saya dan Anika tidak makan nasi goreng kemarin

interogatif: apakah saya dan Anika makan nasi goreng kemarin?

2. positif: Guru dan guru (menikah) minum kopi di kafe minggu lalu

negatif: Guru dan guru (belum menikah) minum kopi di kafe minggu lalu

interogatif: Apakah guru dan guru (menikah) minum kopi di kafe minggu lalu?

negatif: Siswa Kelas 6E tidak membaca buku cerita Jumat lalu di perpustakaan

negatif: Satpam tidak mendengar musik Sabtu lalu di laboratorium bahasa

interogatif: Apakah satpam mendengarkan musik Sabtu lalu di laboratorium bahasa?

negatif: Guru (belum menikah) mengajar bahasa Inggris 2 minggu yang lalu di kelas 6E

interogatif: Apakah guru (belum menikah) mengajar bahasa Inggris 2 minggu yang lalu di kelas 6E?

Jawaban:

Simple Past Tense:

1. (+) Anika and I ate fried rice yesterday.

(-) Anika and I did not eat fried rice yesterday.

(?) Did Anika and I eat fried rice yesterday?

2. (+) Our married teachers drank some coffee in a café last week.

(-) Our married teachers did not drink some coffee in a café last week.

(?) Did Our married teachers drink some coffee in a café last week?

3. (+) The students from 6E read story books in the library last Friday.

(-) The students from 6E did not read story books in the library last Friday.

(?) Did the students from 6E read story books in the library last Friday?

4. (+) Mr. Security Guard listened to music in a language laboratory last Saturday.

(-) Mr. Security Guard did not listen to music in a language laboratory last Saturday.

(?) Did Mr. Security Guard listen to music in a language laboratory last Saturday?

5. (+) A single female teacher taught English language in class 6E two weeks ago.

(-) A single female teacher did not teach English language in class 6E two weeks ago.

(?) Did a single female teacher teach English language in class 6E two weeks ago?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Simple Past Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan di masa lampau dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Terjemahan:

1. (+) Anika dan aku makan nasi goreng kemarin.

(-) Anika dan aku tidak makan nasi goreng kemarin.

(?) Apakah Anika dan aku makan nasi goreng kemarin?

2. (+) Guru kami yang sudah menikah minum kopi di kafe minggu lalu.

(-) Guru kami yang sudah menikah tidak minum kopi di kafe minggu lalu.

(?) Apakah guru kami yang sudah menikah minum kopi di kafe minggu lalu?

3. (+) Siswa dari 6E membaca buku cerita di perpustakaan Jumat lalu.

(-) Siswa dari 6E tidak membaca buku cerita di perpustakaan Jumat lalu.

(?) Apakah siswa dari 6E membaca buku cerita di perpustakaan Jumat lalu?

4. (+) Pak Satpam mendengarkan musik di laboratorium bahasa Sabtu lalu.

(-) Pak Satpam tidak mendengarkan musik di laboratorium bahasa Sabtu lalu.

(?) Apakah Pak Satpam mendengarkan musik di laboratorium bahasa Sabtu lalu?

5. (+) Seorang guru perempuan lajang (belum menikah) mengajar bahasa Inggris di kelas 6E dua minggu lalu.

(-) Seorang guru perempuan lajang (belum menikah) tidak mengajar bahasa Inggris di kelas 6E dua minggu lalu.

(?) Apakah seorang guru perempuan lajang (belum menikah) mengajar bahasa Inggris di kelas 6E dua minggu lalu?

Simple Past Tense:

Kalimat Verbal Positif:

(+) Subject + verb 2 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(+) We attended the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Negatif:

(-) Subject + did not + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(-) We did not attend the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Interogatif:

(?) Did + subject + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)?

(?) Did we attend the festival last week?

6. Tugas hari ini adalah mentranslate kan soal soal di bawah ini menggunakan present continuous tense (Subject + To be + Verb ing + Object)1. Mereka sedang belajar Bahasa Inggris di perpustakaan2. Dia sedang makan siang di kantin3. Mereka sedang rapat di ruang guru4. Dia sedang membeli sesuatu di toko buku5. Dia sedang memasak nasi di dapur​


Jawaban:

1. They are studying English in the library

2. He is having lunch in the cafeteria

3. They are meeting in the teacher's room

4. He's buying something at the bookstore

5. She is cooking rice in the kitchen

Penjelasan:

Dalam pengertiannya, present continuous tense atau yang sering kali disebut present progressive tense ini adalah tenses bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk menunjukkan sebuah tindakan atau kejadian yang sedang terjadi pada saat pembicaraan sedang berlangsung.

Selain itu, present continuous tense ini pun juga bisa digunakan untuk membuat suatu rencana atau janji di masa depan.

Rumus tenses bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk membentuk kalimat present continuous tense/present progressive tense ini adalah:

Subject + Am / Is / Are + Verb –ing (Continuous Form)

Kata Sinyal

at the moment, just now, now, right now, Listen!, Look!

Kalimat Positif

Look! He is talking with his classmates.

The teachers are having a meeting at the moment.

Kalimat Negatif

He is not playing computer games now.

Listen! He is not laughing anymore.

Kalimat Tanya

Is he playing computer games at the moment?

Dijawab dengan “Yes, he is.” Atau “No, he is not.”

Ingredients:

1. ½ cup of sugar

4. Cool.

5. To get the best result, put it in the refrigerator for 3 hours.

6. Yield: 6 servings

7. Preparation time: 5 minutes

“How to Make Easy French Toast”

To make French Toast, you need:

1. 1 spoon of sugar

2. 2 eggs

3. 4 pcs of bread

4. ¼ cup of milk

5. Butter

6. Pan

7. Fork

8. Plate

9. Bowl

Steps:

1. Break the eggs, then pour the milk in the bowl.

2. Add a spoon of sugar.

5. Put butter on the pan and heat.

6. After the pan is hot, take out the bread from the bowl and put it in the pan.

7. Flip the bread after you cook one side, to the other side.

8. After finishing the bread, cook the other 3 pieces of bread with the same steps.

9. Now, serve the bread on the plate.
