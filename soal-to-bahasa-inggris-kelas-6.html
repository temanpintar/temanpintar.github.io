Tenses yang digunakan pada kalimat-kalimat tersebut adalah simple past tense.

Simple past tense adalah tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang terjadi di masa lalu dan telah berakhir di masa lalu.

Rumus Simple Present Tense:

1. Nominal Sentence

⇒ Positive

S + Be (was/were) + O

⇒ Negative

S + Be (was/were) + not + O

⇒ Interrogative

Be (was/were) + S + O + ?

2. Verbal Sentence

⇒ Positive

S + V2 + O

⇒ Negative

S + did not + V1 + O

⇒ Interrogative

Did + S + V1 + ?

Catatan:

Jika subjeknya adalah tunggal (I/he/she/it) maka to be yang digunakan adalah was.Jika subjeknya adalah jamak (you/they/we) maka to be yang digunakan adalah were.

The use of "doesn't" instead of "don't" depends on whether the subject is singular or plural.

2. "Don't" is used with plural subjects:

- They don't like chocolate.

The choice between "doesn't" and "don't" is determined by the number (singular or plural) of the subject. In the case of a name like "Alice," it's a singular subject, so you use "doesn't." If you're talking about multiple people or things, you would use "don't" with a plural subject.
