Pembahasan:

Literal meaning dalam bahasa Inggris berarti arti sebenarnya/ makna sederhana sebuah kata.
Figurative meaning dalam bahasa Inggris berarti arti perumpamaan/ metafora sebuah kata.

Example:
The word 'fall'
Literally means jatuh. e.g: He fell off the bike yesterday.
Figuratively means keruntuhan, musnah, hilang e.g: The Roman Empire fell as the Islam rose and ruled the world.

19. contoh kalimat yg figurative meaning sma literal meaning


Literal language means exactly what it says, while figurative language uses similes, metaphors, hyperbole, and personification to describe something often through comparison with something different.

Contoh kalimat yang "Literal Meaning"
Grass looks green.Sand feels rough.The flower smells sweet.Grasshoppers make a high pitched noise.

Contoh kalimat yang "Figurative Meaning"
The grass looks like spiky green hair (simile)Sand is solid water (metaphor)The flower has the sweetest smelling petals in the world. (hyperbole)Grasshoppers are fiddlers who play their legs (personification)

V1:

The first form of the verb is called Verb 1 (V1) or present. This verb is the original form of the verb (infinitive) which is usually used in simple present tense sentences.

V2:

The second form of the verb is referred to as verb 2 or simple past. This form of the verb is used in sentences that describe an event in the past.

V3:

The third form of the verb is called the past participle. In simple terms, verb 3 is used to make a sentence that shows an event has been completed.

38. Berikan 3 contoh kata Lexical Meaning dan Grammatical Meaning ​


Jawaban:

Lexical meaning adalah makna kata itu sendiri dalam kamus. Sedangkan grammatical meaning adalah makna yang terkait dengan fungsi kata dalam kalimat.

Contoh Lexical Meaning:

1. Book (buku) - Merujuk pada benda tertentu yang berisi tulisan atau gambar.

2. Happy (bahagia) - Menggambarkan perasaan positif atau keadaan emosional tertentu.

3. Run (berlari) - Menunjukkan gerakan tubuh yang cepat dengan menggunakan kaki sebagai alat.

Contoh Grammatical Meaning:

1. The (artikel) - Digunakan sebelum kata benda untuk menunjukkan bahwa benda itu sudah dibahas sebelumnya atau sudah dikenal dalam konteks pembicaraan.

2. -ed (sufiks) - Digunakan untuk membentuk kata kerja bentuk lampau (past tense).

3. -ing (sufiks) - Digunakan untuk membentuk kata kerja bentuk sekarang (present participle) atau kata benda (gerund).
