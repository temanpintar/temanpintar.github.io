13. bantu soal bahasa Inggris gw broContoh soalHow ... every morning? (you feel)Jawab: How do you feel every morning ​


Jawaban:

Simple Present Tense:

How do you feel every morning?Does she go to the market? Said always cleans his cat cutely.You watch movies every Saturday night.I sit in this office every afternoon.Does he give them money every month?My Mother cooks rice every day.Her Mom sells some fish in the market every day.They always stand in the place every month.Mr.Budi makes some bread every day.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Simple Present Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan berulang sehari-hari dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kalimat-kalimatnya:

Bagaimana perasaanmu setiap pagi? Apakah dia pergi ke pasar? Said selalu membersihkan kucingnya dengan manis. Kamu menonton film setiap Sabtu malam. Aku duduk di kantor ini setiap sore. Apakah dia memberi mereka uang setiap bulan? Ibuku memasak nasi setiap hari. Ibunya menjual ikan di pasar setiap hari. Mereka selalu berdiri di tempat setiap bulan. Pak Budi membuat roti setiap hari.

Simple Present Tense:

Kalimat Positive (+)  :

Verbal     :  Subject + Verb1/Verb1+s/es + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning)

Kalimat Negatif (-)    :

Verbal     :  Subject + do/does not +Verb1 + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning)

Kalimat Interrogative (?)  :

Verbal    :  Do/Does + Subject + Verb1 + Object + adverb of time (every day / every morning) + ?

5W1H (How) + Do/Does + Subject + Verb-1 + Object + adverb of time (every morning) + ?
