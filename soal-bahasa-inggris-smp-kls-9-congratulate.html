16. Buku mozaik, Bahasa Inggris smp kelas 3Lanjutan soal:3. What expressions are used to compliment someone?4. How do they respond the expressions of compliment?5. When should we congratulate others?6. When should we compliment others?7. So, what is the purpose of expressing congratulation and compliment? Mohon bantuan nya ​


Jawaban:

3. Expressions used to compliment someone may include:

- "You did an excellent job!"

- "You look great today!"

- "I'm impressed by your skills!"

- "You're so talented!"

- "You have a wonderful sense of style!"

- "You have a beautiful smile!"

- "You're such a kind-hearted person!"

4. Responses to expressions of compliment can vary, but some common ways to respond include:

- "Thank you so much!"

- "That's so kind of you to say!"

- "I really appreciate your compliment!"

- "You made my day!"

- "I'm glad you think so!"

- "It means a lot to me!"

5. We should congratulate others on special achievements, milestones, or happy occasions. Some examples include:

- Graduation

- New job or promotion

- Getting married

- Having a baby

- Winning a competition or award

- Buying a new house

- Celebrating a birthday or anniversary

6. We should compliment others when we genuinely appreciate or admire something about them. Compliments can be given for various reasons, such as:

- Someone's appearance or style

- Their skills or abilities

- Their kindness or generosity

- Their hard work or achievements

- Their creativity or talent

- Their positive attitude or perseverance

7. The purpose of expressing congratulations and compliments is to show appreciation, admiration, and support to others. It helps to build positive relationships, boost morale, and make others feel valued and appreciated. By expressing congratulations and compliments, we uplift and empower others, fostering a positive and encouraging environment.
