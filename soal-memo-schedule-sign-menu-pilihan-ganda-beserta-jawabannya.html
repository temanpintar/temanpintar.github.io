11. What is the meaning of schedule and sign​


Jawaban:

Jadwal atau tabel waktu sebagai alat dasar manajemen waktu, terdiri dari daftar waktu yang mana tugas, kejadian atau tindakan dimaksudkan untuk berlangsung atau dari rentetan peristiwa dalam urutan kronologi ketika hal-hal tersebut dimaksudkan untuk dilakukan.

terjemahkan

Schedule or time table as a basic tool of time management, consists of a list of times for which tasks, events or actions are intended to take place or from a series of events in chronological order when they are intended to be performed.

Tanda dalam linguistik didefinisikan sebagai guratan yang tampak pada permukaan, bersifat konvensional dan dipakai sebagai satuan grafis dasar dalam sistem aksara. Tanda digunakan untuk menggambarkan atau merekam gagasan, kata, suku kata, fonem, atau bunyi.

terjemahkan

Signs in linguistics are defined as strokes that appear on the surface, are conventional in nature and are used as basic graphical units in the script system. Signs are used to describe or record ideas, words, syllables, phonemes, or sounds.

**Dialog about Memo, Menu, Schedule, and Sign for 3 People**

*Characters:*

- **Diana** (Restaurant Manager)

- **Adi** (Chef)

- **Rika** (Waitress)

---

**Scene: In the Restaurant Manager's Office**

**Diana:** (Reviewing a memo) Good morning, everyone. There are a few things we need to discuss. First, I've prepared a memo about changes to the restaurant's operating hours. (Shows the memo)

**Adi:** (Nods) Okay, Mba. What are the changes?

**Diana:** (Points to the memo) Our opening hours will be moved up by one hour starting next week. Make sure all employees are aware of this.

**Rika:** (Hands over a schedule) Mba Diana, here's the waitstaff schedule for next week. It's adjusted according to the new operating hours.

**Diana:** (Takes the schedule) Great, Rika. Ensure all waitstaff understand this change. Next, Adi, we need to add some new dishes to our menu.

**Adi:** (Excited) What are they, Mba Diana?

**Diana:** (Points to the menu) I want to add a new pasta dish and a seafood dish. Can you create the recipes and display them on the menu?

**Adi:** Certainly, Mba. I'll get the ingredients sorted right away.

**Rika:** (Points to the sign) Mba Diana, our menu board needs a refresh. Some parts have faded.

**Diana:** (Jots down in the memo) Duly noted. The rest, let's discuss this week's specials and promotional items for social media. Meeting adjourned.

**Dialog about Memo, Menu, Schedule, and Sign in a Café for 3 People**

- **Café Manager (CM)**

- **Chef (Ch)**

- **Waitstaff (W)**

---

**Scene: Café Manager's Office**

**CM:** Good morning, team. We've got a few things to cover today. Firstly, I've drafted a memo regarding the upcoming promotional events. (Shows the memo)

**Ch:** (Nods) Sure, what's the plan?

**CM:** We're introducing a new coffee blend next week, so the memo outlines the details. Make sure all staff members are informed.

**W:** (Hands over a schedule) Here's the schedule for the upcoming week, including shifts for the new promotion.

**CM:** (Takes the schedule) Thanks. Ensure everyone gets a copy. Chef, we've received some feedback about diversifying our menu.

**Ch:** (Enthusiastic) Great! What are we thinking?

**CM:** (Points to the menu) I'd like to add a couple of vegan options and perhaps a signature dessert. Can you come up with some ideas for those?

**Ch:** Absolutely, I'll start experimenting with recipes right away.

**W:** (Points to a sign) Manager, the outdoor sign near the entrance needs to be updated. It's looking a bit worn out.

**CM:** (Takes note on the memo) Good catch. We'll get that sorted this week. Anything else to discuss?

**Ch:** (Shakes head) I'm good.

**W:** (Nods) Same here.

**CM:** Alright then, let's keep up the great work. If there are any more ideas or concerns, feel free to bring them up. Meeting adjourned.
