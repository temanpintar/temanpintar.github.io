Si Kotak Ajaib

John Logie Baird lahir di Skotlandia, 13 Agustus 1888. Ia adalah penemu yang pada kesempatan itu untuk pertama kalinya menunjukkan bahwa gambar hidup dapat ditransmisikan melalui pesawat yang sekarang dikenal sebagai televisi. Baird mendemonstrasikan televisi di depan para bapak-bapak dan ibu-ibu, di Royal Institute-London untuk pertama kalinya pada tahun 1925. Seiring dengan perkembangan teknologi, lalu televisi kemudian terus berkembang hingga menjadi semakin canggih seperti saat ini.

Prinsip kerja televisi secara umum adalah mengubah sinyal elektonik dan magnetik menjadi gambar dan suara. Sinyal tersebut berupa gelombang elektromagnetik, yaitu gelombang yang terdiri atas medan magnet dan medan listrik. Gelombang elektromagnet dapat merambat, dipantulkan, dan dibiaskan tanpa media perantara. Gambar yang kita lihat di layar televisi adalah hasil produksi dari sebuah kamera. Pada awalnya, objek gambar yang ditangkap kamera akan diubah menjadi sinyal gelombang elektromagnetik. Sinyal tersebut akan ditransmisikan oleh pemancar ke pesawat penerima pada televisi. Pesawat televisi akan mengubah sinyal yang diterima menjadi objek gambar yang utuh sesuai dengan objek yang ditranmisikan, dalam bentuk hitam putih atau berwarna.

Jadi, televisi adalah sebuah media telekomunikasi yang berfungsi sebagai penerima siaran gambar bergerak beserta suara. Kata “televisi” merupakan gabungan dari bahasa Yunani “tele” artinya jauh dan bahasa Latin “visio” artinya penglihatan. Sehingga televisi dapat diartikan sebagai “alat komunikasi jarak jauh yang menggunakan media visual/penglihatan”. Penemuan televisi juga dikategorikan sebagai penemuan besar yang mampu mengubah peradaban dunia.

5. Identifying the Finite and Non-finite Clauses Identify the finite and non-finite clauses which function as circumstance! 1. I went to the house where my mom grew up. 2. She came to the party when everyone had left.3. Hit by truck, the tree fell on the ground. 4. Being born in London, he became the citizen of U.K. 5. To perform traditional dance, all the children were ready. 6. I'm going to broadway where many popular artists play. 7. Found the boy, the rescuers injured. 8. When the daughter spoke loudly, her cousin came. 9. Speaking any languages, my cousin became polyglot. 10. To do homework, Rita went to the library at night.​


Jawaban:

Sure! Here are the finite and non-finite clauses that function as circumstances for the given sentences:

1. I went to the house where my mom grew up.

- Finite clause: I went to the house

- Non-finite clause as circumstance: where my mom grew up

2. She came to the party when everyone had left.

- Finite clause: She came to the party

- Non-finite clause as circumstance: when everyone had left

3. Hit by a truck, the tree fell on the ground.

- Non-finite clause as circumstance: Hit by a truck

4. Being born in London, he became a citizen of the U.K.

- Non-finite clause as circumstance: Being born in London

5. To perform traditional dance, all the children were ready.

- Non-finite clause as circumstance: To perform traditional dance

6. I'm going to Broadway where many popular artists play.

- Finite clause: I'm going to Broadway

- Non-finite clause as circumstance: where many popular artists play

7. Found by the boy, the rescuers injured.

- Non-finite clause as circumstance: Found by the boy

8. When the daughter spoke loudly, her cousin came.

- Finite clause: When the daughter spoke loudly

- Non-finite clause as circumstance: None

9. Speaking multiple languages, my cousin became a polyglot.

- Non-finite clause as circumstance: Speaking multiple languages

10. To do homework, Rita went to the library at night.

- Non-finite clause as circumstance: To do homework

Please note that in sentence 7, "Found by the boy, the rescuers injured," the sentence structure is a bit unclear and could be improved for better clarity.
