Example of Narrative Text in English with Moral Message:

Title: The Ant and the Grasshopper

Once upon a time, there was an ant who lived in a colony near a field. One day, he met a grasshopper who was singing and playing all day long. The ant asked the grasshopper why he wasn't storing food for the winter. The grasshopper replied that he was enjoying life and would think about the future when it comes.

As the summer passed, the weather started to change and the days became colder. The ant had stored a lot of food in his colony, but the grasshopper had nothing. He realized that he had been foolish and went to the ant's colony to ask for help. The ant kindly shared his food with the grasshopper, but told him to be more responsible in the future.

The moral message of this story is that it is important to plan ahead and work hard to prepare for the future. We should not waste our time and resources on things that do not have any long-term benefits.

Keywords: ant, grasshopper, planning, hard work, responsibility, moral message.

Jawaban:

Teks Prosedur adalah teks tulisa atau lisan yang berisi instruksi kepada pembaca atau pendengar untuk membuat atau melakukan sesuatu. Teks ini didisain untuk menggambarkan bagaimana sesuatu dicapai melalui serangkaian tindakan-tindakan atau langkah-langkah.

Oleh karena itu, teks ini bisa berbentuk rangkaian instruksi, petunjuk atau sebuah manual.

a. Fungsi Sosial

untuk menginformasikan kepada para pembaca atau pendengar bagaimana cara membuat atau melakukan sesuatu.

b. Struktur Teks

1. Goal (tujuan):

memberikan informasi berdasarkan tujuan dan maksud teks.

2. Materials:

Daftar bahan-bahan yang diperlukan untuk mencapai tujuan.

3. Steps:

Serangkaian urutan instruksi atau kegiatan untuk mencapai tujuan melalui urutan langkah-langkah yang benar.

c. Unsur kebahasaan

Terdapat beberapa ciri kebahasaan dalam Procedure Text, diantaranya yaitu:

1. Menggunakan pola kalimat imperative (Perintah), misalnya, Cut, Don’t mix, dsb.

2. Menggunakan action verbs, misalnya turn, put, don’t, mix, dsb.

3. Menggunakan connectives (kata penghubung) untuk mengurutkan kegiatan, misalnya then, while, dsb.

4. Menggunakan adverbials (kata keterangan) untuk menyatakan rinci waktu, tempat, cara yang akurat, misalnya for five minutes, 2 hours, dsb.

5. Menggunakan simple present tense

Jawaban:

1. Narrative text is a type of text that consists of imaginary stories, engineered true stories, or fairy tales. The narrative text tells a story that has a chronological collection that is interconnected. The purpose of the narrative text is to please the reader.

2. Narrative text consists of three stuctures as explain below:

Orientation: This part is introducing the story through introducing the characters, the place and time where the story began and so on.

Complication: This part is about the problem appear in the story when the main character begins to face it.

Resolution: This part is telling the way the main character overcome the problem and also the part in which the story is ended.

3. Linguistic Feature :

Nouns tertentu sebagai kata ganti orang, dalam cerita, misalnya : stepsister, housework,Adjectives yang membentuk noun phrase , misalnya : long back hair, two red apples,Time connectives dan conjunctions untuk mengurutkan kejadian, misalnya : then, before that, dsb.Adverb dan adverbial phrase untuk menunjukkan lokasi kejadian atau peristiwa misalnya : here, in the mountain, happily ever after, dsb.Saying verbs yang menandai ucapan, seperti : said, told, dan thinking verbs yang menandai pikiran, perasaan tokoh misalnya : thought, felt, dsb.Action verbs dalam past tense, misalnya : stayed, dsb.

4. Kinf of narative text :

FAIRY TALE (DONGENG) : Cerita yang sudah umum di ketahui oleh orang-orang di seluruh dunia, contoh : Cinderella, Snow White, dll.LEGEND ( LEGENDA ) : Cerita tentang asal usul terjadinya suatu daerah, benda, binatang, gedung, missal : Tangkupan Perahu, Roro Jonggrang, Lake Toba.MYTHE ( MITOS ) : Cerita yang sudah menjadi kepercayaan rakyat dan mempengaruhi kehidupan masyarakat di sekitarnya. Contoh : Nyai Roro Kidul.FABLE ( FABEL ) : Cerita binatang yang bertingkah laku seperti manusia. Contoh : Mouse deer.FOLKLORE ( CERITA RAKYAT ) : Cerita yang ada di masyarakat yang biasanya disebarluaskan dari mulut ke mulut. Contoh : Angkling Dharma, Wali Songo, dll.

12. buatlah teks naratif beserta generic strukturnya dalam bahasa inggris​


Penjelasan:

Narrative text adalah salah satu jenis teks yang bertujuan untuk menghibur pembaca dengan menyampaikan cerita atau peristiwa. Berikut adalah contoh teks naratif beserta generic structure-nya dalam bahasa Inggris:

Title: The Lost Wallet

Orientation:

Last weekend, I went shopping at the mall with my friends. It was a sunny day and we were having a great time browsing through the stores. However, when I got home, I realized that I had lost my wallet somewhere in the mall.

Complication:

I panicked and tried to remember where I had last used my wallet. I remembered buying a smoothie at the food court and paying for it with my debit card. I decided to call the mall's customer service and asked if anyone had found a lost wallet. Unfortunately, they said that no one had turned in a lost wallet.

Resolution:

Feeling upset and frustrated, I decided to retrace my steps at the mall. I went back to the food court and asked the staff if they had seen my wallet. One of the staff members said that a customer had turned in a lost wallet to the mall's lost and found office. I felt a sense of relief and rushed to the lost and found office. Thankfully, my wallet was there, with all of my cash and cards still inside.

Re-orientation:

I was grateful that someone had turned in my wallet to the lost and found office. I made a mental note to be more careful with my belongings in the future.

Generic Structure:

Orientation: Introduces the setting and characters of the story.

Complication: Presents a problem or challenge that the character faces.

Resolution: Describes how the character overcomes the problem or challenge.

Re-orientation: Concludes the story and provides a reflection or lesson learned


13. contoh teks naratif bahasa Inggris dengan artinya,beserta pesan moral ​


Once upon a time, there was a small ant named Anto. Anto lived in a colony with many other ants. One day, Anto saw a piece of bread lying on the ground and decided to bring it back to the colony to share with the other ants.

On his way back, Anto met a grasshopper who was singing and playing his violin. The grasshopper asked Anto for some food, but Anto refused, telling the grasshopper about the winter that was coming and how the ants were storing food for the winter months.

However, the grasshopper laughed and told Anto that he didn't need to worry about the future and that he should just enjoy life and not work so hard.

When winter came, the grasshopper found himself in a difficult situation. He was cold and hungry, and all the food was gone. Anto and the other ants took pity on the grasshopper and shared their food with him, saving him from starvation.

The moral of the story is: "It is best to be prepared for the future and work hard, rather than live for the moment and be caught without resources when the tough times come."

(Artinya: Dulu ada seekor semut kecil bernama Anto. Anto tinggal di sebuah koloni bersama semut lainnya. Suatu hari, Anto melihat sepotong roti tergeletak di tanah dan memutuskan untuk membawanya kembali ke koloni untuk dibagikan dengan semut lainnya.

Di jalan pulang, Anto bertemu dengan seorang belalang yang sedang menyanyi dan memainkan violinya. Belalang itu meminta Anto untuk memberinya makanan, tapi Anto menolak, menceritakan tentang musim dingin yang akan datang dan bagaimana semut sedang menyimpan makanan untuk bulan-bulan musim dingin.

Namun, belalang tertawa dan memberi tahu Anto bahwa dia tidak perlu khawatir tentang masa depan dan bahwa dia harus hanya menikmati hidup dan tidak bekerja terlalu keras.

Ketika musim dingin datang, belalang menemukan dirinya dalam situasi sulit. Dia kedinginan dan lapar, dan semua makanan sudah habis. Anto dan semut lainnya merasa kasihan pada belalang dan membagikan makanannya dengannya, menyelamatkannya dari kelaparan.

Pesan moral dari cerita ini adalah: "Lebih baik bersiap untuk masa depan dan bekerja keras, daripada hidup untuk saat ini dan terjebak tanpa sumber daya saat saat-saat sulit datang.")

Once Sura and Baya were looking for some food. (segera sesudah sura dan baya melihat beberapa makanan) Suddenly, Baya saw a goat.(Tiba-tiba, baya melihat seekor kambing).

"Yummy, this is my lunch," said Baya.
("Enakk, ini adalah makanan siangku," kata Baya).

"No way! This is my lunch. ("Tidak akan! Ini adalah makanan siangku.) You are greedy" said Sura. (kamu rakus", kata Sura). Then they fought for the goat. (Lalu, mereka berkelahi untuk mendapatkan kambing itu.) After several hours, they were very tired. (Setelah beberapa jam, mereka lelah).

Feeling tired of fighting, they lived in the different places. (merasa lelah untuk berkelahi, mereka tinggal di temoat yg berbeda). (Perbatasannya adalah pantai, sehingga mereka tidak akan pernah berkelahi lagi.)

Text “Lutung Kasarung”

A long time ago in west Java there was a kingdom. The king was Prabu Tapak Agung. He had two beautiful daughters and no son. Purba Rarang was his first daughter, and Purba Sari was his second daughter. When the king was about to pass away he gave the throne to his second daughter, Purba Sari. Purba Rarang was very disappointed. She thought that she deserve to replace her father as the ruler. She discussed the situation with her fiancé, Indrajaya.

Then she got an evil idea. She asked a witch to cast a spell to Purba Sari. Soon after that Purba Sari had a strange skin disease. There were black dots on her skin. She also had skin rash. Consequently Purba Rarang had a reason to tell people that her sister had a great sin and she was cursed by god. She told her people that such a person did not qualify to be a leader.

After that she ordered the army to send Purba Sari to a wood and had her exiled there. The army then built a wooden house for Purba Sari in a wood. So Purba Sari lived in the wood. As there were many animals in the wood she got along with them well.

Her best friend was a black monkey. She called the monkey Lutung Kasarung. Lutung was very attentive and very kind to her. He gave fruits and vegetables to Purba Sari.

Lutung Kasarung was not an ordinary monkey. He often meditates like human being. One night when there was a full moon he sat meditating. He was praying to God. Suddenly a spring emerged beside Lutung. It became bigger and bigger and finally it became a lake. The water was very clear and aromatic.

The next day Lutung came to see Purba Sari. He asked her to follow him. Lutung took her to the lake and asked her to take a bath. When Purba Sari took bath in the lake something strange happened. Her skin disease was gone and her smooth fair skin was back. Purba Sari was very happy and thankful to God.

Meanwhile Purba Rarang who lived in the palace wanted to see her sister. So she went to the wood with her soldiers. She was very surprised when she saw Purba Sari was in good condition and looked beautiful. Her evil heart led her to find a way to beat her sister. Then she asked her sister to measure the length of their hair. The one who had the longest hair would win. Purba Sari’s hair proved to be longer than Purba Rarang’s.

Purba Rarang was very jealous to her sister. She thought hard to find a way to beat Purba Sari. Then she got another idea. She asked her sister to compare their fiancé. Purba Rarang was sure that she would win because Indra Jaya was very handsome. She was sure that Purba Sari did not have any fiancé. When Purba Rarang showed Indra Jaya, Purba Sari was confused. So she just appointed Lutung Kasarung as her fiancé.

Purba Rarang laughed out loud.

‘So your fiancé is a monkey?’

Lutung Kasarung then sat on the ground. He was meditating and praying to God. Then amazingly he changed into a very handsome man. Initially Lutung Kasarung was a handsome man who was punished by God and became a monkey. After some years that day he got clemency from God and he became human being again.

Purba Rarang was very surprised. He had no choice but to accept that her sister was better than her. She asked for apology. Purba Sari gave her apology. After that they went back to palace. Purba Sari became the queen and married to Lutung Kasarung.
