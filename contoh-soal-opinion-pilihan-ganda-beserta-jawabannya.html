10. tuliskan 10 contoh soal PG tentang stating opinion and thoughtbeserta isinya


Untuk membuat 10 soal opinions, kita harus memahami bagaimana membuat kalimat expressing opinions yang benar. Ada beberapa kalimat yang dapat digunakan untuk menyampaikan opini. Kalimat-kalimat tersebut adalah:


In my opinion, ...

I think ...

I believe ...

I strongly believe ...

It seems to me that, ...

As far as I know ...

Berikut adalah contoh kalimat menyampaikan pendapat atau opinions:


In my opinion, students should play video games after school.

I think our school needs more trees.

It seems to me that she always forgets to do her homework.

I strongly believe that there should be screen time limits for kids.

19. Berikan 3 contoh soal esay dan jawaban tentang suggestion dan opinion?


Expression Giving Suggestion and Opinion

Expression Giving Suggestion

Expression giving suggestion adalah ekspresi untuk memberikan saran kepada orang lain, biasanya menggunakan should/had better/ought to/how about atau why don't. Untuk lebih jelasnya berikut contoh dalam kalimat :

You should study hard for the coming exam.Why don't you go home early tonight?You had better tell him the truth.

Asking and Giving Opinion Expression

Dalam asking opinion atau menanyakan tentang pendapat seseorang mengenai suatu hal, phrase yang sering digunakan sebagai berikut :

What is your opinion about....What do you think/reckon?Do you have any idea..Do you have any commentsDo you agree with me?What are your thoughts on that?Would you agree with me that...

Giving Opinion

Dalam giving opinion atau memberikan pendapat, phrase yang sering digunakan diantaranya :

In my opinion/in my humble opinion..I reckon/I think..From my point of viewAs far as I concernedIn my viewI believe/I feel/certainI am surePembahasan
