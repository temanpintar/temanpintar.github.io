9. Buatlah dialog tentang pemesanan kursi di seminar kedalam bahasa Inggris!


Making reservation adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk memesan sesuatu. Biasanya ungkapan ini digunakan untuk memesan kursi, tiket, ruangan/ kamar atas nama seseorang.


Contoh ungkapan-ungkapan yang digunakan untuk making and taking reservation:

Making Reservation:

I’d like to book/ reserve a table for 2 for 8 pm tonight I’d like to make reservation under the name “Elisa” Can we have a non-smoking table?I’d like to a flight from Jakarta to Yogyakarta on November 21

Taking Reservation:

How many people is the booking for? Under whose name the reservation should be made? Can you spell your name?Can I have your name please? Can I have your credit card number?
Pembahasan

Membuat dialog tentang pemesanan kursi pada seminar:

A: Hello, I would like to make a seat reservation.

B: How many seats is the booking for?

A; It is for two people.

B: Can I have your name please?

A; My last name is Al Qodri.

B: Can you spell your name?

A; ei - el - qyu - ou - ar - ai

B: Thank you. You must pay $20 for two people reservation. You may pay at the National Bank near your house or office before 7.30 PM.

A: I will pay it as soon as possible.

B: Thank you so much. See you on the seminar.

21. wacana deskripsi bahasa Inggris yang berjudul My mother


My Lovely Mother

I have a lovely mother. Her name is Wati. She is the best mother in the world for me. She always helps me and is always there whenever I need her or whenever I need a help. She always listens to my stories and my problems and she likes to give me the best advices.

My mother was born on September, 20th 1968. She has a beautiful face as me, or she is even more beautiful than me. She has an oval face with chubby cheeks. She has wavy short hair. Her eyes are black as well as her hair. Her skin is rather dark, just the same as me. It is the kind of Asian skin, especially for those who live in tropical countries, such as Indonesia, Malaysia, or Singapore. We call it here as ‘sawo matang’ skin. Besides, she has loud voice. It is not very common for Javanese person, especially Javanese woman, has loud voice. But, my mother has a loud voice because she is a teacher so that she is used to talk loudly so that all of her students are able to listen to her clearly in the class.

My mother is a discipline person. Since she was young, my mother is always used to be discipline and is used to put everything in its place. She likes everything tidy. She will get mad if her children do not put everything that they just use back to its place where they take it. So, my mother’s behavior, in my opinion as her child, is aso in her children’s behavior. I am a discipline person. Even though my mother is a discipline person, she is also a humorous person. She likes to get a joke to everyone. She sometimes like to pinch me or my sister. Nevertheless, what I adore my mother is she is an independent woman. She likes to do everything by her own. As long as she can do it alone, then she will do it. I really want to be like her. Moreover, my mom has a soft heart. She can easily cry if there is a thing or even a little thing touch her heart. My mother is the best mother in the world for me. I want to be like her and I want to educate my children later in the future just like the way she educates me until I become someone independent and diligent. I really love my mother.

22. Bagaimana contoh kalimat pembukaan untuk seminar proposal skripsi dalam bahasa inggris yg bukan sebagai moderator ? Ada yg bisa bantu ?


Ini adalah contoh salam pembuka yang bisa kita gunakan saat seminar proposal skripsi:

Good afternoon. First of all, I would like to extend my greetings to distinguished examiners:

Ms. Aminah, the first examiner

Mr. Budi, second examiner

Mr. Hidayat, third examiner and my advisor

Today I'm going to present my research with a title "...."

Berikut adalah terjemahannya dalam Bahasa Indonesia:

Selamat sore. Pertama-tama, saya ingin menyampaikan salam kepada para penguji yang terhormat:

Bu Aminah, sebagai penguji pertama

Pak Budi, sebagai penguji kedua

Pak Hidayat, sebagai penguji ketiga dan dosen pembimbing saya

Hari ini, saya ingin mempresentasikan proposal/skripsi/penelitian saya yang berjudul "...."

Nama-nama penguji pada contoh di atas bisa diubah sesuai nama dosen penguji dan dosen pembimbing yang hadir dalam seminar proposal skripsi tersebut. Jika beragama muslim, dapat juga menambahkan ‘Assalamualaikum’ pada salam pembuka.

Ucapan ‘good afternoon’ juga bisa sesuaikan dengan waktu pelaksanaan ujian. Misalnya jika harus seminar proposal skripsi di pagi hari kita dapat menggantinya dengan ‘good morning’.  

Pembahasan

Seminar proposal skripsi adalah salah satu tahapan yang harus dilalui seorang mahasiswa sebagai syarat kelulusan. Biasanya, untuk meraih gelar sarjana seseorang harus menyelesaikan skripsi yang kemudian diuji oleh beberapa dosen sekaligus.

Seminar proposal skripsi adalah ujian dimana seorang mahasiswa harus mempresentasikan rancangan penelitiannya kepada penguji. Rancangan penelitian ini kemudian akan dilanjutkan dengan penelitian dan dituliskan dalam skripsi.

Jika skripsi sudah berhasil diselesaikan, mahasiswa tersebut akan menghadapi sidang skripsi. Dalam sidang skripsi, mahasiswa harus mempresentasikan hasil penelitiannya yang tertuang dalam skripsi tersebut.

37. puisi berbahasa bali tentang Bhakti Ring Catur Guru


Subakti Ring Guru

“Om Swastyastu”

Inggih ida dane sareng sami sane wangiang titiang, para pamilet lomba pidarta basa bali, para guru miwah astiti sane wangiang titiang. Sadurung titiang ngawitin pidarta puniki, ngiring ngastawa ring Ida Sang Parama Kawi, santukan Ida ledang mapaica kerahajengan, mawinan titiang miwah ida dane prasida kacunduk masadu ajeng sekadi mangkin. Sane mangkin titiang jagi ngaturang pidarta sane mamurda“Subakti Ring Guru”.

Sadurung ngeranjing ring wewidangan bebaosan punika, jagi aturang titiang indik pamiiteges catur guru. Catur guru wenten akehnyane patpat guru. Sapatutne iraga uning sapasira kemanten sane kabaos pinaka guru. Kaping siki sang maraga guru rupaka. Wantah I Meme lan I Bapa utawi I Biang lan I Aji sane ngembasang tur miara iraga sareng sami. Iraga patut ngelaksanayang piteket-piteket danene samaliha nenten dados tungkas, tulak ring rerama.

Kaping kalih sang maraga guru pengajian wantah guru ring sekolahan sane ngicen para sisiane keweruhan mangda para sisiane dados anak dueg. Sane tan uning dados uning. Iraga patut bakti tan dados tulak ring guru pengajian.

Kaping tiga sang maraga guru wisesa sane kabaos pemerintah. Pemerintah sekadi mangkin sane ngaryanang awig-awig ring para jana. Iraga patut ngalaksanayang awig-awig punika mangda jagate kerta raharja.

Kaping pat sang maraga guru swadyaya wantah Ida Sang Hyang Widhi Wasa. Ida sane ngicen kahuripan ring jagate. Iraga patut bakti ring Ida Sang Hyang Widhi Wasa mangdane ngamolihang keselamatan miwah kerahayuan ring jagate.

Inggih ida dane sinamian sane wangiang titiang, wantah asapunika atur pidartan titiang dumogi wenten pikenohnyane. Titiang nunas sinampura mantuk ring saluir kaiwangan atur titiang sane tan manut ring arsa. Pinaka panguntat puputang titiang antuk parama santi.
