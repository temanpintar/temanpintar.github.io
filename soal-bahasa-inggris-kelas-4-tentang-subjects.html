29. change sentences into passive sentences 1. rony prepares subjects 2.he can do hard subjects 3. they are doing english test 4. rony prepares subject yesterday


CHANGE INTO PASSIVE VOICE

_____________________

JAWABAN :

POLA SIMPLE PRESENT PASSIVE.V

S+to be(is/am/are)+Verb 3+by+ Agent

1. Rony prepares subjects. ( ACTIVE )

[ INDONESIAN = Rony MEM-persiapkan pelajaran ]

=> Subjects ARE PREPARED by Rony.

( Passive )

[INDONESIAN = Pelajaran DI-persiapkan oleh Tony ]

_______________

POLA MODAL AUX. PASSIVE

S+Modal Aux.+be+Verb 3 +by+agent

¶ Modal auxiliary ( can, will, should, shall, could, would, may, might , must etc )

2.he can do hard subjects ( Active )

[ INDONESIAN = Dia bisa MENG-erjakan pelajaran yang sulit ]

=> Hard subjects CAN BE DONE by him. ( Passive )

[ INDONESIAN = Pelajaran yang sulit bisa DI-kerjakan oleh nya ( dia ) ]

____________________

POLA PRESENT CONTINUOUS PASSIVE. V

S+to be ( is/am/are)+being+verb 3 + by + Agent

3. they are doing english test. ( Active

[ INDONESIAN = Mereka sedang MENG-erjakan tes bahasa inggris ]

=> English test IS BEING DONE by them ( PASSIVE )

[ INDONESIAN = Tes bahasa inggris sedang DI-kerjakan oleh mereka ]

___________________

POLA SIMPLE PAST PASSIVE . V

S+to be ( was/were)+verb 3 + by + Agent

4. rony prepared subject yesterday. ( Active )

[ INDONESIAN = Rony telah MEM-persiapkan mata pelajaran kemarin ]

=> Subject WAS PREPARED by Rony yesterday . ( Passive )

[ INDONESIAN = Mata pelajaran telah DI-persiapkan oleh Rony kemarin ]

∆ PENJELASAN ∆

=> Passive menitikberatkan pada tindakan aksi daripada si pelaku aksi.

=> Jika ingin merubah kalimat aktif ke bentuk passive, harap diperhatikan struktur tenses nya karena pada dasarnya kalimat pasif mengikuti tenses masing-masing kalimat.

=> Tentukan obyek dalam kalimat aktif. Obyek ini akan dirubah posisi menjadi SUBYEK dalam kalimat pasif. Sedangkan subyek ( pelaku aksi ) dalam kalimat aktif akan berubah posisi menjadi AGENT dalam kalimat pasif . Harap diperhatikan posisi penempatan kata ganti orangnya ( Pronoun ) .

31. Please help now all those questions, The question is in the attachment above ! INCLUDE EXPLANATION ! [ THANKYOU ] _____________________________ SUBJECTS : BAHASA INGGRIS THEORY : EXPRESSING CAUSE AND EFFECT ( such ... that and so ... that) CLASS : XII SMA​


Jawaban:

EXPRESSING CAUSE AND EFFECT.

(There are 2 example of CAUSE & EFFECT)

1. Such that

2. So that

Example:

Cause: It was too windy.

Effect: We couldn't go sailing.

Answer: It was so windy that we could't go sailing. (This is the example of "so that")

TO THE QUESTIONS.

7. I can't figure out what this sentence says. His handwriting is illegible.

Cause: His handwriting is illegible.

Effect: I can't figure out what this sentence says.

Answer: His handwriting is so illegible that I can't figure out what this sentence says. (Using "so that")

8. Angel has too many girlfriends. He can't remember all of their names.

Cause: He has too many girlfriends.

Effect: He can't remember all of their names.

Answer: He has such many girlfriends that he can't remember all of their names. (Using "such that")

9. It took us only ten minutes to get there. There was little traffic.

Cause: There was a little traffic.

Effect: It took us only ten minutes to get there.

Answer: There was a such little traffic that it took us only ten minutes to get there. (Using "such that")

10. Few people bought tickets to the concert. They had to cancel it.

Cause: Few people bought tickets to the concert.

Effect: They had to cancel it.

Answer: Few people bought such tickets to the concert, that they had to cancel it. (Using "such that")
