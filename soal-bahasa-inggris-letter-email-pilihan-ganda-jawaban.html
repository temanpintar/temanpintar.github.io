An email has three basic parts. First is the Header, which is a set of lines containing information about the transmission of a message, such as the sender's address, recipient address, or stamp indicating the time when the message was sent by an intermediary server to a transport agent (MTA), which acts as an electronic mail sorting office.



The header also records all transmission paths that the message passes until it reaches the destination server. By using the Header, you can see the path taken by email in the transmission process and how long it will take for each server to process the transmission.



The second is the message, which consists of the following two elements: Header is a set of lines that describe the settings of the message, such as sender, receiver, date, etc. An email has at least three of the following headers: from the one containing the sender's e-mail address, to the one containing the recipient's e-mail address, the date that contains the time when the e-mail was received is various information about the intermediate server and the date when the message was processed, reply to i.e. the reply address, the subject of the message subject, the Message ID which is the unique identification of a message, the body of the e-mail containing the message separate from the header by a line.

Penggunaan bahasa Inggris ternyata tidak luput dalam bentuk penulisan lewat media apapun. Perihal ini tercermin dengan banyaknya hal dalam penulisan atau pun non-penulisan yang sifatnya untuk berkomunikasi. Secara filosofis, fungsi daripada bahasa itu adalah menyampaikan pesan dengan berbagai bentuk, entah visual, verbal, audio, audio visual, atau pun kinestetik.

Seiring dengan berkembangnya zaman, maka teknologi pun juga menggunakan bahasa sebagai alat komunikasi. Dengan teknologi yang terus berkembang dan merebak luas ke seluruh dunia, bahasa Inggris pun pada akhirnya juga menjadi bahasa internasional yang mampu menyokong hampir setiap esensi perkembangan teknologi tersebut.

Relevansi antara bahasa Inggris dengan perkembangan teknologi yang kian waktu kian berkembang ternyata juga mampu menembus batas dimensi waktu yang dipisahkan oleh lautan dan samudra yang membentang luas. Media yang dimaksud dalam perihal ini adalah surat elektronik (email), maka mari sama-sama belajar bahasa Inggris.

Personal Letter:

A personal letter is a type of communication where individuals express their thoughts, feelings, and experiences to someone they have a personal connection with. It is usually written in an informal and conversational tone, allowing for a more intimate and personalized expression of thoughts and emotions.

Cause and Effects:

Cause and effect is a relationship between events or actions where one event (the cause) brings about another event (the effect). It explores the reasons why something happens (cause) and the consequences or results that follow (effect). This type of analysis helps to understand the underlying factors and outcomes of various phenomena, actions, or situations.

Song:

A song is a musical composition typically consisting of lyrics and melody. It is a form of artistic expression that combines words and music to convey emotions, tell a story, or express ideas. Songs can evoke various emotions and can be used for entertainment, self-expression, social commentary, or communication of personal experiences.

Explanation text is a type of non-fiction text that aims to explain or clarify a particular subject or concept. It provides factual information, definitions, processes, or descriptions to help readers understand a specific topic. 38. contoh soal bahasa Inggris(email) beserta jawaban dan penjelasan nya​


E-Mail

Surat Elektronik adalah surat yang ditulis secara tidak resmi dengan media elektronik atau E-mail. E-mail mempounyai banyak tujuan, antara lain:

Memberi kabar dan menanyakan kabar Menceritakan pengalaman Melamar pekerjaan

Generic Structure dari Private Letter adalah:

1. Sender and Receiver in the form of e-mail address

2. Greeting: Some times we call it “salutation”. It is the introductory phrase. It can be a phrase like:

Dear Tono, Dear My friend, Dear My Brother, etc

Body: All kinds of letter formal or informal have the letter body. This is the complete text of the letter or the subject matter content.

3. Closing: It is the farewell phrase. That is a word that precedes the signature and is followed by a comma. The phrases can be like; Yours truly, Sincerely, Respectfully yours, Regards, etc.

Language features dari Private letter adalah:

1. Use simple present tense, untuk menanyakan kabar maupun memberi kabar.

2. Use simple past tense, saat digunakan untuk menceritakan pengalaman

3. Use informal language, menggunakan bahasa tidak formal.
