13. Dalam suatu kelas terdapat 34 siswa. Banyak siswa yang gemar olah raga dua kali banyak siswa yang gemar kesenian, sedangkan banyak siswa olah raga dan kesenian 5 orang. Jika terdapat 6 siswa yang tidak gemar olah raga maupun kesenian, banyak siswa yang hanya gemar olah raga adalah


olahraga=2×kesenian
5 suka or dan kesenian
6 tidak suka keduanya

misal
olahraga=x
kesenian=y

34-6=28
(x-5)+(y-5)=28
ingat bahwa
olah raga=2×kesenian
x=2y
maka x bisa disubtitusi 2y
(2y-5)+(y-5)=23
3y-10=23
3y=33
maka y=11
kembali ke persamaan x=2y, subtitusi y=11
maka x=22

- jalan kaki

- bersepeda

- senam kesegaran jasmani

- lari kecil/ jogging

- berenang

- yoga

Pembahasan:

Kegiatan olahraga adalah sebuah kegiatan yang mengutamakan aktivitas fisik untuk menjaga kesegaran jasmani dan juga kesehatan. Kegiatan olahraga sedikit berbeda dengan permainan olahraga karena dalam kegiatan olahraga tidak ada kompetisi atau persaingan untuk menjadi pemenang dalam sebuah pertandingan. Kegiatan tersebut lebih bersifat personal, individu atau beberapa orang saja tanpa ada aturan-aturan yang menentukan atau mengikat. Sehingga kegiatan olahraga lebih bersifat menyenangkan tanpa ada tekanan tertentu. Maka dari itu, contoh yang ada adalah jenis-jenis kegiatan fisik yang digemari individu masing-masing seperti jogging, berlari, renang, senam/ aerobik, yoga atau latihan fisik di pusat kebugaran.

38. deskriptif bahasa Inggris tentang olah raga​


Sports play a significant role in society, providing both physical activity and entertainment. They encompass a wide range of activities, from individual pursuits to team competitions, each with its own unique characteristics and benefits.

One popular sport is football, also known as soccer. Played by two teams on a rectangular field, the objective is to score goals by maneuvering the ball into the opponent's net using any part of the body except the arms and hands. Football requires teamwork, coordination, and skillful footwork, making it a thrilling and fast-paced game loved by millions worldwide.

Basketball, another widely enjoyed sport, involves two teams aiming to score points by shooting a ball through the opponent's hoop. Players dribble, pass, and shoot while adhering to rules governing fouls and violations. Basketball emphasizes agility, speed, and strategic thinking, making it an exhilarating sport to watch and participate in.

Gymnastics showcases exceptional strength, flexibility, and grace. This sport involves performing a series of coordinated movements, including flips, twists, and balances, on various apparatus like bars, balance beams, and floor mats. Gymnasts display precise control, body awareness, and artistic expression, captivating audiences with their breathtaking routines.

Track and field events encompass a variety of athletic disciplines, including sprinting, long-distance running, jumping, throwing, and hurdling. These individual sports test speed, endurance, strength, and technique. Athletes compete to achieve personal bests and set new records, inspiring others with their determination and athletic prowess.

Other popular sports such as tennis, swimming, cricket, and golf each have their own unique rules and requirements. Regardless of the sport, participation offers numerous benefits. Sports promote physical fitness, improve cardiovascular health, enhance coordination and motor skills, and foster discipline, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

In conclusion, sports are diverse and encompass a wide array of activities. Whether it's the thrill of team competition, the grace of individual performances, or the pursuit of personal goals, sports provide opportunities for physical activity, entertainment, and personal growth.
