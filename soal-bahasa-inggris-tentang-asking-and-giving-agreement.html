4. buatlah 10 kalimat asking and giving fact dalam bahasa inggris​

Contoh

When will you finish your paper? (kapan kamu akan menyelesaikan tugasmu?)

Answer : I will finish it on the next meeting (aku akan menyelesaikan itu pada pertemuan selanjutnya)

What kind of music do you like? (Jenis musik apa yang kamu sukai?)

Answer: I like Jazz Music (aku suka musik Jazz )

Where is Mitha? (Dimana Mitha?)

When you were born? (Kapan kamu lahir?)

Answer: in 1 April 1988 (pada April 1988)

Answer: She is absent, because she’s sick. (dia tidak hadir karena dia sakit)

6. Tuliskan struktur asking and giving information, asking and giving opinion, agreement


1. Asking n giving information
    > Wh? + Helping Verb + Subject + Verb
2. Asking n giving opinion
    > Wh? + opinion verb + Subject + verb
3. Asking n giving agreement
    > Subject + Agreement verb + with + object

B: Hi, A

A: What happen to you? Why do you look confused?

B: Oh A, I have some problem about where I go after graduating from junior high school next. I'm confused because I don't know what I should choose between SMA or SMK. Could you give me your opinion about my problem?

A: Of course, B. In my opinion, it's not about talking among SMA and SMK, but it talks about what you need and want to do for next step. You can take SMA for getting wide knowledge, and You can take SMK for preparing your softskills in working world.

Asking and Giving Agreement

X: Hi, Z

Z: Hello, X

X: Z, I want to ask you something

Z: Okay X, what is your question about?

X: Do you agree if Indonesia is the richest country exactly in traditional culture?

Z: Yes, I agree. As we can see that indonesia has many traditional culture from each province consisting their identity, one of the most famous culture is traditional dance such as Saman, Kecak, and etc

Expression of congratulation

27. definis expression asking and giving permission dalam bahasa inggris


asking permission is a situation when you ask for permission, it is desirable to use please, but not always.
for example : can i go, please ? or, do you mind if i use your pen ?

giving permission is a situation when you give a permission.
