Penjelasan:

1. Ubahlah kalimat berikut ke dalam bahasa Inggris dengan memperhatikan pola rumus yang sudah diberitahukan!

a. Saya sakit kemarin

I was sicked yesterday

Menggunakan kata sicked karena past tense dari 'sick' adalah 'sicked'.

b. Joni tidak pergi ke sekolah senin lalu

Joni didn't go to school last Monday

Menggunakan kata go bukannya 'went' karena itu kalimat lampau tetapi tidak terjadi karena 'Joni tidak pergi ke sekolah senin lalu' yang berarti itu sebuah kata lampau tetapi tidak terjadi.

Jadi saya gunakan kata go.

c. Apakah mereka datang malam tadi?

Did they come last night?

Menggunakan kata come karena itu adalah sebuah kalimat tanya lampau, yang berarti kita tidak tahu itu terjadi atau tidak.

Jadi saya gunakan kata come bukannya 'came'.

2. Terjemahkan ke bahasa Indonesia!

a. Maya was sad last night

Artinya = Maya sedih tadi malam

b. Did you cook with your mom yesterday?

Artinya = Apakah kamu memasak dengan ibumu kemarin?

Past continuous tense or also commonly known as past progressive tense is a tense that is used to express activities that are happening at a certain time in the past.
The action / activity has been and is ongoing but has not been completed at that time and sometimes also interspersed with an action / activity that occurs briefly.

as example ;
1.my mother 'was' cooking fried rice while I 'was' repairing my laptop.

2.'were' yo u working all night yesterday?

3.rina 'wasn't' doing her home work when Santi called her.

4.we 'weren't' playing outside when the teacher 'came'.

15. perbedaan antara present tense past tense and future tense dalam bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

if the present tense is used for events that are usually done or can be called 'habits', if past tense is used for activities that have occurred in the past,

while future tense is used for something that has not yet happened but has been planned, so you could say ideals.

Jawaban:

simple present tense

Used to show facts, habits, and general conditions that occur at this time.

formula: S + V / -s, es + complement

The time signal word for this tense is usually, always, every, sometimes, normally, seldom, etc.

Simple Past Tense

Used to indicate an event in the past.

formula: S + V2 + complement

The said time signals for this tense are last year, last month, yesterday, in 1996, last Sunday, etc.

Simple Future Tense

Used to indicate an action or a situation in the future.

formula: S + will / be going to + V1 + complement

The said time signal for this tense is next, tomorrow, five days later, etc.
