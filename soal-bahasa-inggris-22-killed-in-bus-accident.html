40. change into passive in two ways 1. the accident took 2 lives 2. the bridge will collapse in two years 3. the virus has killed millions of people​


Rubah ke dalam kalimat pasif dengan dua cara:

1. The accident took 2 lives.

       a. Two lives were taken by the accident.

       b. Lives were taken in two by the accident.

2.  The bridge will collapse in two years.

       a. In two years will be the collapsed of the bridge.

       b. It will be the collapsed of the bridge in two years.

3.     The virus has killed millions of people.

        a. Millions of people have been killed by the virus.

        b. People were killed in millions by the virus.

Penjelasan:

• Kalimat Aktif (Active Voice) adalah sebuah kalimat dimana sebuah subjek melakukan aksi/tindakan terhadap sebuah objek.

• Kalimat Pasif (Passive Voice) adalah sebuah kalimat dimana sebuah Objek dikenai aksi/tindakan oleh sebuah Subjek.

Contoh:

Kalimat Aktif: He throw a ball. (Dia melempar sebuah bola.)

Kalimat Pasif: A ball is thrown by him. (Sebuah bola dilempar oleh dia.)

Kalimat Aktif: My uncle will give me a present. (Paman saya akan memberikan saya sebuah hadiah.)

Kalimat Pasif: A present will be given to me by my uncle. (Sebuah hadiah akan diberikan kepada saya oleh paman saya.)

Struktur Kalimat Aktif dan Pasif (Active dan Passive Voice):

Present Tense

Active: Subjek + Verb 1 + Objek

Passive: Objek + to be (is/am/are) + by Subject

Present Continuous

Active: Subjek + to be (is/am/are) + Verb 1 + ing + Objek

Passive: Objek + to be (is/am/are) + being + Verb 3 + by Subject

Present Perfect

Active: Subjek + has/have + Verb 3 + Objek

Passive: Objek + has/have + been + Verb 3 + by Subject

Future Tense

Active: Subjek + will + Verb 1 + Objek

Passive: Objek + will be + Verb 3 + by Subject

Should

Active: Subjek + should + Verb 1 + Objek

Passive: Objek + should be + Verb 3 + by Subject
