20. ringkasan pengertian suggest and offer dalam bahasa inggris


suggest: to mention and idea,possible plan,or action for other people to consider.
e.g.: i suggest that we wait a while before we make any firm decision.

offer: to ask someone if they would like to have something or if they would like you to do something.
e.g.: my father offered to take us to the airport.

26. kesimpulan tentang suggest dan offer dalam bahasa inggris dan indonesia


Answer: 

In English and probably any other languages, suggestion and offer are quite different. Suggestion is used to propose or give new ideas to others for an event, solution to any issues, merely activities or any other situation. Meanwhile, offer is used to offer a help to others. Therefore, the form of it in a sentence is different. 

Here's the translation: 
Dalam bahasa Inggris dan mungkin di bahasa lainnya, pengusulan ide dan penawaran cukup berbeda. Pengusulan ide (suggestion) digunakan untuk memberi ide kepada pihak lain, baik tentang event, solusi permasalahan, kegiatan ataupun lainnya. Sementara penawaran (offer) digunakan untuk menawarkan bantuan ke orang lain. Karena itulah, bentuknya dalam kalimat pun berbeda.

Rumus Kalimat: Subject + Verb + That + Object offering : Modal verb + Subject + Object

1. Would you like a dance?

Artinya maukah kami menari ?

2. Do you want more sugar in your orange juice?

4. Wait a minute, please. And what would you like to eat?

Artinya Tunggu sebentar. Dan apa yang ingin anda makan?

Artinya Bolehkah saya menawarkan teh?

Artinya Apakah Anda ingin duduk?
