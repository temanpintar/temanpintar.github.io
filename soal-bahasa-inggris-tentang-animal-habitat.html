Hibernation is a condition of inactivity and decreased metabolism in animals characterized by lower body temperature, slower breathing, and lower metabolic rate. Hibernating animals try to conserve energy, especially during winter when food scarcity occurs, burning energy reserves, body fat, slowly. Dragonfly Habitat

Dragonflies are a group of insects belonging to the order Odonata.

Most dragonfly life cycles are spent below the surface of the water, from laying eggs to pre-adult times, using internal gills to breathe. After adulthood, dragonflies can only live a maximum of four months.

Dragonfly's life cycle varies from six months to a maximum of six or seven years.

Dragonflies lay their eggs in plants that live in water. After the eggs hatch, the dragonfly larvae then live and develop in the bottom of the water, and experience metamorphosis into the nymph, before finally escaping from the water as an adult dragonfly.

38. Habitat ikan dalam bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

Writing Descriptive Text

Fish are aquatic animals which belong to the group of vertebrates. Unlike mammals, fish are cold-blooded. Their temperature mostly influenced by the environment surrounding them.

There are more than 30.000 species of fish in the ocean in the world. Each species have their own measurement, usually range from 8 mm to 16 meter. Some of the fishes have unique pattern on their scales. Those fishes are usually used by people as a decorative items.

Fish contain a lot of good nutrients for body such as protein, omega 3 and vitamins. Some species of fish, especially freshwater fish has been breed in the fish farm to fulfill needs of human. Other kind of fish like Koi and Betta are famous for their appearance and they’re usually kept in a pond as decoration.

[Terjemahan]

Ikan adalah binatang air yang masuk dalam golongan hewan vertebrata. Tidak seperti mamalia, ikan adalah hewan berdarah dingin. Suhu tubuh mereka sangat dipengaruhi oleh lingkungan sekitar.

Ada lebih dari 30.000 spesies ikan di laut di dunia. Setiap spesies punya ukurannya masing-masing, biasanya berkisar dari 8 mm hingga 16 meter. Kebanyakan dari ikan memiliki motif yang unik pada sisik mereka. Ikan-ikan tersebut biasanya digunakan sebagai bentuk dekorasi.

Ikan mengandung banyak nutrisi baik untuk tubuh seperti protein, omega 3 dan vitamin. Sebagaian spesies dari ikan, terutama ikan air tawar dikembangbiakan untuk memenuhi kebutuhan manusia. Jenis ikan lainnya seperti ikan Koi dan Betta terkenal atas penampilannya dan mereka biasanya dipelihara di kolam sebagai dekorasi.

Pembahasan

Deskripsi tentang ikan di atas masuk dalam kategori descriptive text. Descriptive text adalah sebuah bacaan yang memberikan informasi mengenai suatu tempat, orang, binatang, dll. Pada descriptive text sifatnya lebih mengarah kepada penggambaran, pengungkapan secara detil dan penjelasan.

Generic structure dari descriptive text adalah:

Identification => Pendahuluan, yakni gambaran umum suatu topik

Description => Ciri-ciri tentang topik yang dibahas, sifat-sifat yang dimiliki, tampilan khas, dll.
