17. Problem Math 15Soal Olimpiade sains tingkat Kabupaten/kota(OSK) 20171. Diketahui x − y = 10 dan xy = 10. Nilai x⁴+y⁴ adalah#Nospam(dilarang jawab asal)#followme#soalOlimpiade


xy = 10
x- y = 10
(x-y)² = 10²
x²+y² - 2xy = 100
x² +y² = 100+ 2xy
x² +y² = 100+ 20 = 120

(x⁴ + y⁴) = (x² +y²)² - 2(xy)²
x⁴ + y⁴= (120)² - 2(10)²
x⁴ + y⁴ = 14.400 - 200 = 14.200
