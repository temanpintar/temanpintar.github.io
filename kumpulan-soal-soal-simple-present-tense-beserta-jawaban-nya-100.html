Present continous:
1. Can I go swimming this friday?
A: yes, you can

2. Are you going ti the party today?
A: yes, I am

3. Is she crying?
A: yes she is
Simple Present:
1. Andi doesn't eat three time a day
= Andi tidak makan tiga kali sehari
2. Mr. Okto goes to Jakarta
= Pak Okto pergi ke Jakarta
3. Do they study English in the course?
= Apakah mereka belajar di tempat kursus?

23. Pengertian Simple Present Tense dan Past Tense berserta contoh nya?


present tense
when you are referring to habitual action that you always or never do
when you are referring to unchanging
or general statements of fact
pattern = s+verb 1 + object
past tense
pattern: s+ v 2+ object
when an activity began and ended at a particular time in pastSimple present tense ialah suatu kata kerja untuk menyatakan fakta, kebiasaan, atau kejadian yang terjadi pada saat ini. Bentuk kata kerja ini paling sering digunakan dalam bahasa Inggris.
Contoh : She likes eating out
Sedangkan Simple past tense ialah bentuk kata kerja sederhana untuk menunjukkan bahwa suatu kejadian terjadi di masa lampau.
Contoh : I was a stamp collector

25. membuat 10 soal direct questions dan dirubah ke indirect.. tiap 2 soal berbasic tenses(simple present, present continuous,simple past tense,present prefect tense dan simple future tense) dan mengandung to be dan verb


1. My friend said to me,"I want to be a great CEO one day"
My friend said to me that he wanted to be a great CEO one day.
2. His friend said to me,"They are very loud, I can't concentrate"
His friend said to him that they were so loud so he couldn't concentrate.
3. My mom said to me,"I am going to the shop now."
My mom said to me that she was going to shop at the moment.
4. My dad asked me,"Are you eating ice cream now?"
My dad asked me if I was eating ice cream at the moment.
5. My brother said to me,"I went to the gym yesterday"
My brother said to me that he had gone to gym the day before.
6. My sister asked me,"Where did you go last night?"
My sister asked me where I had gone the night before.
7. My uncle said to me,"My Wife has bought me a ticket."
My uncle said to me that his wife had bought him a ticket
8. My aunt asked me,"Have you gone to the birthday party?"
My aunt asked me if I had gone to the birthday party.
9. The teacher asked me,"Will you take my bag upstairs?"
The teacher asked me if I would take his bag upstairs.
10. The police told me,"You will be a great policeman someday,"
The police told me that I would be a great policeman someday.
