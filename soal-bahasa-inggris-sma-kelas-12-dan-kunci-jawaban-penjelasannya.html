1. contoh soal bahasa inggris sma dan kunci jawabannya


This text is for questions 1 and 2.

Mr. Hasan Renaldi

PT Jaya Sentosa

Jl. Hasanudin 73

Makasar

Dear Mr. Hasan Renaldi,

In response to your letter of December 20, 2015 we apologize for the error in shipment. We are sending immediately the additional 500 boxes of glossy photo papers, that were not included in the shipment.

We value our relationship with your company, and we regret the inconvenience. You can be assured that this will not happen again in the future. We also enclose some catalogues of our new products.

Sincerely yours,

Hilman

Manager of order Department

1) The purpose of sending the letter is to …
  A) request the paper excluded in the previous shipment.
  B) complete another request from PT Jaya Sentosa.
  C) request more orders of glossy photo papers.
  D) ask for apology for the error in shipment.
  E) enclose some new catalogues.

Pembahasan:
Membaca surat tentu saja lebih mudah daripada membaca cerita karena kamu pernah mempelajari penulisan surat dan memahaminya. Lakukan screening terhadap teks yang disajikan dan temukan tujuan penulis mengirimkan surat tersebut.
Dalam teks ini, kamu akan menemukannya pada baris kelima teks. Dengan demikian, jawaban yang tepat adalah D.

2) We know from the text that the first shipment was…
A) not complete.
B) not insured.
C) damaged.
D) wrong.
E) late.

Pembahasan:
Pertanyaan ini memiliki jawaban yang tidak tersurat, melainkan tersirat pada baris keenam dan ketujuh teks. Penulis menyatakan bahwa bersamaan dengan surat tersebut, ia juga mengirimkan 500 boks kertas foto tambahan yang tidak terkirim pada pengiriman sebelumnya. Dengan demikian, jawaban yang tepat adalah A.

3)  Rearrange the following sentences into the correct and meaningful paragraph!
1. Being afraid Maya and Rita sat closely to each other.     
2. What a relief! The shadow was their camp leader doing an inspection.     
3. It was midnight, but Maya and Rita were still awake.     
4. Then, they saw a tall and big shadow outside their tent.     
5. They peeped outside through a small hole in the tent.     
6. Suddenly, they heard a soft voice outside.     
7. After the campfire, all the scouts enter their tents to have a rest, including Maya and Rita.

A) 7 – 3 – 6 – 4 – 1 – 5 – 2
B) 7 – 3 – 2 – 5 – 4 – 6 – 1
C) 7 – 5 – 4 – 3 – 1 – 6 – 2
D) 7 – 3 – 4 – 5 – 2 – 1 – 6
E) 7 – 2 – 1 – 5 – 3 – 4 – 6

Pembahasan:
Lakukan penghematan waktu untuk soal semacam ini. Alih-alih mencoba menyusunnya sendiri yang bisa saja memakan waktu, cobalah mencoba susunan yang tersedia pada pilihan a hingga e. Kemudian, pilihlah susunan kalimat yang menghasilkan paragraf paling sempurna dengan susunan cerita yang jelas dan masuk akal. Dengan demikian, jawaban yang tepat adalah A.

In our daily life, we often offering help to others. This is one of an important expression that we need to learn in order to be polite and helpful.

As a response of the offering we can accepting or rejecting the offers. Here we will learn more about how to accepting an offer or help.

Some phrases that we usually use when accepting an offer :

Yes, please, I'd like toYes, please that would be very helpfulIf you wouldn't mindThank you, I wouldThank you, that would be greatThank you, that's very kind of you

Here an example how to use those phrases in a dialog :

Dina : You look so sad today. What is happening? How can I help you? (Kamu kelihatan sedih hari ini. Apa yang terjadi?Apa yang bisa kubantu?)

Ari : I failed my math test today. (saya gagal dalam ujian matematika)

Dina : Don't worry, you still have one more chance for a retest, Would you like some help with your study? (Jangan khawatir, masih ada satu kesempatan untuk mengulang, apa kamu perlu bantuan dalam belajar?

Ari :  Yes please. That would be very helpful. (Ya, itu akan sangat membantu)

Dina : OK, let's study together tomorrow afternoon after school. (OK, kita bisa belajar bersama besok setelah sekolah)

Ari : That's great!. See you tomorrow afternoon then! (Ya, sampai ketemu besok!)

Dina : See you! (Sampai ketemu!)

News item is a text which informs readers about events of the day. The events are considered newsworthy or important.

Generic Structure of News Item

Main Events / Newsworthy event(s): it recounts the event in summary form. Elaboration / Background event(s): they elaborate what happened, to whom, in what circumstance Resource of Information (Source) : it contains comments by participants in, witnesses to and authorities expert on the event

Language Feature of News Item

Focusing on circumstances using a simple language in writing the text Using saying verbs: “….”, She said, He informed, We told, I reported. Sometimes at the beginning of news, the scene is mentioned (Places) : Jakarta – … .. / Bangkok – … ../Manila - .... Using Past tense in explaining news events. Usually the events are in the past But if it is a fact that until now still happen or still in the form of fact, then can use simple present tense. Short, telegraphic information about story captured in headline Using adverbs: time, place and manner. Uses of material processes to retell the event
