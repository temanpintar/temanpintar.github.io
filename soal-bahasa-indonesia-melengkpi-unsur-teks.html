1. tina lives IN timika ON jln. teratai AT 13 jln.teratai.

2. dony lives IN west sumatra ON kec. padang AT kelurahan padang barat.

3. my family lives IN biak ON perum. sumberker AT 15/block c perum. sumberker

4. tomi lives IN east java ON sby AT jln. kodam

5. my uncle lives IN ambon ON perum. hartaco 24 AT jln. mawar

Jawaban:

1. Tina lives (in) Timika (on) Jln. Teratai (at) 13 Jln. Teratai.

2. Dony lives (in) West Sumatera (in) kecamatan Padang (in) kelurahan Padang Barat.

3. My family lives (in) Biak (in) perumahan Sumberker (at) 15/block c perumahan Sumberker.

4. Tomi lives (in) East Java (in) Surabaya (on) Jln. Kodam.

5. My uncle lives (in) Ambon (in) perumhan Hartaco 24 (on) Jln. Mawar.

Penjelasan:

-In intended to explain the existence of an area, island, city, country, province, continent and so on.

-On is used to show a place that is general but more specific. The word on is usually used to describe an address or street name.

-At serves to show a more specific place or show the name of a place.
