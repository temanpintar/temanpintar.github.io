7. tolong translate ke bahasa inggris [a lot of ,much , many] penggunaan nya harus sesuai jgn translate dari google


A lot of, much dan many dalam bahasa Inggris adalah Quantifier. Arti dari A lot of, much dan many secara umum adalah banyak, Namun dalam penggunaannya memiliki perbedaan.

Di dalam bahasa Inggris benda diklasifikasikan menjadi dua yaitu, benda yang dapat dihitung (countable noun) dan benda yang tidak dapat dihitung (uncountable noun). Contoh Countable noun : apple, chair, friend, egg. Kata benda yang countable noun memiliki bentuk single dan plural misalnya : apple-apples, friend-friends, chair-chairs, egg-eggs.

Contoh uncountable noun adalah water, money, time rice, sugar, kata benda uncountable noun tidak memiliki bentuk jamak (plural).

A lot of

A lot of artinya banyak dan bisa digunakan baik untuk countable maupun uncountable noun. A lot of adalah bentuk informal dari much dan many.

They have a lot of friends in this city.

You drink a lot of water.

He has a lot of work to do.

Many

Many digunakan untuk countable noun

I have many friends.

She reads many books.

Many people go to the festival.

Much

Much digunakan untuk uncountable noun.

I don't have much money to spend.

She has much time to do this homework.

How much is this book cost?

Note : A lot of hanya digunakan dalam kalimat positif. Untuk kalimat tanya dan negatif kita biasanya menggunakan much atau many.

Pembahasan

1. She has a lot of works to do today.  I don't have many works to do today. Do you have many works to do? I don't think so.

2. You need a lot of time to do that task. You don't need much money to do that. Do you need much time? Yes, I do.

3. She wants many people to help her. She doesn't want many places to do that. Does she want many friends in there? No, she doesn't.

4. We managed to collect  a lot of information today.  We don't manage to collect much money. Do you manage to collect much information about it?No, we don't.

5. Our company has a lot of branches. Our company doesn't have many offices. Does your company have many staffs? Yes, it does.

6. There are a lot of guests in my company. There were not many visitors yesterday. Are there many orders today? Yes, there are.

7. There is a lot of rubbish in the corner of this room today. There were not much rubbish yesterday. Is there much water? Yes, there is.

8. Is there much milk in that tin? Yes there is. There isn't much ink in that bottle. Is there much dust in your table? No, there isn't.

9. He has to buy a lot of paint. He doesn't have much time to paint. Does he have to buy many brushes for painting? No, he doesn't.

10. She sent a lot of cards to her friends last month. She doesn't get many cards from her friends. Does she send many cards this month? Maybe.

11. There are many male teachers in my course. In my course, we don't have many female teachers. Are there many female students in here? There are a lot of female students in here.

17. kegunaan kata much dalam bahasa inggris


Much
Much memilki arti yang sama dengan many yaitu banyak. Lalu apa perbedaannya dengan kata many? Jika many digunakan untuk benda-benda yang dapat dihitung maka much digunakan untuk benda-benda yang tidak dapat dihitung. Contoh penggunaannya dalam suatu kalimat adalah:
I have so much coffee in my cabinet.
Karena kata coffee termasuk dalam kata benda yang tidak dapat dihitung, maka kita menggunakan much untuk menjelaskan jumlahnya.

18. 1. Tuliskan 5 kalimat interogatif, negatif, dan positif bahasa inggris (2 tobe, 3 do/does)2. Buatlah 5 soal bertema How many/ How much berserta jawaban menggunakan There is/are


Simple Present Tense

Penulisan kalimat dalam bentuk simple present tense mengikuti pola/rumusan berikut:

Bentuk verbal:

(+) S + verb-1(+s/es) ...(-) S + do/does + not + verb-1 ...(?) Do/Does + S + verb-1 ... ?

Bentuk nominal:

(+) S + am/is/are ...(-) S + am/is/are + not ...(?) Am/Is/Are + S ... ?

Dengan kata "There":

(+) There is/are ...(-) There is/are + not ...(?) Is/Are there ... ?
Pembahasan

Bagian 1 - Tuliskan 5 kalimat dalam bentuk positif, negatif dan interogatif:

1. (+) Susan walks to her office.

(-) Susan doesn't walk to her office.

(?) Does Susan walk to her office?

2. (+) The boys enjoy playing football.

(-) The boys don't enjoy playing football.

(?) Do the boys enjoy playing football?

3. (+) I usually go to school by bicycle.

(-) I don't usually go to school by bicycle.

(?) Do you usually go to school by bicycle?

4. (+) Michael is very good at physics.

(-) Michael is not very good at physics.

(?) Is Michael good at physics?

5. (+) The girls are in the gym.

(-) The girls are not in the gym.

(?) Are the girls in the gym?


Bagian 2 - Pertanyaan dengan 'How many/How much...' beserta jawaban menggunakan 'There is/There are ...'

1. How many students are (there) in your class?

There are 35 students in my class.

2. How much petrol is (there) in the tank?

There is about 3 litres of petrol in the tank.

3. How many books are (there) in the box?

There are 30 books in the box.

4. How much water is (there) in the can?

There is no water in the can.

5. How many days are (there) in April?

There are 30 days in April.

40. Penggunaan kata some,many,much pada bahasa inggris menyatakan jumlah .... beserta pengertiannya​


penggunaan kata some :

"some" memiliki arti "beberapa", atau "sedikit". ini digunakan untuk countable dan uncountable nouns, dan hanya untuk kalimat positif.

contoh :

- i have some milk

- i eat some apples

kebalikannya yaitu "any". sama seperti tadi, tapi ini digunakan hanya untuk kalimat negatif. dan juga digunakan untuk kalimat tanya.

contoh :

- i don't have any money

- he doesn't eat any meat

penggunaan kata many :

"many" memiliki arti "banyak". ini digunakan hanya untuk countable nouns.

contoh :

- he read many books at the library

- how many cows are there in the farm?

penggunaan kata much :

"much" memiliki arti "banyak". ini digunakan hanya untuk uncountable nouns.

contoh :

- he drinks much water

- how much time do we have left?
