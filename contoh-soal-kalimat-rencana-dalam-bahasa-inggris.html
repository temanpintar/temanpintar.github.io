13. contoh teks rencana dalam bahasa inggris ..pliss dijawab


planning ...
"They Are was planning for they holiday"Contoh I : 

Cindra:
"Do you have any plans tonight?" (Apakah anda mempunyai rencana malam ini?)
Yoga:"I have class until 5 o’clock, but I’m free after that." (Saya sekolah sampai jam 5, tetapi setelah itu longgar)
Cindra:"Do you want to grab dinner?" (Apakah anda ingin makan malam?)
Yoga:"I don’t have any dinner plans so that sound great" (Saya tidak mempunyai rencana makan malam, itu usulan yang bagus)
Cindra:"What time should we meet?" (Jam berapa seharusnya kita bertemu?)
Yoga:"I need to go to the book store first, so let’s meet at 6 O’clock" (Pertama, saya harus ke toko buku, jadi kita ketemu jam 6.)

Contoh II :

Dimas:"Let’s go to Everland next week" (Mari kita pergi ke Everland minggu depan)
Noven:"I can’t. I’m way too busy." (Saya tidak bisa. Saya terlalu sibuk)
Dimas:"What are you doing next week?." (Apa yang anda lakukan minggu depan?)
Noven:"On Monday, I’m going to meet my professor. Tuesday, I have a job interview. Wednesday, I’m going to the dentist. And on Thursday, I have to go visit my parents." (Pada hari Senin, saya akan bertemu dengan profesor saya. Selasa, saya mempunyai tugas wawancara. Rabu, saya pergi ke dokter gigi. Dan pada hari Kamis, saya harus pergi mengunjungi orangtua saya.)
Dimas:"Why are you so busy?" (Mengapa anda begitu sibuk?)
Noven:"I’ve been procrastinating for a long time" (Saya menunda-nunda terlalu lama)

Contoh III:

Wawan:"What are you doing over the weekend." (Apa yang sobat lakukan setelah akhir pekan?)
Hendik:"I’m going to exercise, meet some friends, and then watch tv." (Saya akan latihan, bertemu beberapa teman, dan kemudian menonton tv.)
Wawan:"Who are you going to meet?" (Siapa yang akan kamu kunjungi?)
Hendik:"My friend from middle school is visiting, so we are going to have dinner on Saturday." (Mengunjungi teman saya dari sekolah menengah, Kami akan malam pada hari Sabtu)
Wawan:"How long is he going to stay?" (Berapa lama ia akan tinggal?)
Hendik:"He is only here for a day" (Ia hanya di sini selama satu hari)
Wawan:"Where are you going to eat dinner?" (Di mana anda akan makan malam?)
Hendik:"I plan on taking him to an Italian restaurant" (Saya berencana untuk mengatakan padanya di sebuah restoran Italia)

Quiz.Bahasa inggris Apa ?Contoh kalimat ?Bahasa inggris Dimana ?Contoh kalimat ?Bahasa inggris Kapan ?Contoh kalimat ?bahasa inggris Siapa ?Contoh kalimat ?Bahasa inggris Mengapa ?Contoh Kalimat ?Bahasa inggris Bagaimana ?Contoh kalimat ?Bahasa inggris berapa ?Contoh kalimat ?Nt : First ? akhirnya dapat bagian pertama​


Jawaban:

1. Bahasa inggris Apa ? What

Contoh kalimat ? “What made you happy today?”

2. Bahasa inggris Dimana ? Where

Contoh kalimat ? “Where is a suitable place for a picnic?”

3. Bahasa inggris Kapan ? When

Contoh kalimat ? “When did you come back from Surabaya?”

4. Bahasa inggris Siapa ? Who

Contoh kalimat ? "Who picked the flower?"

5. Bahasa inggris Mengapa ? Why

Contoh Kalimat ? “Why are you crying?"

6. Bahasa inggris Bagaimana ? How

Contoh kalimat ? “How are you today?”

7. Bahasa inggris berapa ? How many / How much

Contoh kalimat ? “How much does that book cost?”

20. contoh essay tentang rencana/mimpi dalam bahasa inggris


My dream is to become a professional photographer. It is important to me because photography is a good way of keeping a record of the places you visit, people you know, and things that you see. It is also fun and a creative hobby.

To reach my dream I will take photography classes in the summer and take a photography course in school. It will most probably be hard work but achieving your goal is also probably hard work.

I also chose these pictures because photography is all about art and you also have to develop the film and change the lenses. There are many types of cameras to choose and work with but they all work on the same principle and have many parts in common.

Whenever seeing a good picture that I have taken gives me a great sense of achievement and there is nobody in this world that doesn't like succeeding. There are times when you fail but you have to get up and try again and again until you succeed.

The thing I like most about my popped balloon is that it kind of represents the way I feel sometimes. Whenever I'm frustrated I feel like my world is falling apart like the pieces of the balloons. But then I usually calm down and everything is okay.

If I could find a perfect picture for my dream it would probably be a picture of me in the future when I have achieved my goal. I would be standing on a hill that overlooks great scenery just snapping away with my very expensive (hopefully) camera. But the most important thing is that I'll be happy doing what I do. If you're not happy with what you do with you living, then it will just become an awful thing that you don't look forward to doing. If you don't look forward to it then why bother? Your life will be completely miserable.

21. buatkan 5 kalimat rencana dalam bahasa Inggris​


Jawaban:

itu yah contoh nya maaf ya kalau ga benar dan lebih dari 5

Jawaban:

I will buy some water because they are trusty (Saya akan membeli air karena mereka kehausan)

• I am bored, I will go to coffee shop (Saya bosan, saya akan pergi ke kedai kopi)

• Will you go with me? (Apakah kamu mau pergi dengan saya?)

• The phone is ringing, I will pick it up (Telepon berbunyi, saya akan mengangkatnya)

• I am going to Pekanbaru tomorrow (Saya akan pergi ke Pekanbaru besok)

Penjelasan:

rumus kalimat rencana

Rumus pertama menggunakan kata “will”, kata will ini digunakan untuk mengungkapkan suatu hal yang tidak direncanakan sebelumnya atau disebut juga suatu perencanaan. Contoh kalimatnya : “She gives me a cupcake, I will thank to her” (Dia memberikan saya sebuah cupcake, saya akan berterimakasih padanya)

Rumus kedua mengunakan kata “going to” yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan suatu aktivitas atau rencana yang sudah direncanakan sebelumnya. Contoh kalimatnya : “I am going to take a bath” (Saya akan mandi).

Dan terakhir menggunakan present continuos tense. Present continous tense sendiri sering digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan yang terjadi sekarang atau saat ini. Namun present continous tense juga sering digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kegiatan yang direncanakan. Contoh kalimatnya : “They are going to the school this afternoon” (Mereka akan pergi ke sekolah siang ini)
