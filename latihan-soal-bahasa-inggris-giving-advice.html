1. Buatlah surat tentang Giving Advice dalam bahasa inggris


I am honored that you would ask for my advice regarding which college you should attend. I can only tell you about my experience attending State University in Springfield. I had a very good experience there; the computer science department is the best in the state and the classes are small and personal. Compared with many other colleges, the cost of tuition is low. The social life at State is fun but not rowdy. If you are planning to study computer science, I think it would be a good choice. If you plan to study something else, I am not the best person to give advice.

I wish you the best of luck and have every confidence that you will make the right decision for you. Please contact me again if I can be of further assistance.

Elena,

Here is an example of a conversation asking and giving advice about photography:

A: Hi, I'm interested in photography and I want to improve my skills. Do you have any advice for me?

B: Sure, I'd be happy to help. What kind of photography are you interested in?

A: I'm interested in landscape and nature photography.

B: Great! One thing you can do is to study the work of other photographers in that genre. Look at their composition, lighting, and use of color. You can also practice by going out and taking photos yourself. Try experimenting with different angles and perspectives to create unique shots.

A: That's a good idea. Do you have any recommendations for camera settings?

B: It depends on the lighting and the effect you want to achieve. For landscape photography, you may want to use a smaller aperture to create a larger depth of field and capture more detail. You can also try using a slower shutter speed to create a sense of motion in waterfalls or clouds.

A: Thanks for the tips. Do you have any recommendations for editing software?

B: There are many options out there, but some popular ones include Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop, as well as free options like GIMP and RawTherapee. You can experiment with different software to find the one that works best for you.

A: That's helpful. Thanks for your advice!

B: You're welcome. Remember to keep practicing and experimenting, and don't be afraid to try new things. Good luck with your photography!

39. 10 contoh soal dan jawaban tentang giving suggestion (dalam bahasa inggris)


Giving suggestion (memberikan saran) diucapkan oleh seseorang kepada lawan bicaranya dengan tujuan untuk menyampaikan saran atau masukan kepada lawan bicaranya, agar orang tersebut bersedia melaksanakan saran atau masukannya. Saran atau suggestion yang diberikan ini bisa berupa nasihat, pendapat, gagasan, dan yang semacamnya.

Beberapa contoh ekspresi yang sering digunakan untuk giving suggestion di antaranya adalah:

I think .......

What about .............

What if ....................

You should ....................

Let's ..............................

Maybe you want to ............................

How if .......

How about .....

Contoh 10 pertanyaan pilihan ganda tentang giving suggestion.

1. Tom looks very tired. What's your suggestion to him?

a. you should eat more.

b. you should study harder

c. I think you ought to get some rest

d. what if you go for a walk?

jawaban: c

2. Linda is working on her homework, but she finds some difficult questions. What will you suggest her?

a. I think you should stop studying.

b. maybe you can sleep now.

c. What if you drink to refresh yourself?

d. what about going to your friend's house to study together?

jawaban: d

3. A: .............................. go to the cinema and watch a good film?

B: That's a good idea

a. you must

b. you have

c. I think you should

d. what if we

jawaban: d

4. A: I feel drowsy.

B: ........................................

a. you should take a rest.

b. what if you study harder?

c. what about calling your friend for help?

d. you had better call the police.

jawaban: a

5. This is Saturday night. What will you suggest your friend?

a. I think we should go to the town square for sight seeing.

b. what about going to the doctor?

c. you should speak to your teacher.

d. what if we take this to be repaired?

jawaban: a

6. Doctor: ...................... smoking if you don't want to get sick.

Patient: Yes sir, I will try.

a. what if you stop

b. you should stop

c. how about

d. maybe you can stop

jawaban: b

7. A: I am so hungry.

B: .......................................

a. I think you should take a rest.

b. You ought to read it more carefully.

c. Calm down! You should be more relaxed.

d. What about going to a restaurant?

jawaban: d

8. Mother: I want to cook an omelette.

You: ..............................

a. Let's buy some eggs.

b. Let's buy some syrup.

c. I think you should buy a ticket first.

d. I think you should go to a barbershop.

jawaban: a

9. Your friend needs a book for his homework. But he doesn't have enough money to buy it. What will you suggest him?

a. Maybe you can borrow from our seniors?

b. I think you shouldn't do the homework.

c. What if we go to the zoo?

d. You ought to get some rest.

jawaban: a

10. You and your friends are on a trip to a beach in Banyuwangi. However, none of you knows about the route the that beach. What will be your suggestion?

a. I think we can go home now.
