7. Apa kegunaan Past future tense dalam bahasa inggris


Simple Past Future Tense dibentuk dengan dua tipe, pertama yaitu ‘would’ dan bare infinitive (bentuk simpel dari verb). Tipe kedua dibentuk dari ‘was/were’, present participle ‘going’ dan infinitive. Infinitive yaitu suatu kata yang terdiri dari to + bare infinitive. Tipe kedua lebih sering digunakan untuk menyatakan aksi yang telah direncanakan.

14. perbedaan antara present tense past tense and future tense dalam bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

if the present tense is used for events that are usually done or can be called 'habits', if past tense is used for activities that have occurred in the past,

while future tense is used for something that has not yet happened but has been planned, so you could say ideals.

Jawaban:

simple present tense

Used to show facts, habits, and general conditions that occur at this time.

formula: S + V / -s, es + complement

The time signal word for this tense is usually, always, every, sometimes, normally, seldom, etc.

Simple Past Tense

Used to indicate an event in the past.

formula: S + V2 + complement

The said time signals for this tense are last year, last month, yesterday, in 1996, last Sunday, etc.

Simple Future Tense

Used to indicate an action or a situation in the future.

formula: S + will / be going to + V1 + complement

The said time signal for this tense is next, tomorrow, five days later, etc.

22. Buat fungsi simple past tense dan future tense, rumusnya ,contoh kalimatnya (+, _, ?) Dan contoh soal pilihan ganda 4 (2 past tense, 2 future tense)​


Jawaban:

Simple past tense untuk membicarakan aksi yang terjadi pada satu waktu tertentu di masa lampau

future tense untuk membicarakan aksi yang terjadi pada satu waktu tertentu di masa yang akan datang.

rumus:

simple past

+ ) s + v2+ O.

-) s + did + v1 + O

?) did + s +v1 + O ?.

simple future

+ ) s + will/shall +v1 + O

- ) s + will/shall+not+v1+O

? ) will/shall+s+v1+O ?.

contoh :

simple past tense

+ ) i ate rice , yesterday( saya makan nasi kemarin)

-) i did eat rice, yesterday(saya tidak makan nasi kemarin)

? ) did i eat rice,yesterday ?.(apakah saya makan nasi kemarin?)

simple future

+ ) i will eat rice tomorrow ( aku akan makan nasi besok)

- ) i wil not eat rice tomorrow(aku tidak akan makan nasi besok)

? ) will i eat rice tomorrow ?.(akankan aku makan nasi besok?)

note: untuk penggunaan "will" ketika subjek kalimat berbentuk (i,you,we,they,she,he,it,dan nama orang lebih dari satu nama ex: doni and riza)

untuk penggunaan shall (I,she,he,it,dan nama orang tunggal ex : doni )

23. tolong buatkan cerita bahasa inggris yang mengandung simple future tense,simple past future tense,dan present continous tense


Explanation:

- Simple future tenseThis tense is used to tell about an action that will be happened in the future.The structure: S + will + V1 + O
For example, I will go to Bali next month.

- Simple past future tense
This tense is used to indicate an action which would be happened in the past. This tense is mostly used to show wish or hope.
The structure: S + would + V1 + O
For example, I wish he would forgive me.

- Present continuous tenseThis tense is used to tell about something that is in progress right now.
The structure: S + to be (am / is / are) + V-ing + O
For example, She is reading a book in the library.

34. contoh kalimat simple past,past continuous,past perfect,past p.continus,future, future continuous, future perfect,f.p cont,past future,p.f.continouse,past perfect continuous,​


Here are examples of each tense:

Simple Past:

I watched a movie last night.She cooked dinner for us yesterday.They visited their grandparents on the weekend.

Past Continuous:

I was studying when the phone rang.He was playing football while it was raining.We were talking about our plans for the weekend.

Past Perfect:

She had already finished her homework before dinner.They had visited the museum before it closed.He had gone to the store before it started raining.

Past Perfect Continuous:

She had been studying for three hours before taking a break.They had been waiting for the bus for half an hour before it arrived.He had been working on the project all day before submitting it.

Future:

I will go to the beach next weekend.She will visit her family during the holidays.They will attend the concert next month.

Future Continuous:

I will be studying for my exams tomorrow.He will be working on his presentation all morning.They will be traveling to Europe next year.

Future Perfect:

She will have finished her project by the end of the week.They will have graduated from college in a few months.He will have completed the course before starting the new job.

Future Perfect Continuous:

She will have been studying English for two years by next summer.They will have been living in the city for five years by next month.He will have been working at the company for ten years by his retirement.

Past Future:

She said she would help me with my project tomorrow.They promised they would call me later in the evening.He told me he would come to the party next week.

Past Future Continuous:

She said she would be studying all night for the exam.They promised they would be working on the project until it's finished.He told me he would be traveling for the next two weeks.

1. Simple Present

(Active):

(Passive):

- Their friends must be invited to the party.

- The clients must be invited for a business meeting.

(Active):

- She must have invited him to the wedding.

- The company must have invited them to the event.

(Passive):

- He must have been invited to the wedding by her.

- They must have been invited to the event by the company.

3. Future

(Active):

- You must invite everyone to the charity event tomorrow.

- We must invite our neighbors to the block party next week.

(Passive):

- Everyone must be invited to the charity event tomorrow.

- Our neighbors must be invited to the block party next week.
