How to Make Fried Rice

Ingredients:

- 1 plate of cooked rice

- 2 eggs

- 1 teaspoon of salt

- 2 medium onions, chopped

- 1 cloves of garlic, chopped

- Chili powder (adjust based on your spicy level)

- 1 tablespoon of tomato sauce

- 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

Utensils:

- Frying pan

- Stove

- Spoon

- Spatula

- Plate

Steps of how to cook fried rice:

1. Heat a medium frying pan and pour the vegetable oil.

2. After it’s heated enough, add the garlic, onion, and chili.

3. Stir-fry the ingredients until it’s tender.

4. Add the eggs and scramble it until half cooked.

5. Pour the cooked rice, then keep stir for a minute.

6. Next, add the seasoning such as the sauce, pepper, and salt, and mix them all with the rice.

7. After it’s well cooked, take the fried rice and serve on the plate.

Procedure text on How to make a Jasuke (Jagung Susu Keju) or Corn, Milk and Cheese:

Ingredient:

Corn kernelsA tabelspoon butter and margarineSweetened condensed milkCheddar Cheese

Step by step instructions:

First of all, you have to steam the corn until it cooked completely. Meanwhile, you grate the cheddar cheese. When the corn is cooked, you then mix it with the butter or if you like, with a margarine and little salt. After that pour the corn into the prepared cup and add the milk, while you can sprinkle it with the cheese. Finally the delicious jasuke is ready.

Pembahasan

Di dalam soal di atas kita diminta untuk membuat sebuah procedure text (teks prosedur) tentang bagaimana cara membuat makanan ringan yang sangat lezat, yaitu jasuke atau singkatan dari jagung susu keju. Di dalam sebuah teks prosedur bahasa atau tenses yang digunakan adalah simple present tense, jadi penting kamu ingat untuk selalu menggunakan simple present tense setiap kali membuat sebuah teks prosedur. Di dalam sebuah procedure text ada 3 bagian utama yaitu bagian ingredients (bahan-bahan), bagian tools (alat-alat) bila ada, dan bagian steps (langkah-langkah), sedangkan di dalam teks dalam jawaban di atas hanya ada dua bagian yaitu ingredients dan steps.

5. procedure text how to make kebab


How to make kebab
*Makes four patties which are 20 centimeter (7.9 in) x 5 cm (8" x 2").
*500-700g (around 1 1/4 lb) ground/minced meat; traditionally this dish is made with lamb meat mixed with fat (preferably from the lamb tail) in proportion of 1:5; you can also use half beef, half mutton or just beef
*1 onion
*1 red sweet pepper (or substitute dried red pepper flakes for a more traditional and spicier variation)
*1/2 tbsp coriander
*1/2 tbsp cumin
*1 tbsp butter
*1 tbsp biber (Turkish red pepper paste)
*A little bit olive oil
*Salt and pepper
@Steps
1.Put the minced meat in a bowl. If you have half beef, half mutton then mix it a bit.
2.Cut the butter and the onion into small pieces and add them to the minced meat. Add also the coriander, cumin and biber.
3.Cut the sweet pepper into a few big chunks and use an immersion blender to puree the sweet pepper. If you have no immersion blender you can also cut the sweet pepper into very small pieces.
4.Add the puree to the minced meat.
5.Add salt and pepper.
6.Knead the minced meat.
7.Divide the minced meat into four equal sized, rectangle shaped kebabs.
If you are using the long skewer, you may not need to divide the meat. You would make a long patty to put on the skewer, as shown.
8.Cover the kebabs with olive oil.
Grill the kebabs in an oven, electric indoor grill or on an outdoor grill. With the oven it takes about 15 minutes, with an electric indoor grill it takes about 5 minutes.

How to make an omelette

Ingredients

2 eggs Onion, roughly chopped - 2 tablespoons Paprika, cut into squares - 2 tablespoons Tomato. cut into boxes - 2 tablespoons Champignon mushroom, thinly sliced ​​- 2 pieces Sausage, thinly sliced ​​- 2 pieces Mozzarella or cheddar cheese melts quickly, cut into squares - 1 tablespoon Cheddar cheese, grated - 1 tablespoonLiquid milk - 3 tablespoons Salt - 1/2 tsp Pepper - 1/4 tsp Margarine, for sauteing - 2 tablespoons

The steps

In a container, beat the eggs with grated cheese, liquid milk, salt, and pepper until they are even. Set aside. Heat margarine. Saute onions, peppers, mushrooms, and sausages. Cook until fragrant bombai and cooked mushrooms. Flatten the ingredients throughout the pan. Reduce the fire. Pour the beaten eggs and cook until half cooked. Add the tomatoes and checkered cheese. Fold the omelette into a half circle shape. Cover the pan briefly and cook until the cheese has melted. Lift. Ready to be served.

TIPS

Omelette can be created with filling according to taste, such as smoked meat, shredded chicken, and spinach. Use a non-stick pan, so you can make perfectly round omelets that are evenly cooked. Make sure the bottom or outside of the omelette is rather dry so that it doesn't break when folded.

Procedure Text

Tujuan dari teks prosedur adalah untuk memberitahu pembaca bagaimana melakukan atau membuat sesuatu melalui urutan tindakan atau langkah-langkah.

“The purpose of procedure text is to tell the reader how to do or make something through a sequence of actions or steps.”

Generic Structure of Procedure Text

Procedure text memiliki generic structure. Berikut ini generic structure of procedure text:

Goal: memberikan informasi tentang maksud dan tujuan prosedur dan memprediksi suatu kesimpulan.  (provides information about the purpose and objectives of the procedure and predicts a conclusion)Materials: berisi daftar materi atau bahan-bahan yang dibutuhkan untuk melakukan suatu prosedur atau langkah-langkah.  (contains a list of materials or materials needed to carry out a procedure or steps)Steps: daftar urutan instruksi/aktivitas untuk mencapai tujuan dalam urutan langkah yang benar.  (list the sequence of instructions / activities to reach the destination in the correct sequence of steps)

Language features of Procedure Text

Mengunakan Simple Present Tense (S+V1) Berbentuk imperative/bentuk perintah, Menggunakan action verbs, contoh: make, take, boil, cook, Menggunakan temporal conjunctions, contoh: First, then, next, after that, last

Ingredients:
300 grams of bulled rice
1 sheet of pandanus leaves (20cm)
1 tbsp of grated turmeric
350 cc of coconut milk
2 stem (@ 10 cm) of Serai (Citronella) then bruised
3 pieces of salam leaves (laurellike leaf used in cooking)
2 pieces of lime leaves
Salt

How to create:

Washed the bulled rice and drained, and then steamed with pandanus leaves until half-cooked during 20 - 30 minutes then lift and move to the bowl.Meanwhile, mixed the grated turmeric with 1 tbsp of coconut milk, and then filtered. Then Mix with coconut milk then entered the stem of Serai (Citronella), Salam leaves, lime leaves and salt. And cook using medium fire until boiled, while stirred to avoid break of coconut milk, continue cooking for 2 - 3 minutes, then lift from the fire.Pour piecemeal of coconut milk seasoned into the bowl which containing rice, stir well until evenly mixed till the coconut milk gone then move into the boiler and re-steam the rice until cooked.

How to Make Instant Noodle: Pop Mie Goreng

Materials and Tools:

1 cup of Pop Mie Goreng

350 cc of hot water (about three cups)

A pair of scissors

Cooking steps:

First, open the lid of the Pop Mie Goreng cup halfway.

Second, remove all the seasoning packs and included fork from the cup.

Third, pour the hot water into the noodle cup. If you do not have any hot water, boil some water first.

Fourth, close the lid back to the cup. Then let it sit for 3 minutes.

After 3 minutes are up, drain water completely from the cup.

Next, open the seasoning packs by using scissors or tearing them. Then add the seasoning to the noodle. You can also add some chopped sausage if you want.

Last, stir the noodle by using included fork until the noodle and seasoning are well combined.

Finally, a cup of stir fry Pop Mie noodle is ready to serve.

19. text procedure dari how to make soup


How To Make Chicken Soup
Ingredients :

1 (3 pound) whole chicken     
 4 carrots, halved
4 stalks celery, halved
1 large onion, halved
 water to cover salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules



1.Directions Put the chicken, carrots, celery and onion in a large soup pot and cover with cold water. Heat and simmer, uncovered, until the chicken meat falls off of the bones (skim off foam every so often).
2.Take everything out of the pot. Strain the broth. Pick the meat off of the bones and chop the carrots, celery and onion. Season the broth with salt, pepper and chicken bouillon to taste, if desired. Return the chicken, carrots, celery and onion to the pot, stir together, and serve.

26. procedure texthow to make kite​


jawaban:

1.) prepare materials: scissors, glue,twine, ribbon, two bamboo Dowels,tape, paper,Rope/kenur rope/glass thread.

2.) Fold the paper in half diagonally. Fold the paper neatly and open it again.

3.) Make a frame. Place the shorter piece of bamboo on the fold of the paper, then tape it. The bamboo frame must be right at the corners of the paper.

4.) Install the second frame. Take a longer piece of bamboo and tape one end to the corner of the paper that has not been framed. The short bamboo must be taped all over, but the long bamboo only needs to be taped at the ends.

5.) Cut the ribbon. Glue the ribbon to the kite. Attach the ribbon following the same line as the short frame. The ribbon will create a colorful tail and help the kite fly.[1]

6.)Tie a rope, kenur, or glass thread. Tie the string to one of the curved sides of the frame. Once the glue on the ribbon is dry, the kite is ready to fly. Tie the remaining string around the cardboard tube used for rolled tissue to make it easier for you to roll and unroll.

artinya:

1.) siapkan bahan: gunting, lem, benang, pita, dua buah pasak bambu, selotip, kertas,Tali/kenur/benang gelasan.

2.) Lipat kertas menjadi dua secara diagonal. Lipat kertas dengan rapi dan buka kembali.

3.) Buatlah bingkai. Letakkan potongan bambu yang lebih pendek pada lipatan kertas, lalu rekatkan. Bingkai bambu harus berada tepat di sudut kertas.

4.) Pasang frame kedua. Ambil sebatang bambu yang lebih panjang dan rekatkan salah satu ujungnya ke sudut kertas yang tidak dibingkai. Bambu pendek harus dipasang seluruhnya, sedangkan bambu panjang hanya perlu dipasang di bagian ujungnya saja.

5.) Potong pitanya. Rekatkan pita ke layang-layang. Pasang pita mengikuti garis yang sama dengan bingkai pendek. Pita akan membuat ekor berwarna-warni dan membantu layang-layang terbang.

6.) Ikat tali, kenur, atau benang kaca. Ikat tali ke salah satu sisi bingkai yang melengkung. Setelah lem pada pita mengering, layang-layang siap diterbangkan. Ikat sisa benang pada tabung karton yang digunakan untuk gulungan tisu agar memudahkan Anda menggulung dan membuka gulungannya.

- Agar-Agar White 1 Pack
- Milk 400 Ml Liquid
- 50 gr Dark Cooking Chocolate, Chopped
- 2 Tablespoons Granulated Sugar
- 1 Egg Yolk, Beaten
- Egg Whites 3 Eggs 

Tools:Pan, Bowl, Stove, Spoon, Mold, Refrigerator, Water
Steps:

Second, take a little dough, then mix with the egg yolks, pour back into the hot mixture. Cook until boiling. Lift.
