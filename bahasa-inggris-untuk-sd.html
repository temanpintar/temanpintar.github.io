I am honored today to talk to you all regarding an important issue that we are facing right now. That issue has a lot to do with our environment. As you all know, we are now facing the greatest environmental crisis that the Earth has ever seen since the era of Ice Age. We are on the verge of mass extinction if were to take no further action. But I am telling you right now, Ladies and Gentlemen, that we could start the change now within ourself. We could start by reducing our own small share to the pollution and start giving back to the Earth by planting trees on our backyard. We could start small, for a big resolution.

(Namun saya beritahu sekarang, Tuan dan Nyonya, bahwa kita bisa memulai perubahan sekarang dari dalam diri kita sendiri. Today, I would like to talk about the importance of keeping our environment clean and how it can benefit us all. I know that you, as elementary school students, have already learned about the importance of cleanliness and the impact it has on our health and well-being. However, I believe that we can do even more to keep our environment clean and healthy.

Firstly, keeping our environment clean can prevent the spread of diseases. By throwing away trash in the proper bins and washing our hands regularly, we can reduce the number of germs and bacteria that can make us sick. This is especially important for us students who spend most of our time in school, surrounded by many other people.

Secondly, a clean environment can create a more pleasant and welcoming atmosphere. When we see a clean and well-maintained environment, we feel more comfortable and relaxed. This can help us to focus better on our studies and other activities.

Lastly, keeping our environment clean is important for the future. We need to ensure that our environment is healthy and sustainable for generations to come. By taking small steps now, such as reducing plastic waste or planting trees, we can help to create a better future for ourselves and our planet.

In conclusion, I urge all of us to take responsibility for our environment and do our part to keep it clean and healthy. Let's work together to create a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable future for all of us.

Thank you for listening!

32. tuliskan cerita tentang kegiatan sehari sehari dalam bahasa inggris dan artinya (untuk anak SD)


Daily Activity at SchoolI study at school started at 7.30 am. This is Monday and I have a mathematic class. While I wait for the teacher, I have a short daily conversation with some friends. Then, no longer after that, the chairman of the class asks us to line up in front because the teacher has come. After that, my friends and I enter the class one by one and greet to the teacher.Our teacher asks to submit the homework. So I submit my homework. After that, the teacher asks me to open page 23 and read the book aloud. After reading the passage, I am asked to explain what I have read in front of the class. Then the teacher explains her subject further. Then we write her explanation on our books. After finishing all the lessons, we come home at 12.00 am.      Artinya:Kegiatan Sehari-Hari di SekolahSaya belajar di sekolah mulai pada pukul 7:30. Ini adalah hari Senin dan saya memiliki kelas matematika. Sementara saya menunggu guru datang, saya bercakap-cakap pendek dengan beberapa teman. Kemudian, tidak lama setelah itu, ketua kelas meminta kami semua untuk berbaris di depan karena guru telah datang.
