8. [PROVERBS] - Bahasa Inggris Peminatan​


EXPLANATION

Proverb atau Peribahasa adalah sekumpulan petuah atau wejangan bagaimana dalam menjalani kehidupan yang bermakna. Pada umumnya proverb menggunakan majas/bahasa kiasan di balik makna sebenarnya.

BERDASARKAN SOAL YG DITANYA

1) Every cloud has a silver lining

Meaning : You should never feels hopeless because difficult times are always followed by better days

Badai pasti berlalu. Hari baik pasti tiba di balik kesulitan yang ada.

2) Money is the root of all evil

Meaning : All wrongdoing is because of wanting wealth

Menghalalkan segala cara selalu ditempuh bagi mereka yang ingin cepat kaya.

3) When in Rome do as the Romans do

Meaning : When visiting a foreign land, follow the customs of those who live in it.

Pintar-pintarlah beradaptasi di manapun langkah berpijak

4) Beggars can't be choosers

Meaning : People who are in great need must accept any help that is offered, even it's a not complete solution to their problems.

Jangan menolak kesempatan yang ada jika tidak ada pilihan yang lain

5) Beauty is in the eye of the beholder

Meaning : Different people will find different things beautiful and the differences of opinion don't matter too much

Setiap orang memiliki tujuan masing-masing.

6) A barking dog seldom bites

Meaning : Those who make loud threats seldom carry them out

Tong kosong bunyinya nyaring. Orang yang suka menggertak, nihil praktiknya.

7) Don't put all your eggs in one basket

Meaning : You should not focus all resources on one hope or possibility, because it is too risky.

Jangan mengerahkan semuanya pada satu harapan jika tidak ingin kecewa nantinya.

8) Too many cooks spoil the broth

Meaning : If too many persons are managing an activity, they can easily ruin it.

Jika terlalu banyak yang ikut campur dalam menyelesaikan pekerjaan, jangan harap pekerjaan tersebut berjalan lancar.

9) A leopard can not change its spots

Meaning : One can't change one's essential nature

Jika sudah menjadi bagian dari karakternya akan sulit untuk berubah

10) Curiosity killed the cat

Meaning : This proverb is used to warn against being too curious lest one should suffer harm

Terlalu penasaran akan sesuatu membahayakan diri sendiri

12. Diketahui peminat PMR ada 1/4 bagian dari seluruh siswa. Perbandingan peminatInggris, Pencak Silat dan Seni Budaya adalah 2 : 3:6. Peminat basket adalah dua kalipeminat Bahasa Inggris. Jika jumlah siswa kelas X adalah 200 siswa, pernyataan berikutbenar adalah​


peminat pmr = 1/4 x 200 = 50 org
peminat bhs inggris : pencak silat : seni budaya = 2 : 3 : 6
peminat basket = 2 x peminat bhs inggris
maka peminat basket = 2 x 2 = 4

perbandingannya bila digabungkan:
peminat bhs inggris : pencak silat : seni budaya : basket = 2 : 3 : 6 : 4

jumlah angka perbandingannya = 2 + 3 + 6 + 4 = 15 org

jmlh siswa peminat bhs inggris, pencak silat, seni budaya, & basket = 200 - 50 = 150 (angka 50 adalah jumlah peminat PMR)

maka:
peminat bhs inggris = 2/15 x 150 = 20 org
peminat pencak silat = 3/15 x 150 = 30 org
peminat seni budaya = 6/15 x 150 = 60 org
peminat basket = 4/15 x 150 = 40 org

4) Everyone has a chance to shine in the end. [ 10. Everyday has its day]

5) It is easier to find fault in other people than do it properly [ 9. Everyone can find fault, few can do better ]

6) Most of the people who talk a lot and are always teaching others is usually do not do much work [ 2. Great talkers are little doers ]

7) One who doesn't get what he needs is frustrated person and will lose their temper easily [ 6. A hungry man is an angry man]

8) People are not grateful for they have until they lose it. [ 5. We never miss the water until the well runs dry]

9) You can not only depend on divine help but must do something yourself to get what you want [4. God helps to those who help themselves]

10) You're never too old to change your way. Learning is always possible. [3. It's never too late to mend]

EXPLANATION

Proverb atau Peribahasa adalah sekumpulan petuah atau wejangan bagaimana dalam menjalani kehidupan yang bermakna. Pada umumnya proverb menggunakan majas/bahasa kiasan di balik makna sebenarnya.

BERDASARKAN SOAL YG DITANYA

1) A friend in need is a friend indeed

Meaning : A person who helps you when you really need help is a true friend.

[Teman sejati adalah seseorang yang selalu ada ketika kamu membutuhkannya]

2) Great talkers are little doers

Meaning : Most of the people who talk a lot and are always teaching others is usually do not do much work

[Tong kosong bunyinya nyaring. Orang-orang yang banyak ngomong dan selalu mendikte orang lain biasanya praktiknya nihil]

3) It's never too late to mend

Meaning : You're never too old to change your way. Learning is always possible.

[Nggak ada kata terlambat untuk belajar dan memperbaiki diri menjadi lebih baik. Bahwa sesungguhnya manusia dilahirkan untuk terus belajar selama hidupnya.

4) God helps those who help themselves

Meaning : You can not only depend on divine help but must do something yourself to get what you want.

[Jangan hanya mengandalkan bola datang, berusahalah mengejarnya sampai dapat. Bahwa sesungguhnya Allah meridhoi orang-orang yang mau bekerja keras atas rezeki mereka]

5) We never miss the water until the well runs dry

Meaning : People are not grateful for they have until they lose it.

[Orang yang tidak pernah bersyukur, akan selalu merasa kurang dan kurang sampai pada suatu masa mereka menyadari kehilangannya]

6) A hungry man is an angry man

Meaning : One who doesn't get what he needs is frustrated person and will lose their temper easily.

[Seseorang yang tidak bisa mendapatkan apa yang diinginkan akan menjadi frustasi dan bahkan hilang akal sehatnya]

7) It takes two to make a quarrel

Meaning : An argument is never only one person's fault.

[Lihatlah sisi yang lain sebelum menilai kesalahan orang]

8) It's no use crying over split milk

Meaning : Don't be upset about making a mistake, since you cannot undo.

[Nasi telah menjadi bubur, kesalahan yang telah diperbuat, tidak dapat disesali, namun kita memiliki kesempatan untuk memperbaikinya ke depannya]

9) Everyone can find fault, few can do better.

Meaning : It is easier to find fault in other people than do it properly

[Lebih gampang mencari kesalahan orang lain daripada berusaha mencairkan situasinya]

10) Everyday has its day

Meaning : Everyone has a chance to shine in the end.

[Bumi terus berputar, setiap orang memiliki kesempatan emas. Kehidupan seseorang terus berubah seiring berputarnya waktu]

Once upon a time, on the north coast of Sumatera lived a poor woman and his son, who called Malin Kundang. His Father eventually died, and his mother lived alone as a poverty-stricken old woman.

Malin Kundang grew up as a skillful young boy. He always helps his mother to earn some money. One day, Malin Kundang decide going to overseas and promise to come back.

After several years had gone by, Malin Kudang finally decide to return to his village. He arrived wearing fine clothes and traveling on one of his ships. Someone arriving in such splendor was uncommon to the villagers, so many of them went down to the harbor to view the sight.

One of the villagers recognized Malin Kundang form a scar that he had received while playing as a child. Upon recognizing the mark, the villager went to tell Malin Kundang's mother that her son had returned. Excitedly, she went to the shore and recognized her son the minute her eyes fell on him.

In an alternate translation, Malin Kundang was on the ship with just his crew. When his mother attempted to embrace him, he was too embarrassed by her ragged appearance to acknowledge her and instead, had one of them carry her away.

Distraught and finally realizing he son's wickedness, Malin Kundang's mother gave up on her son's acknowledging her and prayed to her god to punish her son for his behavior.

The day after his mother's prayer, Malin Kudang sailed out of the village. Shortly thereafter, the ships was met by a violent storm. Malin Kundang believed the storm was his god's and nature's ways of punishing him for his mistreatment of his mother.

38. Diketahui peminat PMR ada 1/4 bagian dari seluruh siswa. Perbandingan peminat Bahasa Inggris, Pencak Silat dan Seni Budaya adalah 2 : 3 : 6. Peminat basket adalah dua kali peminat Bahasa Inggris. Jika jumlah siswa kelas X adalah 200 siswa, pernyataan berikut benar kecuali ….


Saya hitungkan jumlah peminat masing-masing saja yaa ..., karena di soal tidak ada pilihan pernyataan ...

Jumlah siswa = 200 orang

Peminat PMR = ¼ dari seluruh siswa = ¼ x 200 = 50 orang

Bhs Inggris : Pencak Silat : Seni Budaya = 2 : 3 : 6

Peminat Basket adalah dua kali peminat Bahasa Inggris. Maka, jika digabungkan :

Bhs Inggris : Pencak Silat : Seni Budaya : Basket = 2 : 3 : 6 : 4

Jumlah angka perbandingan peminat Bhs Inggris, Pencak Silat, Seni Budaya, & Basket = 2 + 3 + 6 + 4 = 15.

Jumlah siswa peminat Bhs Inggris, Pencak Silat, Seni Budaya, & Basket = 200 - 50 = 150 (angka 50 adalah jumlah peminat PMR.

Maka :

Peminat Bhs Inggris = 2/15 x 150 = 20 orang

Peminat Pencak Silat = 3/15 x 150 = 30 orang

Peminat Seni Budaya = 6/15 x 150 = 60 orang

Peminat Basket = 4/15 x 150 = 40 orang
