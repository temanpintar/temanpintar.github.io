-5m+ n = 9
4m+ n = 27
-9m = -18
m = 2

-10 + n = 9
n = 9+10
n = 19

m+n = 2 + 19 = 21
Fungsi

f(x)=mx+c

f(-5)=-5m+c=9
f(4) = 4m+c=27
------------ (-)
-9m. =-18
m. =-18/-9
m. =2

4m+c=27
4(2)+c=27
8+c=27
c=27-8
c=19

atau

-5m+c=9
-5(2)+c=9
-10+c=9
c=9+10
c=19

f(x)=mx+c
=2x+19

m+c=2+19=21

Catatan:
Pada soal m+n seharusnya m+c
