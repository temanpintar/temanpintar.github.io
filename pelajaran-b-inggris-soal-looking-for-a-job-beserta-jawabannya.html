11. 2. I’m learning Spanish. I started learning spanish since December. 3. Ann is looking for a job. She began looking six months ago. She ............................ for six months.


3. Ann has been looking for a job for six months.

Pilihan yang tepat untuk melengkapi kalimat tersebut adalah "has been looking". Kalimat yang lengkap akan menjadi: "Ann has been looking for a job for six months."

Penggunaan "has been looking" menunjukkan bahwa Ann telah melakukan tindakan mencari pekerjaan selama enam bulan dan masih berlanjut hingga sekarang. "For" digunakan untuk menunjukkan durasi waktu yang telah berlalu sejak dimulainya tindakan tersebut.

rumus present continuous tense

(+) S+ to be (am/is/are) + V-ing + O

(-) S+ to be (am/is/are) + not + V-ing + O

(?) to be(am/is/are) + S + V-ing + O

jadi

Shilpi is looking for a suitable match for her dress

21. "i'm looking forward to getting your reply soon" looking forward has the similar meaning to.... a. waiting for b. seekong for c. looking at d. looking after e. looking for


Jawaban:

A. Waiting for

Berdasarkan arti:

"i'm looking forward to getting your reply soon"

= "Saya menantikan balasanmu segera"

looking forward, has the similar meaning to....

= looking forward, memiliki arti yang mirip dengan ....

a. waiting for = menunggu ✅

b. seeking for = mencari

c. looking at = melihat ke

d. looking after = merawat

e. looking for = mencari

Kesimpulan:

• yg ditanyakan adalah similar meaning (arti yg mirip)

✅ looking forward = menanti

✅ waiting for = menunggu

• maka Jawaban adalah A karena arti keduanya MIRIP yaitu sama MENANTI/MENUNGGU
