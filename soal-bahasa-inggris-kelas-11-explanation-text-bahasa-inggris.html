1. explanation text tentang pelangi dalam bahasa inggris


A rainbow is a meteorological phenomenon that is caused by reflection, refraction and dispersion of light in water droplets resulting in a spectrum of light appearing in the sky.It takes the form of a multicoloured circular arc. Rainbows caused by sunlight always appear in the section of sky directly opposite the sun.

Rainbows can be full circles. However, the observer normally sees only an arc formed by illuminated droplets above the ground,j and centered on a line from the sun to the observer's eye.

In a primary rainbow, the arc shows red on the outer part and violet on the inner side. This rainbow is caused by light being refractedwhen entering a droplet of water, then reflected inside on the back of the droplet and refracted again when leaving it.

In a double rainbow, a second arc is seen outside the primary arc, and has the order of its colors reversed, with red on the inner side of the arc. This is caused by the light being reflected twice on the inside of the droplet before leaving it.

10. contoh explanation text tentang banana oatmeal cookies dalam bahasa Inggris ​


Jawaban:

Banana Oatmeal Cookies

Banana oatmeal cookies are a delicious and healthy treat that you can enjoy for breakfast or as a snack. They are packed with nutrients and have a soft, chewy texture that is sure to satisfy your cravings.

To make banana oatmeal cookies, you will need the following ingredients:

- 2 ripe bananas, mashed

- 1 cup rolled oats

- 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup

- 1/4 cup melted coconut oil

- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

- 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

- 1/4 teaspoon salt

- Optional add-ins: chocolate chips, raisins, nuts

To start, preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. In a large mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, melted coconut oil, honey or maple syrup, and vanilla extract. Mix well until everything is evenly combined.

Next, add the rolled oats, cinnamon, and salt to the banana mixture. Stir until all the dry ingredients are fully incorporated. If you like, this is also the time to add any optional add-ins like chocolate chips or raisins for extra flavor.

Using a spoon or cookie scoop, drop spoonfuls of the cookie dough onto the prepared baking sheet. Flatten each cookie slightly with the back of the spoon.

Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes, or until they turn golden brown around the edges. Keep an eye on them, as baking time may vary depending on your oven.

Once baked, remove the cookies from the oven and let them cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely. This will allow the cookies to set and become chewy.

Now, your delicious banana oatmeal cookies are ready to be enjoyed! Store them in an airtight container for up to one week.

These cookies are not only tasty but also a healthier alternative to traditional cookies. The bananas provide natural sweetness, while the oats offer fiber and nutrients. You can feel good about indulging in these treats without sacrificing your health.

Title: The Process of Solar Eclipse

Introduction:

Solar eclipse is a fascinating astronomical phenomenon that occurs when the Moon passes between the Earth and the Sun, blocking the sunlight from reaching certain areas on Earth. This natural event has captivated people for centuries. In this explanation text, we will explore the process of a solar eclipse, from its initial stages to its culmination.

Structure:

1. General Explanation

2. Types of Solar Eclipse

3. Partial Solar Eclipse

4. Total Solar Eclipse

5. Conclusion

Body:

1. General Explanation:

A solar eclipse happens when the Moon aligns perfectly between the Earth and the Sun. The Moon casts its shadow on Earth, causing a temporary reduction or complete absence of sunlight in specific regions. This remarkable event occurs because of the Moon's relative size and position to the Earth and the Sun.

2. Types of Solar Eclipse:

There are two main types of solar eclipses: partial solar eclipse and total solar eclipse. These classifications depend on the alignment and positioning of the Moon, Earth, and Sun during the event.

3. Partial Solar Eclipse:

During a partial solar eclipse, the Moon partially covers the Sun's disk, creating a noticeable darkening of the sky. In this scenario, the alignment is not perfect, and only a portion of the Sun is obscured. This type of eclipse can be witnessed from specific regions on Earth, where the alignment is favorable.

4. Total Solar Eclipse:

A total solar eclipse is a rare and breathtaking phenomenon. It occurs when the Moon completely blocks the Sun, resulting in a dramatic darkening of the sky. The Moon's shadow, called the umbra, falls on a small area on Earth, known as the path of totality. Within this path, the Sun is entirely obscured, revealing the Sun's outer atmosphere, the corona, which appears as a radiant halo around the Moon. This awe-inspiring event is a once-in-a-lifetime experience for many who have the opportunity to witness it.

Conclusion:

The process of a solar eclipse involves the precise alignment of the Moon, Earth, and Sun, resulting in a temporary blocking of sunlight. This celestial event offers a remarkable display of nature's wonders, captivating observers with its beauty and rarity. Whether it's a partial or total solar eclipse, these phenomena provide a unique opportunity for scientists and enthusiasts to study and appreciate the wonders of our universe.

Note: The grammar and structure of the text have been revised to align with standard English grammar rules and conventions.

The Occurrence of Solar Eclipse

Solar eclipse is a natural occurrence that happens when the moon passes between the sun and Earth, casting its shadow on Earth's surface. This rare phenomenon can only be seen in certain locations across the world and is an extraordinary event that has fascinated people for centuries.

The formation of a solar eclipse happens when the moon, which is 400 times smaller than the sun, perfectly covers the sun's disk. This blocks the sunlight, creating a shadow on Earth's surface as the moon aligns itself between the sun and the observer's location.

There are three types of solar eclipses, namely total, partial, and annular. A total solar eclipse occurs when the moon appears to completely cover the sun, leaving only a faint, thin ring or "corona" visible around the moon's disk. Partial solar eclipses occur when the moon only partially covers the sun, leaving a crescent-shaped solar disk visible. Annular solar eclipses, on the other hand, happen when the moon is too far from Earth to completely cover the sun, thus creating a "ring of fire" effect around the sun's disk.

The occurrence of a solar eclipse is a rare and exciting event that requires proper precautions to view safely. Onlookers must utilize specially designed eclipse glasses or other similar devices to protect their eyes and avoid any permanent damage or blindness.

In conclusion, solar eclipses are remarkable marvels of nature that showcase the intricate interaction of the sun, moon, and Earth. Be sure to take the necessary precautions when observing this magnificent event in-person to ensure a safe and unforgettable experience.

The lunar eclipse is an event of the sun, the earth and the moon lie on one line so that the shadow of the earth covers a part or whole moon. The lunar eclipses that occur on this earth distinguished to several types, they are total lunar eclipse, partial lunar eclipse and lunar eclipse penumbra.

A total lunar eclipse is when the moon is right in the umbra (the darkest area). On a partial lunar eclipse, the earth is not entirely blocking the moon from the sun. On the lunar eclipse panumbra, all parts of the moon are in the penumbra. So the moon can still be seen with a gloomy color.

There is also a very rare lunar eclipse phenomenon called super total lunar eclipse red blood. This kind of lunar eclipse occurs due at the time of occurence of the total lunar eclipse.
