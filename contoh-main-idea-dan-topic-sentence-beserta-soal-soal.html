Jawaban dari Instructions: Write a topic sentence for each of the topics below by adding a main idea adalah: Example: Topic: My history professor Topic Sentence: My history professor is a very effective instructor. 1. Topic: My first day of college Topic Sentence: My first day of college was very fun. 2. Topic: Taco Bell Topic Sentence: Taco Bell is one of the famous restaurant in the world. 3. Pembahasan

Dalam penulisan  sebuah topic sentence, kita perlu tahu tentang problem-problem apa yang dapat diangkat untuk menjadi sebuah paragraf atau teks. Topic sentence juga tidak boleh melenceng jauh dari topik utama yang diberikan. Yang lebih penting adalah, penulis harus paham apa yang akan dituliskan setelah topic sentence diketahui.


Sebuah topic sentence memiliki beberapa fungsi penting: itu memperkuat atau mendukung pernyataan tesis esai; itu menyatukan isi paragraf dan mengarahkan urutan kalimat; dan itu menasihati pembaca subjek yang akan dibahas dan bagaimana paragraf akan membahasnya. Pembaca umumnya melihat ke beberapa kalimat pertama dalam paragraf untuk menentukan subjek dan perspektif paragraf. Itulah mengapa seringkali sebaiknya menempatkan topic sentence di awal paragraf. Namun, dalam beberapa kasus, lebih efektif untuk menempatkan kalimat lain sebelum topic sentence, misalnya, kalimat yang menghubungkan paragraf saat ini dengan paragraf sebelumnya, atau yang memberikan informasi latar belakang.

15. Contoh kalimat topic sentence,supporting sentence,concluding sentence


Answer (Example of topic, supporting, and concluding sentence):

1. Topic sentence:

Eating vegetables and fruits are good for your health. Fruit and vegetables are a good source of vitamins and minerals, including folate, vitamin C and potassium. Therefore, we shall include vegetable and fruits into our diet.

2. Supporting sentence:

Eating vegetables and fruits are good for your health. Fruit and vegetables are a good source of vitamins and minerals, including folate, vitamin C and potassium. Therefore, we shall include vegetable and fruits into our diet.

3. Concluding sentence:

Eating vegetables and fruits are good for your health. Fruit and vegetables are a good source of vitamins and minerals, including folate, vitamin C and potassium. Therefore, we shall include vegetable and fruits into our diet.

Explanation:

Topic Sentence (Penyata gagasan utama)

A topic sentence is a sentence that introduces a paragraph by presenting the one topic that will be the focus of that paragraph.

Supporting Sentence (Pendukung gagasan utama)

A supporting sentence is a sentence with information that supports a main idea, it gives the reader a detailed information about the main idea.

Concluding sentence (Penutup)

A concluding sentence indicates that you are bringing closure to a paragraph.

36. Jelaskan dengan lengkap tentang Main Idea dan Supporting Details beserta contohnya.


Grasping the Main Idea:
A paragraph is a group of sentences related to a particular topic, or central theme. Every paragraph has a key concept or main idea. The main idea is the most important piece of information the author wants you to know about the concept of that paragraph.
When authors write they have an idea in mind that they are trying to get across. This is especially true as authors compose paragraphs. An author organizes each paragraph's main idea and supporting details in support of the topic or central theme, and each paragraph supports the paragraph preceding it.
A writer will state his/her main idea explicitly somewhere in the paragraph. That main idea may be stated at the beginning of the paragraph, in the middle, or at the end. The sentence in which the main idea is stated is the topic sentence of that paragraph.
The topic sentence announces the general theme ( or portion of the theme) to be dealt with in the paragraph. Although the topic sentence may appear anywhere in the paragraph, it is usually first - and for a very good reason. This sentence provides the focus for the writer while writing and for the reader while reading. When you find the topic sentence, be sure to underline it so that it will stand out not only now, but also later when you review.

Pembahasan

Dalam penulisan  sebuah topic sentence, kita perlu tahu tentang problem-problem apa yang dapat diangkat untuk menjadi sebuah paragraf atau teks. Topic sentence juga tidak boleh melenceng jauh dari topik utama yang diberikan. Yang lebih penting adalah, penulis harus paham apa yang akan dituliskan setelah topic sentence diketahui.


Sebuah topic sentence memiliki beberapa fungsi penting: itu memperkuat atau mendukung pernyataan tesis esai; itu menyatukan isi paragraf dan mengarahkan urutan kalimat; dan itu menasihati pembaca subjek yang akan dibahas dan bagaimana paragraf akan membahasnya. Pembaca umumnya melihat ke beberapa kalimat pertama dalam paragraf untuk menentukan subjek dan perspektif paragraf. Itulah mengapa seringkali sebaiknya menempatkan topic sentence di awal paragraf. Namun, dalam beberapa kasus, lebih efektif untuk menempatkan kalimat lain sebelum topic sentence, misalnya, kalimat yang menghubungkan paragraf saat ini dengan paragraf sebelumnya, atau yang memberikan informasi latar belakang.
