3. tulislah rumus positive degree dalam bahasa inggris +soal!


Positive Degree : Equality Degree 
Formula : S + tobe + as adjective as + object.

e.g. :
1. my sister is as ... as my mom
a. diligent
b. more diligent
c. the most diligent

13. tuliskan 5 kata bijak dalam bahasa inggris beserta artinya dengan menggunakan : positive degree , comparative degree dan superlative degree. mksh


Answer:

- Positive degree is the same as normal sentence with adjective.
Example: he's a good student.
- Comparative degree is the sentence to compare two things.
Example: He's better than his brother.
- Superlative degree is the sentence to show the superlative expression.
Example: He's the best player in school.
The wise words in English can be in a form of an idiom, proverbs or sayings. They tend to use a simple or fancy words but memorable ones. Here are some of them:
- Better late than never (it's much better to start doing something late than not doing at all. 
- Easier said than done (it's always easy to say a solution for a problem but doing it is different kind of story)
- He laugh best who laugh last (the satisfaction and success which brings smile to us would be best if it happens in the end)
- Every man is his own worst enemy (our own chance of success could be ruined by our own fear, doubts or prejudice)
- A friend's eye is a good mirror ( a real friend will tell you the truth)

Examples :

Comparative Degree :

Examples :

Superlative Degree :

Example :

Lead is the heaviest metal. Ravi is the strongest boy in his class. Mohan is the tallest boy. India is the more powerful. She is the most beautiful girl in the class. His marks are the lowest in the class.

23. Apa itu comparative degree dalam Bahasa Inggris ?


Penjelasan:
