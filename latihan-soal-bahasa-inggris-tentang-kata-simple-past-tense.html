Jawaban:

1. (-) We did not write a letter for you yesterday.

  (?) Did we not write a letter for you yesterday?

2. (+) They did the exercise last Monday.

   (?) Did they do the exercise last Monday?

3. (+) You read the newspaper two weeks ago.

   (-) You didn't read the newspaper two weeks ago.

4. (+) Mrs. Monika closed the windows last month.

   (?) Did Mrs. Monika closed the windows last month?

5. (+) My uncle understood the lesson five days ago.

9. Apa Saja Rumus Bahasa Inggris Simple Past Tense​


Jawaban:

Rumus dari simple past tense: Subject + Verb 2 + Object.

positive (+) : subject (s) + verb 2 + object (o)

negative (-) : subject (s) + did not + infinitive tanpa to

interrogative (?)
=> positive : did + subject (s) + infinitive tanpa to

=> negative : did not + subject (s) infinitive tanpa to

13. Buat kan cerita simple past tense dalam bahasa inggris dan letak simple past tense nya. kalo bisa 10 kalimat​


Jawaban:

Cerita: A Day at the Beach

1. Yesterday, Sarah went to the beach with her family. (went)

2. They arrived early in the morning and played in the sand. (arrived, played)

3. Sarah built a sandcastle while her brother swam in the sea. (built, swam)

4. The sun was shining brightly, and the waves were gentle. (was shining, were)

5. Sarah's parents prepared a picnic lunch, and they all enjoyed it together. (prepared, enjoyed)

6. After lunch, they flew a kite and ran along the shore. (flew, ran)

7. In the afternoon, they collected seashells and took photos. (collected, took)

8. Sarah found a beautiful seashell and kept it as a souvenir. (found, kept)

9. As the evening approached, they packed their belongings and headed home. (approached, packed, headed)

10. Sarah fell asleep in the car, feeling happy about their fun day at the beach. (fell asleep, feeling)

Note: Simple past tense dalam cerita ini ditandai dengan kata kerja bentuk lampau seperti "went," "played," "built," "was shining," "prepared," "flew," "collected," "found," "approached," dan "fell asleep."

Jawaban:

Terima kasih telah bertanya kepada toto. Berikut adalah 20 kata kerja dalam bahasa Inggris dan contoh kalimatnya dalam simple past tense dan simple present tense:

eat (makan)

Simple past: I ate a sandwich for lunch. (Saya makan sandwich untuk makan siang.)

Simple present: I eat a sandwich every day. (Saya makan sandwich setiap hari.)

play (bermain)

Simple past: She played soccer with her friends. (Dia bermain sepak bola dengan teman-temannya.)

Simple present: She plays soccer every weekend. (Dia bermain sepak bola setiap akhir pekan.)

read (membaca)

Simple past: He read a book yesterday. (Dia membaca buku kemarin.)

Simple present: He reads a book every night. (Dia membaca buku setiap malam.)

write (menulis)

Simple past: They wrote a letter to their parents. (Mereka menulis surat untuk orang tua mereka.)

Simple present: They write a letter every month. (Mereka menulis surat setiap bulan.)

sing (menyanyi)

Simple past: She sang a song at the party. (Dia menyanyi sebuah lagu di pesta.)

Simple present: She sings a song every morning. (Dia menyanyi sebuah lagu setiap pagi.)

dance (menari)

Simple past: He danced with his girlfriend. (Dia menari dengan pacarnya.)

Simple present: He dances with his girlfriend every Friday. (Dia menari dengan pacarnya setiap Jumat.)

run (berlari)

Simple past: They ran a marathon last week. (Mereka berlari maraton minggu lalu.)

Simple present: They run a marathon every year. (Mereka berlari maraton setiap tahun.)

walk (berjalan)

Simple past: I walked to school this morning. (Saya berjalan ke sekolah pagi ini.)

Simple present: I walk to school every day. (Saya berjalan ke sekolah setiap hari.)

swim (berenang)

Simple past: She swam in the pool yesterday. (Dia berenang di kolam kemarin.)

Simple present: She swims in the pool every summer. (Dia berenang di kolam setiap musim panas.)

fly (terbang)

Simple past: He flew to Bali last month. (Dia terbang ke Bali bulan lalu.)

Simple present: He flies to Bali every year. (Dia terbang ke Bali setiap tahun.)

drink (minum)

Simple past: I drank a glass of water. (Saya minum segelas air.)

Simple present: I drink a glass of water every morning. (Saya minum segelas air setiap pagi.)

sleep (tidur)

Simple past: She slept for eight hours. (Dia tidur selama delapan jam.)

Simple present: She sleeps for eight hours every night. (Dia tidur selama delapan jam setiap malam.)

study (belajar)

Simple past: He studied for the exam. (Dia belajar untuk ujian.)

Simple present: He studies for the exam every week. (Dia belajar untuk ujian setiap minggu.)

work (bekerja)

Simple past: They worked at the office. (Mereka bekerja di kantor.)

Simple present: They work at the office every day. (Mereka bekerja di kantor setiap hari.)

cook (memasak)

Simple past: She cooked a delicious meal. (Dia memasak hidangan lezat.)

Simple present: She cooks a delicious meal every weekend. (Dia memasak hidangan lezat setiap akhir pekan.)

watch (menonton)

Simple past: He watched a movie with his friends. (Dia menonton film dengan teman-temannya.)

Simple present: He watches a movie with his friends every month. (Dia menonton film dengan teman-temannya setiap bulan.)

listen (mendengarkan)

Simple past: I listened to music on my phone. (Saya mendengarkan musik di ponsel saya.)

Simple present: I listen to music on my phone every day. (Saya mendengarkan musik di ponsel saya setiap hari.)

speak (berbicara)

Simple past: She spoke English with her teacher. (Dia berbicara bahasa Inggris dengan gurunya.)

Simple present: She speaks English with her teacher every week. (Dia berbicara bahasa Inggris dengan gurunya setiap minggu.)

drive (mengemudi)

Simple past: He drove his car to the mall. (Dia mengemudi mobilnya ke mal.)

Simple present: He drives his car to the mall every Saturday. (Dia mengemudi mobilnya ke mal setiap Sabtu.)

clean (membersihkan)

Simple past: They cleaned their room. (Mereka membersihkan kamar mereka.)

Simple present: They clean their room every day. (Mereka membersihkan kamar mereka setiap hari.)

28. Sebutkan rumus dari bahasa Inggris simple present tense, simple future tense, Simple Past Tense, simple past future tense, rumusnya bentuk aktif dan pasif makasih​


Jawaban:

Simple present tense

Active voice : S + V1s/es

Passive voice : S + am/is/are + verb-3

Simple Past Tense

Active voice: S + verb-2

Passive voice: S + was/were + verb-3

Simple Future Tense

Active voice: S + will + V1

Passive voice : S + will + be + verb-3

Simple Past Future Tense

Active voice: S + would + V1

Passive Voice : S + Would + be + verb-3

Pembahasan

Berikut pembahasan mengenai tenses yang digunakan dalam cerita diatas:

SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE

Simple Present Tense adalah tenses dalam bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk menyatakan habit (kebiasaan), unchanging situation, general truth dan fixed arrangement.

Bagaimana membentuk simple present tense?

Simple present tense memiliki beberapa rule:

Jika subyeknya I, we, they maka menggunakan verb-1 seperti like, want, need, love. Sedangkan bentuk negatifnya menggunakan DO+NOT.Jika subyeknya orang ketiga tunggal seperti he, she, my sister, Ana, Tommy maka menggunakan verb-1+s/es seperti likes, wants, needs, loves. Bentuk negatifnya menggunakan DOES+NOT.Keterangan waktu yang biasanya mengikuti simple present tense diantaranya always, sometimes, every day, every saturday, once a week, usually, normally dan lain sebagainya.

Contoh kalimat yang menggunakan simple present tense dalam cerita diatas adalah:

I am 15 years old.I live in Jakarta.She studies engineering in University of Indonesia.My father is a scientist.We practice twice a week after school.SIMPLE PAST TENSE

Simple past tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk kegiatan yang sudah diselesaikan pada waktu lampau. Kejadian tersebut bisa saja baru saja terjadi dan sudah selesai atau pada beberapa waktu yang lalu.

Bentuk dari Simple Past Tense

(+) Subject + Verb-2

(-) Subject + did not + infinitive without to

(?) Did + subject + infinitive without to

Contoh kalimat yang menggunakan simple past tense dalam cerita diatas adalah:

My family moved to Jakarta.At first, I only played computer games.

SIMPLE FUTURE TENSE

Simple Future Tense adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk membicarakan masa depan (future). Pola yang digunakan dalam simple future tense adalah:

Subject + Will + infinitive without to

contoh kalimat yang menggunakan simple future tense dalam cerita diatas:

I hope in the future, I will get a job as a computer scientist.I will do my best to inspire others.Pelajari Lebih Lanjut
