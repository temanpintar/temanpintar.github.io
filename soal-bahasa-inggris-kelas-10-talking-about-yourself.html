15. kesimpulan tata bahasa talking about object!!mohon bantuannya!!!​


Jawaban:

Object pronounsadalah kata ganti milik (orang atau benda) yang berfungsi sebagai “object”. Dengan kata lain “kata ganti milik yang dikenai tindakan”.

Contoh: I make a cake

Yang merupakan objek pada kalimat di di atas adalah "cake" karena "cake"-lah yang menjadi sasaran subjek untuk dibuat.

Dalam bahasa Inggris kita mengenal adanya object pronoun/kata ganti objek. Berikut merupakan kata ganti objek untuk setiap pronoun:

Subject = Object

I= me

You= You

we= us

they= them

she= her

he= him

it= it

a rabbit= it

rabbits= them

a cable= it

cables= them

Timmy= him

Jessica= her

Timmy and Rina = them

Contoh:

My mother hugs Tini.

My mother hugs her.

The chef cuts the chicken’s wing.

The chef cuts it.

She brings food to all the staff.

She brings food to them.

Handi helps his mother clean the room.

Handi helps her clean the room

23. tentang "A time you found out something about yourself"Essay yang dibuat minimal 400 kata Bahasa Inggris​


A Time You Found Out Something About Yourself

Discovering something new about ourselves is a significant milestone in our personal growth. It often occurs unexpectedly and can lead to transformative experiences. One such instance in my life stands out vividly, when I realized an essential aspect of my character and how it influenced my relationships and decision-making. This revelation occurred during a challenging period of my life, and it played a pivotal role in shaping the person I am today.

It was a cold winter evening, and I found myself sitting alone in my room, engulfed in feelings of confusion and discontent. I had been experiencing a series of failures and setbacks in both my academic and personal life. Doubts and insecurities crept into my mind, leaving me questioning my abilities and worth. It was during this introspective moment that I stumbled upon an article about the power of self-reflection. Intrigued, I decided to embark on a journey of self-discovery.

I began by engaging in introspective activities such as journaling, meditation, and engaging in open conversations with close friends. Through these exercises, I gradually unraveled the layers of my personality and identified recurring patterns in my thoughts and behaviors. However, it was a particular incident that truly unveiled a crucial aspect of my character.

One afternoon, while conversing with a friend, she made an offhand remark about how I always sought validation from others before making decisions. Intrigued by her observation, I delved deeper into this pattern and realized that I had been relying heavily on external validation to shape my choices and opinions. This revelation shook me to the core, as I had always believed myself to be an independent thinker.

Reflecting on this newfound understanding, I began to recall various instances where I had compromised my own beliefs and values to appease others. Whether it was choosing a college major to please my parents or altering my behavior to gain acceptance from a social group, I had unknowingly succumbed to the pressure of seeking external validation. This realization left me feeling vulnerable yet determined to transform my approach to decision-making.

Armed with this knowledge, I embarked on a journey of self-empowerment. I actively sought opportunities to challenge myself and make decisions based on my own convictions rather than seeking approval from others. It was a gradual process that required immense courage and self-assurance. Initially, I faced resistance from those accustomed to my previous behavior. However, as I stood firm in my choices and communicated my reasons assertively, I began to garner respect and admiration from both friends and family.

The impact of this newfound self-awareness extended beyond decision-making. It seeped into my relationships, transforming the way I interacted with others. I became more assertive in expressing my needs and boundaries, and I started attracting people who valued my authentic self. This shift in dynamics helped me cultivate deeper and more meaningful connections, as I was no longer sacrificing my identity to fit into predetermined molds.

Moreover, the newfound independence in decision-making instilled a sense of self-confidence within me. I began to trust my instincts and embrace the consequences of my choices, whether they led to success or failure. This resilience allowed me to navigate challenges with a newfound sense of clarity and determination. Instead of dwelling on failures, I saw them as stepping stones toward personal growth and self-improvement.

In conclusion, the time I discovered my tendency to seek external validation was a transformative experience that shaped my character and relationships. Through introspection and self-reflection, I unraveled this aspect of myself and embarked on a journey of self-empowerment. By embracing my authentic self and making decisions based on personal conviction, I have cultivated a stronger sense of identity, built deeper connections, and developed resilience in the face of challenges. This realization has proven invaluable in my personal growth, reminding me of the importance of self-awareness and the power of embracing one's true self.

Ceritakan pengalaman kerja secara singkat

Contoh: I have been working as a sales manager at Company Y for three years. My role is to lead the sales team. We have to make sure that we hit the sales targets. (Saya bekerja sebagai manajer penjualan di Perusahaan Y selama tiga tahun. Tugas saya adalah untuk memimpin tim penjualan. Kami harus memastikan bahwa kami mencapai target penjualan)

Berikan contoh konkret tentang kemampuan yang bisa diberikan kepada perusahaan.

Contoh: When I worked for Company S, I successfully increased sales 5% each year. I believe I can do the same for your company. (Saat saya bekerja untuk Perusahaan S, saya berhasil menaikkan penjualan 5% setiap tahun. Saya yakin saya dapat melakukan hal yang sama untuk perusahaan Anda)

Sebutkan hal-hal positif lainnya yang berhubungan dengan posisi yang dilamar

Contoh: I graduated five years ago with a Bachelor’s degree in business. I took a job at Company Z as soon as I graduated, and got promoted to be a manager three years later. (Saya lulus lima tahun yang lalu dengan gelar sarjana bisnis. Saya bekerja di Perusahaan Z segera setelah lulus, dan mendapatkan promosi menjadi manajer tiga tahun kemudian)

2. Social Situation

Tentunya menjawab “tell me about yourself?” dalam situasi sosial berbeda dengan saat melakukan wawancara kerja. Dalam situasi sosial, setelah menjawab pertanyaannya pun kita harus balik menanyakan pertanyaan yang sama kepada orang yang bertanya pada kita. Ada berbagai jawaban yang dapat digunakan untuk menjawab pertanyaan ini dalam konteks sosial. Berikut adalah jawaban tersebut:

Your Nickname

Pertanyaan “tell me about yourself?” biasanya diajukan setelah si penanya mengetahui nama kita. Si penanya memang mengetahui nama kita, tetapi ia tidak mengetahui apa nama panggilan kita. Untuk itulah kita bisa menjawab pertanyaan ini dengan memberitahu nama panggilan kita dan jika memungkinkan ceritakanlah asal-usul mengapa kita bisa mendapatkan nama panggilan tersebut. Contoh:

“My name is Arthur, but people tend to call me Legs since I have long legs. I know it is a silly nickname, but I love it. How about you? Tell me a bit about yourself.” (Nama saya Arthur, tetapi orang-orang cenderung memanggil saya Legs karena saya memiliki kaki yang panjang. Saya tahu itu adalah nama panggilan yang konyol, tetapi saya menyukainya. Bagaimana dengan Anda? Ceritakan sedikit tentang Anda pada saya)

Where You Live and Come from

“Tell me about yourself?” juga bisa dijawab dengan menyebutkan di mana kita tinggal dan dari mana kita berasal. Jawaban ini dipakai terutama apabila kita adalah orang baru. Berikut adalah pola yang bisa diikuti untuk menggunakan jawaban ini:

I live at + nama jalan

I live in + nama wilayah

I live near + nama gedung atau landmark terkenal

I come from + daerah asal

I am from + daerah asal

Contoh:

I come from Makassar, and now I live at Y Street. (Saya dari Makassar, dan sekarang saya tinggal di Jalan Y)

I am from England, but right now I live near the cinema in the downtown. (Saya berasal dari Inggris, tetapi sekarang saya tinggal dekat dengan bioskop di pusat kota)

I am from Lombok. Right now I live in West Jakarta. (Saya berasal dari Lombok. Saat ini saya tinggal di Jakarta Barat)
