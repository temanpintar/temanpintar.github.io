How to Make Fried Rice

Ingredients:

- 1 plate of cooked rice

- 2 eggs

- 1 teaspoon of salt

- 2 medium onions, chopped

- 1 cloves of garlic, chopped

- Chili powder (adjust based on your spicy level)

- 1 tablespoon of tomato sauce

- 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

Utensils:

- Frying pan

- Stove

- Spoon

- Spatula

- Plate

Steps of how to cook fried rice:

1. Heat a medium frying pan and pour the vegetable oil.

2. After it’s heated enough, add the garlic, onion, and chili.

3. Stir-fry the ingredients until it’s tender.

4. Add the eggs and scramble it until half cooked.

5. Pour the cooked rice, then keep stir for a minute.

6. Next, add the seasoning such as the sauce, pepper, and salt, and mix them all with the rice.

7. After it’s well cooked, take the fried rice and serve on the plate.

30. Lrocedure text how to make fried chicken !


How to make fried chicken crispy:

1. Create a liquid layer mixture by mixing the flour, cornstarch, milk powder, baking powder, pepper, salt and flavorings. Stir well.

2. Add egg yolk and water gradually, stir until blended. Then store in the refrigerator.

3. Make dry layer by mixing all the dry layer material. Stir well and then set aside

4. Spice up chicken meat with salt, pepper, chili that has been crushed and garlic. Then stir well. In order to the seasoning absorb well, save for 15 minutes.

5. Roll the chicken meat into the dry layer mixture until the entire surface of the meat is covered by flour.

6. Dip into a liquid layer mixture and then roll back into the dry layer. Knead the chicken meat in order to absorb well. Repeat this step up to 2 times to get crispier and tastier fried chicken.

7. Heat oil, and then fry chicken meat using medium heat.

8. Once the chicken meat is yellow-brown and dry, Remove and drain.

9. Crunchy fried chicken is ready to be served with a sauce or gravy

Jawaban:

Berikut adalah contoh procedure text how to make fried banana lengkap dengan ingredients, spices, tools, procedures:

Ingredients:

- 4 ripe bananas

- 100 grams of all-purpose flour

- 50 grams of rice flour

- 2 tablespoons of margarine, melted

- 2 tablespoons of sugar

- 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla essence

- A pinch of salt

- Water as needed

- Oil for frying

Spices:

- 2 tablespoons of powdered sugar

- 50 grams of dark chocolate, melted

Tools:

- A knife

- A cutting board

- A mixing bowl

- A whisk

- A spoon

- A frying pan

- A paper towel

- A plate

Procedures:

Peel the bananas and cut them into small pieces, about 1 centimeter thick. Set aside. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, rice flour, margarine, sugar, vanilla essence, and salt. Whisk well and gradually add water until the batter is smooth and not too thick or runny.Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Dip the banana pieces into the batter and coat them generously. Using a spoon, drop them into the hot oil and fry them for about 4 minutes or until golden brown and crispy. Turn them occasionally for even browning.Remove the fried bananas from the oil and drain them on a paper towel to remove excess oil. Transfer the fried bananas to a plate and sprinkle with powdered sugar. Drizzle with melted chocolate and enjoy.
