Yield : 6 servings

Prep. Time :5 minutes

Cook Time : 40 minutes

Total Time : 45 minutes

½ teaspoon vanilla

Bake at 35calcius for 40 minutes, or until certer is almost set. Cool.

Procedure text is a text that is designed to describe how

something is achieved through a sequence of actions or steps

Example:

How to Clean an LCD Screen

1. Shake up the detergent and put it 15 — 20 cm away from the LCD screen.

Making Compost Fertilizer

Compost is a fertilizer from the breakdown of animal and plant remains with the help of living organisms. Compost is a type of organic fertilizer.

Plants and gardens will become more fertile and make the soil more loose by using compost. Therefore, compost can be used as one of the best fertilizer choices.

Here's how to make your own compost easily:

Tool

Hoe

Gloves

Gunny sack

Shovel

Ingredients

Water

Cattle manure

Chopped straw

Leaf litter

husk charcoal

Sawdust

Sugar

EM4

Steps

First, prepare in advance the media to make compost that is safe from rain and sunlight.

Second, make a decomposer solution. The trick is to dissolve EM4 with sugar into water.

Third, make the first layer of compost. By stirring the mixture of manure and husk charcoal evenly. Then add the decomposer and mix until smooth.

Then create a second layer. Sprinkle leaf litter, sawdust, and chopped hay. Stir until smooth and water again with the decomposer.

Close tightly the fertilizer material with a burlap sack.

The next day, re-mix the ingredients until evenly distributed.

Check regularly in the morning and evening. The trick, put on gloves and put your hands into the compost mixture. If it feels hot and your hands can't handle the heat, it means that the fertilizer has not been finished and cannot be used.

Go back to check the fertilizer on the fourth day. Usually the fertilizer is ready to use on the fourth day.

If your hands are able to withstand the heat of the fertilizer mixture, it means that the fertilizer is ready to be used.

Homemade compost is finished.

18. contoh teks prosedur bahasa inggris membuat kue


How to Make Cake


Ingredients:
12 eggs
1/4 kg of butter
3/8 kg of sugar
2 sachet of vanilla
1/2 tbs of Ovalet
3 1/2 ons of wheat flour
5 tbs of milk powder
1 package of chocolate powder.
Steps:
Remove the egg yolk and albumen and place them in different bowl.
Mix the butter, sugar, vanilla, and ovalet for 10 minutes.
Put the yolk into the dough and blend again at least 10 minutes.
Pour the wheat flour little by little into the dough and stir well.
Pour the milk powder little by little into the dough and stir well.
Pour the chocolate powder little by little into the dough and stir well.
Blend the albumen around 5 minutes.
Pour the albumen into the dough. Blend them around 10 minutes.
Prepare a baking pan lubricated with butter. Pour the dough. Put into the oven and bake around 40 minutes in 25 degrees celcius.
Lift the cake and put on the plate. Spread some sugar on it. The bolu cake is ready to serve.

How to Make Scrambled Eggs

Let’s learn how to make yummy scrambled eggs at your home with this easy recipe!

Ingredients:

2 eggs

Milk (if desired)

1 teaspoon of butter

A pinch of salt and pepper

Steps:

First, crack the eggs into a small bowl and add a pinch of salt and pepper. Whisk until all well blended.

In a frying pan, add butter and let it melt.

Pour the eggs and milk, and for around 20 seconds, do not stir it.

After that, fold the eggs to the center and stir with spatula.

Repeat the previous step until all the eggs are mostly cooked

Remove eggs from heat, and keep continue to fold and stir the eggs for around a minute.

Lift the eggs, put on the serving plate and your scrambled eggs are ready.

How to Insert Sim Card Cellphone”

Cellphone is a modern communication device which connects one to the others by voice, written message and data. However this device can not work until the SIM card is inserted.

When inserting the SIM Card to cellphone, make sure that the cellphone has been switched off and follow the direction bellow :

First of all, press the locking catch and slide the cover then lift it off the phone.

After that, push two catches in the opposite directions and remove the battery.

Next, slide the SIM card carefully into the slot and make sure that the golden connect arson are facing to the connector of the phone.

Then, put the battery and align it until snaps into its place.

Finally, insert the two catches of the back cover corresponding slot in the phone and slide the cover forward button of the phone until locks into place

Don’t forget to switch on the cellphone. Wait until it is ready to use.

“Cara Memasukkan Kartu Sim ke Dalam Ponsel”

Ponsel adalah perangkat komunikasi modern yang menghubungkan orang satu dengan yang lain melalui suara, pesan tertulis dan data. Namun perangkat ini tidak bisa bekerja sampai kartu SIM dimasukkan.

Saat memasukkan Kartu SIM ke ponsel, pastikan ponsel telah dimatikan dan ikuti petunjuk di bawah ini:

Pertama-tama, tekan pengunci ponsel bagian belakang dan geser penutup lalu lepaskan penutup dari ponsel.

Setelah itu, dorong dua pengunci ke arah yang berlawanan dan lepaskan baterai.

Selanjutnya, geser kartu SIM dengan hati-hati ke dalam slot dan pastikan penyambungan sinyal yaitu bagian emas telah menghadap ke konektor ponsel.

Kemudian, letakkan baterai dan luruskan sampai terkunci pada tempatnya.

How to Make Bolu Cake.


Ingredients:

12 eggs

1/4 kg of butter

3/8 kg of sugar

2 sachet of vanilla

1/2 tbs of Ovalet

3 1/2 ons of wheat flour

5 tbs of milk powder

1 package of chocolate powder.


Steps:

1. Remove the egg yolk and albumen and place them in different bowl.

2. Mix the butter, sugar, vanilla, and ovalet for 10 minutes.

3. Put the yolk into the dough and blend again at least 10 minutes.

4. Pour the wheat flour little by little into the dough and stir well.

5. Pour the milk powder little by little into the dough and stir well.

6. Pour the chocolate powder little by little into the dough and stir well.

7. Blend the albumen around 5 minutes.

8. Pour the albumen into the dough. Blend them around 10 minutes.

9. Prepare a baking pan lubricated with butter. Pour the dough. Put into the oven and bake around 40 minutes in 25 degrees celcius.

10. Lift the cake and put on the plate. Spread some sugar on it. The bolu cake is ready to serve.

How to Make a Cup of Tea

Introduction:

Making a cup of tea is a simple and enjoyable process. Follow these step-by-step instructions to make a perfect cup of tea.

Materials:

- Tea bag or loose tea leaves

- A cup

- Boiling water

- A teaspoon

- Sugar, milk, or lemon (optional)

Procedure:

Step 1: Boil Water

1. Start by boiling a cup of fresh water. You can use a kettle or a microwave to heat the water.

Step 2: Place a Tea Bag or Tea Leaves

2. Place a tea bag in the cup or, if you prefer loose tea leaves, use a tea strainer. If you're using a tea bag, make sure the string is hanging over the edge of the cup for easy removal.

Step 3: Pour Hot Water

3. Once the water reaches boiling point, carefully pour it over the tea bag or tea leaves in the cup. Be sure to fill the cup but leave some space at the top.

Step 4: Steep the Tea

4. Let the tea steep in the hot water for about 3-5 minutes. This allows the flavors to infuse.

Step 5: Remove the Tea Bag

5. After steeping, carefully remove the tea bag by pulling it up using the string or take out the tea strainer.

Step 6: Add Extras (Optional)

6. If desired, add sugar, milk, or a slice of lemon to your tea. Stir gently with a teaspoon to blend the flavors.

Step 7: Enjoy

7. Your cup of tea is ready to enjoy. Sip it slowly and savor the taste.

Conclusion:

Making a cup of tea is a simple yet delightful process. You can adjust the ingredients to suit your taste and preferences. Whether you like it plain or with added flavorings, a well-made cup of tea can be a soothing and comforting beverage.
