22. buatlah soal present perfeed tense ​S+have/has+V3+0-Adverd


Jawaban:

Berikut adalah beberapa contoh soal dalam bentuk Present Perfect Tense (sekarang sempurna) dengan pola S+have/has+V3+0-Adverb, beserta jawaban:

**Soal (+)**:

1. She ___ her assignment.

  a) have finished

  b) has finished

  Jawaban: b) has finished

2. They ___ the new restaurant downtown.

  a) have tried

  b) has tried

  Jawaban: a) have tried

**Soal (-)**:

1. He ___ his car yet.

  a) haven't washed

  b) hasn't washed

  Jawaban: b) hasn't washed

2. We ___ that movie.

  a) haven't seen

  b) hasn't seen

  Jawaban: a) haven't seen

**Soal Pertanyaan (?)**:

1. ___ you ever been to Europe?

  a) Have

  b) Has

  Jawaban: a) Have

2. ___ she visited the art museum recently?

  a) Have

  b) Has

  Jawaban: b) Has

Dalam Present Perfect Tense, "have" digunakan untuk subjek jamak (I, you, we, they) dan "has" digunakan untuk subjek tunggal (he, she, it). Verb ketiga (V3) merupakan bentuk past participle dari kata kerja. Pada kalimat negatif, "not" ditambahkan setelah "have" atau "has".
