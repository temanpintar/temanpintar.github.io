Dragonfly Habitat

Dragonflies are a group of insects belonging to the order Odonata.

Most dragonfly life cycles are spent below the surface of the water, from laying eggs to pre-adult times, using internal gills to breathe. After adulthood, dragonflies can only live a maximum of four months.

Dragonfly's life cycle varies from six months to a maximum of six or seven years.

Dragonflies lay their eggs in plants that live in water. After the eggs hatch, the dragonfly larvae then live and develop in the bottom of the water, and experience metamorphosis into the nymph, before finally escaping from the water as an adult dragonfly.

Hibernation

Hibernation is a condition of inactivity and decreased metabolism in animals characterized by lower body temperature, slower breathing, and lower metabolic rate. Hibernating animals try to conserve energy, especially during winter when food scarcity occurs, burning energy reserves, body fat, slowly. 23. Apa habitat, Activity, food , chraakteristic rusa dalam bahasa inggris


Describing Animals: Deer

Habitat

Deer live in grasslands, prairies & plains, mountains are forests, including hardwoods, coniferous and tropical forests.


Food

Deer are herbivores, they eat plants, fruits, acorns and nuts. During the autumn season, these type of things are more rare, so deer switch to grass and evergreen plants. In the winter season they eat whatever food available, for example like fallen leaves, twigs, bushes and woody plants.


Activity

Deer are active animals, especially during dusk when they leave their beds to go out and looking for food. They rest during the night, sometimes they even looking for food too at such time. Deer live in groups.


Characteristic

Deer have long, powerful legs, diminutive ail and long ears. They are in various size, but the largest could weigh up to 450 kilograms. Male deers are larger than the females.


Pembahasan

Untuk mengidentifikasi binatang berdasarkan informasi yang ada di tabel, pertama-tama kita harus mengetahui apakah itu pengertian dari habitat, food, activity dan characteristic. Berikut adalah penjelasannya.


Habitat is the natural home or environment of an animal, plant or other organism. Sehingga habitat adalah suatu tempat atau lokasi alami dari suatu binatang, tumbuhan atau organisme.


Food is any nutritious substance that people or animals eat or drink or that plants absorbs in order to maintain life and growth. Sederhananya, food adalah makanan yang dikonsumsi oleh manusia, binatang, atau yang diserap tumbuhan agar dapat bertumbuh dan bertahan hidup.


Activity is the condition in which things are happening or being done. Jadi, activity pada dasarnya adalah kegiatan yang dilakukan oleh binatang tersebut.


Characteristic is a typical or noticeable quality of someone or something. Karakteristik merupakan ciri khas yang jelas tentang sesuatu atau seseorang. Sehingga untuk identifikasi binatang, yang dilihat adalah ciri-ciri fisik dari binatang.

Writing Descriptive Text

Fish are aquatic animals which belong to the group of vertebrates. Unlike mammals, fish are cold-blooded. Their temperature mostly influenced by the environment surrounding them.

There are more than 30.000 species of fish in the ocean in the world. Each species have their own measurement, usually range from 8 mm to 16 meter. Some of the fishes have unique pattern on their scales. Those fishes are usually used by people as a decorative items.

Fish contain a lot of good nutrients for body such as protein, omega 3 and vitamins. Some species of fish, especially freshwater fish has been breed in the fish farm to fulfill needs of human. Other kind of fish like Koi and Betta are famous for their appearance and they’re usually kept in a pond as decoration.

Ada lebih dari 30.000 spesies ikan di laut di dunia. Setiap spesies punya ukurannya masing-masing, biasanya berkisar dari 8 mm hingga 16 meter. Kebanyakan dari ikan memiliki motif yang unik pada sisik mereka. Ikan-ikan tersebut biasanya digunakan sebagai bentuk dekorasi.
