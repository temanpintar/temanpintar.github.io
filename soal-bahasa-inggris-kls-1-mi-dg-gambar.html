11. kk jawaban soal matematika kls 4 mi semester 2pengertian dan sifat"persegi​


Jawab:

Persegi adalah sebuah bangun datar yang memiliki empat sisi yang sama panjangnya dan empat sudut yang sama besar. Setiap sisi persegi disebut sisi persegi dan setiap sudut persegi disebut sudut persegi.

Sifat-sifat persegi adalah sebagai berikut:

Setiap sisi persegi memiliki panjang yang sama.Setiap sudut persegi memiliki besar yang sama (90 derajat).Setiap diagonal persegi memiliki panjang yang sama.Diameter diagona persegi adalah sisi persegi yang berpotongan di tengah persegi.Luas persegi adalah sisi persegi kuadrat.Keliling persegi adalah jumlah panjang semua sisi persegi.

17. 1.Perhatikan gambar Tabung Barikut!!Pelajaran : Mtkkls : 6sd/mi​


Jawaban:

246,4 Liter

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Ingatt!!

1 dm³ = 1 liter

Mencari volume tabung:

V = π × r × r × t

V = 22/7 × 2,8 × 2,8 × 10

V = 22 × 0,4 × 2,8 × 10

V = 24,64 × 10

V = 246,4 dm³

V = 246,4 Liter

29. Cara membuat mi gelas dengan bahasa inggris


Jawaban:

How To Prepare Mie Gelas 

Ingredients :

1) A cup of Mie Gelas (karena mie gelas adalah nama merk, jadi tidak bisa ditranslate)

2) Hot water

3) Glass

Steps :

1) First, open Mie Gelas pack

2) Put Mie Gelas into the glass

3) Pour the hot water into the glas

4) Wait for a couple minutes until Mie Gelas is ready

5) Enjoy

34. (Wawancarailah orang tuamu!) Ask your parents to describe their home. (Mintalah orang tuamu mendeskripsikan rumah mereka!) GE Bahasa Inggris SD/MI kls. 4/Smt​


Jawaban:

Me: Good morning, Dad and Mom. I would like to conduct an interview about your home. Could you please describe your home?

Dad: Sure, we'd be happy to. Our home is a two-story house located in a peaceful residential neighborhood. The exterior has a modern design with a combination of neutral colors that give an elegant impression. There is a small garden in front of the house with various decorative plants that provide a fresh and beautiful ambiance.

Me: How about the interior of the house?

Mom: Inside the house, there is a spacious living room with comfortable and modern furniture. The living room is a gathering place for the family to relax together. There is also a dining area connected to the kitchen, which is equipped with modern appliances and a functional cooking area. Additionally, there are several cozy bedrooms furnished according to our taste.

Me: Are there any particular rooms or standout areas in the house?

Dad: Yes, there is a family room located on the second floor. The family room is where we spend time together, watching movies, or playing games with the kids. We also have a comfortable home office, where we can work or engage in other productive activities.

Me: What about the backyard of the house?

Mom: Our backyard is quite spacious and adorned with a beautiful garden. We have a cozy outdoor seating area where we can enjoy the fresh air and host family gatherings or get-togethers with friends. We also have a play area for the kids, including swings and a slide.

Me: Thank you for the detailed description. Your family home sounds very comfortable and enjoyable. Is there anything else you would like to add?

Dad: Thank you. We are happy to live in this home because it not only provides comfort but also serves as a place for our family to gather and create beautiful memories. We strive to make it a warm and loving environment for all family members.

Me: Thank you very much for your time and information. May your family home always be filled with happiness and warmth.

Parents: Thank you, and thank you for conducting this interview.
