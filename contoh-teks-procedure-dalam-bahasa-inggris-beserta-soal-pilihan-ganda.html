- Agar-Agar White 1 Pack

- 1 Egg Yolk, Beaten
- Egg Whites 3 Eggs 


Tools:

Pan,Bowl,Stove,Spoon,Mold,Refrigerator,Water


Steps:



First,Agar-agar cook with milk, chocolate and sugar over medium heat until hot.

Second, take a little dough, then mix with the egg yolks, pour back into the hot mixture. Cook until boiling. Lift.

Next, Beaten egg whites until fluffy and up. Then pour the hot gelatin mixture gradually while stirring constantly until well blended. 

Then, Save or pour into a mold. Allow it to freeze, so quickly refrigerate until cold. 

Finally, prepared pudding served with a complement

- Susu Cair 400 Ml

- 2 sendok makan gula pasir

Alat:

Panci, mangkuk, kompor, sendok, cetakan, lemari es, air

Pertama, masak dengan susu, cokelat, dan gula dengan api sedang sampai panas.

Kedua, ambil sedikit adonan, lalu campur dengan kuning telur, tuang kembali ke dalam campuran panas. Masak sampai mendidih. Tangga berjalan.

Selanjutnya, kocok putih telur hingga mengembang dan naik. Kemudian tuangkan campuran gelatin panas secara bertahap sambil diaduk terus sampai tercampur rata.

Jawaban:

Title: How to Make Ginger Tea

Introduction:

Ginger tea is a soothing and healthy beverage that can help warm you up and boost your immune system. Here is a simple procedure to make ginger tea at home.

Materials:

- Fresh ginger root (about 2 inches)

- Water (2 cups)

- Honey or lemon (optional for flavor)

Procedure:

1. Prepare the ginger:

- Peel the ginger root using a knife or a spoon to remove the outer skin.

- Slice the ginger into thin pieces or grate it using a grater. The size and thickness can vary according to your preference.

2. Boil the water:

- Pour the water into a small saucepan or a kettle.

- Place the saucepan on the stove and bring the water to a boil over medium heat.

3. Add the ginger:

- Once the water reaches a boiling point, add the sliced or grated ginger into the saucepan.

- Reduce the heat to low and let the ginger simmer in the water for about 10-15 minutes. This will allow the ginger to infuse its flavor into the water.

4. Strain the tea:

- After simmering, carefully pour the ginger tea through a fine mesh strainer into a cup or teapot to separate the ginger pieces from the liquid.

- Discard the ginger pieces or save them for other uses if desired.

5. Sweeten or flavor (optional):

- If desired, add a teaspoon of honey or a squeeze of lemon juice to enhance the flavor of your ginger tea. Stir well to combine.

6. Enjoy your ginger tea:

- Your ginger tea is now ready to be enjoyed! Sip it slowly while it's still warm to experience its soothing and invigorating effects.

Note: You can adjust the amount of ginger and the steeping time according to your taste preferences. Some people may prefer a stronger ginger flavor by adding more ginger or steeping it for a longer duration.

Conclusion:

Making ginger tea at home is a simple and rewarding process. It's a delightful way to enjoy the health benefits of ginger and warm up during colder days. So, go ahead and give it a try!

Pembahasan:

Teks prosedur adalah sebuah teks yang berisi langkah atau tahapan untuk melakukan suatu hal. Teks prosedur menjelaskan cara membuat atau mengerjakan sesuatu dengan langkah-langkah terstruktur.

Teks prosedur terdiri dari beberapa struktur. Ada empat bagian, yaitu tujuan, alat dan bahan, langkah pengerjaan, dan penutup.  Tujuan teks prosedur bertujuan untuk menjelaskan atau memberitahu cara membuat, mengoperasikan, atau melakukan sesuatu sesuai dengan urutan tindakan atau langkah. Alat dan bahan berisi mengenai bahan dan alat apa saja yang dibutuhkan dalam melakukan proses atau langkah-langkah tersebut. Langkah pengerjaan terdiri dari tahapan-tahapan yang harus dilalui dari awal sampai akhir.  Penutup merujuk kembali ke hal-hal pokok pada langkah-langkah yang telah disajikan, bertujuan untuk memotivasi orang untuk melakukan aktivitas atau kegiatan. Misalnya, selamat menikmati, semoga bermanfaat, dan simpulan hasil kegiatan yang dilakukan.

Procedure Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menginformasikan cara membuat sesuatu atau cara mengoperasikan sesuatu.


Generic Structure dari Procedure Text adalah:

Goal : (e.g : How to make spaghetti)Material or Ingredient : (e.g : the material to cook omelette are egg, onion, vegetable oil, etc. ) Step : (e.g : first, wash the tomatoes, onion, …., second cut the onions becomes slice. . . )

Language Features dari Procedure Text adalah:

Use adverbial of sequence / Using temporal conjunction (e.g: first, second, third, the last) Use command / imperative sentence (e.g : put the noodles on the . . ., cut the onion. . ., wash the tomatoes. . . ) Using adverbials (Adverbs) to express detail the time, place, manner accurate, for example, for five minutes, 2 hours, etc. Using action verbs, e.g : make, take, boil, cook Using Simple Present Tense

Jawaban:

How to Make Scrambled Eggs

Let’s learn how to make yummy scrambled eggs at your home with this easy recipe!

Ingredients:

• 2 eggs

• Milk (if desired)

• 1 teaspoon of butter

• A pinch of salt and pepper

Steps:

1. First, crack the eggs into a small bowl and add a pinch of salt and pepper. Whisk until all well blended.

2. In a frying pan, add butter and let it melt.

3. Pour the eggs and milk, and for around 20 seconds, do not stir it.

4. After that, fold the eggs to the center and stir with spatula.

5. Repeat the previous step until all the eggs are mostly cooked

6. Remove eggs from heat, and keep continue to fold and stir the eggs for around a minute.

7. Lift the eggs, put on the serving plate and your scrambled eggs are ready.

                                      How To Make Mango juice

Materials: 2 mangoes, sugar, sweetened condensed milk, water, and ice.

Steps:

Peel the mango and wash it with clean water. Cut the mango flesh according to taste.If the mango has been cut into small pieces, puree in a blender with water.Add sugar, sweetened condensed milk and a little ice to the blender.Puree until the mango flesh is really smooth.When the mango is smooth, strain the mango and serve in a serving glass.Add ice cubes to the glass and add milk on top.Mango juice is ready to serve.

Artinya yaitu:

                             Cara Membuat Jus Mangga

Bahan: 2 buah mangga, gula pasir, susu kental manis, air, dan es batu.

Langkah:

Kupas mangga dan cuci dengan air bersih. Potong daging mangga sesuai selera. Jika mangga sudah dipotong kecil-kecil, haluskan dengan blender bersama air. Tambahkan gula pasir, susu kental manis dan sedikit es batu ke dalam blender. Haluskan hingga daging mangga benar-benar halus. Mangga yang sudah halus, saring mangga dan sajikan dalam gelas saji.Tambahkan es batu ke dalam gelas dan tambahkan susu di atasnya.Jus mangga siap disajikan.

Corn Flour Pudding

Ingredients:

2000 ml milk.

200 gr cornflour.

500 gr brown sugar.

4 tbs water to dissolve the sugar.

1 stick of 10 cm cinnamon.

1 tbs butter.

3 tbs cinnamon ground for sprinkling.

Method:

Thoroughly mix milk and cornflour until smooth.Boil brown sugar and water until sugar is dissolved, then sieve.Mix brown sugar with the milk and cornflour mixture, and cinnamon stick. Add butter and simmer whilst stirring until cornflour becomes thick.Pour into a suitable mold and let it cool.When serving, sprinkle with ground cinnamon.

Makes 10—15 portions, best served when cold.

A Basic Recipe for Fried Rice

Ingredients:

2 large eggs.

salt and pepper.

4 tbs cooking oil or as needed.

3 cups cooked rice.

1—2 tbs light soy sauce or oyster sauce, as desired.

Steps:

Lightly beat the eggs with salt and pepper.Heat a wok and add 2 tablespoons of cooking oil. When the oil is hot, pour the eggs. Stir until it is lightly scrambled but not too dry. Remove the eggs and clean out the wok.Add another 2 tablespoons of cooking oil. Add the rice and stir-fry for a few minutes by using a wooden spatula. Put soy sauce as desired.Add the scrambled egg back into the pan. Mix thoroughly. Serve hot.

Penjelasan:

Procedure text merupakan jenis teks yang bertujuan memberi petunjuk cara mengoperasikan menciptakan, atau membuat sesuatu melalui serangkaian tindakan atau langkah.

Ciri kebahasaan procedure text:

Menggunakan kata kerja tindakan (action verb).Menggunakan pola kalimat perintah (imperrative).Menggunakan kata hubung (conjunction) untuk mengurutkan kegiatan.Mengunakan kata keterangan (adverb) untuk menyatakan rincian waktu.

Struktur procedure text:

Goal/Aim/Purpose: tujuan kegiatan atau judul.Materials/Ingredients: alat atau bahan yang diperlukan.Steps/Methods: langkah-langkah kegiatan secara utuh.

How to Make Brownies 


Ingredients
• 150gram of butter.
• 150gram of dark chocolate.
• 300 gram of flour                 
• 4 eggs.
• 600 gram of regular sugar
• 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps
1. Melt the chocolate with the butter.
2. Stir the eggs with the sugar and the vanilla extract.
3. Preheat the oven at low temperature
4. Combine the mixes you made.
5. Add the flour and stir with a wooden spoon.
6. Grease a brownie tin.
7. Add a little flour to cover the tin.
8. Add the brownie dough.
9. Bake for 10-30 minutes.
10. To check the brownies, put a knife in the mix. The knife must be moist!
11. Eat them 15 minutes after you took them out of the oven.
