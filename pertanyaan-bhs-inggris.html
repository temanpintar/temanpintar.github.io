Kata tanya dalam bhs inggris :

What ( apa ) ?

Where ( dimana ) ?

Who ( siapa ) ?

Why ( Kenapa / mengapa ) ?

When ( kapan ) ?

How ( bagaimana ) ?

Contoh :

What would you like for dinner?

( Apa makan malam yang kamu inginkan )

Where is your home?

( Dimana rumahmu )

40. bantu pertanyaan bhs. inggrisku yaa​


Jawaban:May I get your attention?Do you understand?I think ...What do you think?I appreciate your effort

Pembahasan:

1. To get someone’s attention (Untuk mendapatkan perhatian seseorang)

May I get your attention? (Bolehkah saya mendapat perhatianmu)Listen to me (Dengarkan saya)Excuse me (Permisi)

2. Check someone’s understanding (Mengecek pemahamahan seseorang)

Do you understand? (apa kamu faham?)Do you get it? (apa kamu faham?)Do you have any questions? (Apa kamu ada Pertanyaan?)

3. Give your opinion (memberikan pendapatmu)

I think ... (Saya rasa/pikir...)in my Opinion... (Pendapat saya...)in my point of view (dalam sudut pandang saya)

4. Ask someone’ Opinion (Meminta pendapat seseorang)

what do you think? (bagaimana menurutmu?)what is your opinion? (Apa pendapatmu?)what is your point of view? (apa pandanganmu?)

5. Appreciation of something (Menghargai sesuatu)

I appreciate your effort (Saya menghargai kerja kerasmu)Thank you for helping me (Terimakasih sudah membantuku)Thank you for saying it (Terimakasih [karena] mengatakannya)
