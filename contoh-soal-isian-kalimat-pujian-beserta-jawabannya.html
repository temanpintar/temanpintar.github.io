Kritik yaitu ialah sebuah pendapat yang telah berupa dengan sebuah kecaman atau tanggapan dengan disertai sebuah pertimbangan atau alasan yang baik maupun buruk mengenai suatu hal tertentu. Pujian yaitu ialah sebuah ungkapan yang telah diberikan kepada seseorang dengan positif.

21. Berikan contoh 5 kalimat pujian berserta tanggapannya dalam bahasa inggris


Penjelasan:

1. a: what a beautiful necklace

b: thank you

2. a: wow you are so smart

b : thank you

3. a: you such a good person

b : dont mention it

║COMPLIMENT║

Explanation

Compliment is used to reveal praise/admiration on people's general appearance, new look, when we meet him/her for the first time, something people did, and something people have in order to evoke someone' spirit to be better in the future. It looks like a seduction but it has the positive purpose.

Expression of Compliment

What a beautiful paintingThat's incredibleThat's greatYou look charmingPerfectHow clever you areYou are so creativeI just love your carThis cake is superbYour house is very nice and homey

Responding to compliment

Thank you very muchI'm glad you like itThanks. But it's really nothing great.Thank you for saying so.Thank you. It's nice of you to say so

Dialog of compliment expression

{ Your friend just bought a new gown }

Dee  :  Sara, you look great today! where did you buy it?

Sara  : Pardon!? Thanks.

Dee   : I meant, your gown ! What a beautiful gown. You look pretty with that gown.

Sara  : Thanks.  It's nice of you to say so. I bought it at the mall yesterday, Dee!
